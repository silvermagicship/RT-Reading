## 序章｜接替领主舰长的位置 / Take the Lord Captain's place


### 任务上下文

#### 接替领主舰长的位置 / Take the Lord Captain's place

- 中文：按照惯例，领主舰长必须出现在舰桥上。西奥多拉夫人死了，只有她的继承人才有权接替她的位置。
- English: It is customary for the Lord Captain to preside on the bridge. Lady Theodora is dead — only her heir possesses the right to take her place at the helm.
- 地点：虚空舰舰桥 / Voidship Bridge

### 场景


*雷沃转过身，抬头看着你。他犹豫片刻，之后对站在你身边的阿贝拉德开了口。*“总领，导航者圣所没有任何反应。音阵信号很稳定，这意味着……”
*Ravor turns around and looks up at you. He hesitates for a few moments, then addresses Abelard, who is standing next to you.* "Seneschal, the Navigator's Sanctum is silent. The vox-signal is stable, which means..."

*阿贝拉德心不在焉地点了点头。*“亚空间的风暴袭击了冯·瓦兰修斯家族旗舰。导航者为了对抗这场风暴，献出了他自己的生命。我们会悼念他，也会悼念其他在这一天丧命的人们。”
*Abelard nods absent-mindedly.* "The Navigator gave his life battling the storm that nearly claimed the von Valancius flagship. We will honour his memory along with that of all who died this day."

*雷沃并没有移开视线。他正死死地盯着你，白色的眉毛皱了起来。*“总领……现在你可以告诉我们了吗？那个人是谁？船长老大在什么地方？”
*Ravor does not look away. He is giving you a hard stare, his white brows furrowed.* "Seneschal... Will you tell us now? Who is that? Where's the Lord Captain?"

*阿贝拉德花了很长时间才给出回答。你看到他紧紧地咬住牙关，一句话都说不出来。过了很久，他才正了正沾满了血迹的外套，挺直了身子，开了口。*“身为这艘船的大副，我必须行使……我的责任。我怀着无上的遗憾与悲痛通知各位，西奥多拉·冯·瓦兰修斯领主舰长已不幸遇难。愿她的光辉事迹永远不会从王朝的史册上褪色。”
*It takes time for Abelard to respond: you watch as he grits his teeth, unable to bring himself to say anything. Finally, he adjusts his blood-stained coat, straightens, and begins to speak,* "As First Officer, it is my duty... to inform you, with the greatest regret and indelible sorrow, that Lord Captain Theodora von Valancius is dead. May her memory never fade from the annals of the dynasty."

*阿贝拉德等待第一波震惊的呼声结束，之后瞥了你一眼，继续开了口。*“由于当下并没有任何亲族可以质疑这个决定，根据血缘继承权……”
*After waiting out the first wave of shocked cries, Abelard glances at you and continues,* "By right of blood succession and with the absence of other kin who could challenge this decision..."

*穆兰注意到埃克伦迪之后，突然退缩了一下，随后以庄严的声音开了口。*“埃克伦迪兄弟，赞美阿苏焉，我又有幸与你见面。请你立刻接受我的保护吧。”
*Upon noticing Eklendyl, Muaran flinches, then says solemnly,* "Brother Eklendyl, I praise Asuryan for the fortune of seeing you again. Please, step under my protection without delay."

“穆兰兄弟，我并没有感觉到威胁……这种感觉已经消失了。”
"Brother Muaran, I do not feel threatened... not anymore."

*绮贝菈抬头看着先知，目光中满是痛苦。*“理当如此。”
*Kibellah looks up at the Farseer with torment in her eyes.* "You should."

*穆兰看着伊莉耶特，以充满敌意的声音开了口。*“流浪者伊莉耶特，你踏上的道路布满了你犯下的错误。猴子再次来到了我们的领地，而你再次帮助了他们！”
*Muaran looks at Yrliet and says with enmity in his voice,* "Outcast Yrliet, you walk the path paved with your own misdoings. Once more mon-keigh have found their way to our domain, and once more you aid them in doing so!"

“我们这次前来既不是为了开战，也没有任何恶意。你的愤怒毫无道理可言，穆兰。”
"Neither war nor ill intent follow in our wake, Muaran. Your anger is unwarranted."

*穆兰看到玛拉斋的时候，声音低沉得仿佛毒蛇一般。*“这里没有黑暗子嗣的位置。”
*Muaran's voice drops to a venomous hiss when he sees Marazhai.* "Dark Ones have no place here."

*玛拉斋嘲弄地大笑起来。*“又一个先知，打算躲过狂风阴谋团的刀锋！嗯，我可以纠正这个错误，懦夫。”
*Marazhai laughs mockingly.* "Another Farseer who chose to hide from the blades of the Reaving Tempest! Well, it is nothing I cannot rectify, coward."

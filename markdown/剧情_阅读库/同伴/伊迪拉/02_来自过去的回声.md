来自过去的回声

Echo of the Past

### 任务简介

舰桥传来了消息，说早已过世的前任行商浪人出现在了船上。报告中还提到了伊迪拉·特拉斯这个名字，这是位未经授权的灵能者。领主舰长必须立刻采取行动，弄清楚发生了什么事情，消灭潜在的威胁。

A message has come to the bridge reporting that the departed Rogue Trader has appeared on the ship. The name Idira Tlass was mentioned, an unsanctioned psyker. The Lord Captain must act to find out what is happening and to neutralise the potential threat.

异形的游戏

Xenos Games

### 任务简介

黑暗灵族玛拉斋以他自己独有的方式，向领主舰长发出了通告，说人类的舰船在苍灰恶咒遭遇了某种灾难。但话说回来，异形的话恐怕并不值得相信。

Marazhai the Drukhari, in his own unique fashion, informed the Lord Captain about a human vessel that had suffered some kind of calamity in the Cinerus Maleficum. Then again, it is unlikely the words of a xenos can be trusted.

### 完成文本

原来，那艘船是荒野旅人号，是圣德鲁苏斯教团的古老方舟。方舟所在的世界被邪恶的异形摧毁，船上所有人命丧当场。

The discovered ship turned out to be the Wasteland Wayfarer — an ancient ark of Drusian missionaries who perished on a world destroyed by ungodly xenos.

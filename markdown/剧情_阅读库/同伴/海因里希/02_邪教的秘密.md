邪教的秘密

Secrets of the Cult

### 任务简介

海因里希手中掌握着部分线索，这些线索与“终末黎明”教派的计划有关。这个邪教的计划与工业世界基亚瓦伽马星的命运息息相关，若想摧毁邪教，海因里希的调查必不可少。他也正是出于这个原因，才加入了行商浪人的队伍。

Heinrix possesses information about some of the plans of the Cult of the Final Dawn, plans connected to the industrial world Kiava Gamma. The future struggle against the cult will depend on the results of his search, which is the reason for his joining the Rogue Trader's retinue.

### 团聚 / 查明索罗蒙的遭遇

### 任务标题

团聚

Reunion

### 任务简介

行商浪人的整支小队失散在了黑暗之城。行商浪人需要找到失去的同伴，从而争取活着逃离科摩罗的机会。

The Lord Captain's squad has been scattered throughout the Dark City. By reuniting with  lost companions, the Lord Captain will increase  chances of successfully escaping Commorragh.

### 区域

裂隙

Chasm

Find the missing companions

行商浪人需要不断收集传言与信息，弄清楚那些失踪的伙伴都去了什么地方，得知他们的遭遇。

By collecting rumours and information on  missing companions, the Lord Captain will be able to find them and learn about their fate.

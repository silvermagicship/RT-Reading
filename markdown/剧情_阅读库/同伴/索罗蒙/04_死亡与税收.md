死亡与税收

Death and Tithes

### 任务简介

沃尔图尔行政官与斯塔克斯元帅再次请求行商浪人帮忙。这一次，这件事情关系到整个科罗努斯扩区最绝密的情报。

Prefect Vultur and Marshal Strachs entreat the Rogue Trader once more — but this time, it concerns the most closely guarded secret of the Koronus Expanse.

## 第四章｜归舰与舰桥会议

*所有船员盯着人群之中身材壮硕的巨人，目光中充满了恐惧与敬畏。有的人在胸前比着双头鹰手势，还有的人跪倒在地，口中嘴里念念有词地向神皇祈祷。这些虔诚之人震惊的样子丝毫没有让乌尔法失去分寸。他仅仅是抿着嘴笑了笑，好奇地环顾四周。*
*The crew stares in awe and terror at the giant towering over the crowd. Some make the sign of the Aquila on their chests, others collapse to their knees with a prayer to the God-Emperor on their lips. This wave of pious shock does not seem to discompose Ulfar in the slightest — he just chuckles into his beard and glances around curiously.*

*Marazhai's presence alone is enough to elicit gasps of dismay and whispers from the crowd.

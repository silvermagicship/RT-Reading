某天，伊莉耶特终于回到了外界人身边，却发现{mf|他|她}已行将就木。原来，她已经花了数十年的时间寻找灵魂石，而她自己却对此浑然未觉。伊莉耶特与外界人一起度过了最后的时光，当行商浪人最终迎来大限之日时，她最后一次进入了{mf|他|她}的世界。再度年轻的外界人站在那里，微笑着，看着她。滚烫的泪水灼痛了伊莉耶特的皮肤，在那之后，人类灵魂的黯淡光芒便永远熄灭了。伊莉耶特碰了碰外界人的手腕，检查了{mf|他|她}的脉搏，随后闭上眼睛，叹了口气，离开了冯·瓦兰修斯王朝，再也没有回来。

##### English

One day, Yrliet returned to find the elantach on {mf|his|her} deathbed — her search had taken decades, yet decades pass without notice for Aeldari. Yrliet spent the elantach's final days with {mf|him|her}, and when the time came for death to place its hand on the Rogue Trader's shoulder, she sank into {mf|his|her} world one last time. The elantach, young again, looked at her and smiled. Tears burned Yrliet's skin, and then the dim light of the human soul faded. Yrliet touched the elantach's wrist to check {mf|his|her} pulse. She closed her eyes, sighed, and left the von Valancius protectorate, never to return.

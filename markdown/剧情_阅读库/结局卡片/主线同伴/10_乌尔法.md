### 乌尔法判定条件补充 ^cond-ulfar

- 乌尔法尔是目前最清楚的累计型角色之一，主计数旗标就是：`Ulfar_Chapter`、`Ulfar_Loner`、`Ulfar_Wulfen`。
- 游戏里明着存在四轮三向选择：`UlfarChoice1_Chapter/Loner/Wulfen`、`UlfarChoice2_Chapter/Loner/Wulfen`、`UlfarChoice3_Chapter/Loner/Wulfen`、`UlfarChoice4_Chapter/Loner/Wulfen`。可以把它理解成四次给三条路线投票。

##### 中文

乌尔法·雷狼加入了噬风者狼群，不久之后，他们的领袖索巴德光荣战死，而乌尔法成为了新任首领。他给芬里斯捎了个信，说他打算留在科罗努斯扩区，这片狩猎场里有太多邪恶的敌人，应当受到全父之怒的惩戒。于是，这支强大的狼群踏上了辉煌的传说之路。多年之后，乌尔法找到了可以光荣赴死的战斗，但这个时候，亚空间突然裂开了一道口子，绘有狼首的芬里斯舰船从里面冲了出来，前来支援他。乌尔法被他的忠实伙伴哈尔布兰特从战场上救了回来，并被授予了光荣的无畏仪式，穿上了神圣的盔甲。在受祝仪式上，他一边咒骂着，一边要求让他赴死，而他的兄弟们则报以哈哈大笑。恢复意识之后，乌尔法率领他的狼群前往异端群星，因为他濒死时看到了幻象，而全父在幻象中赋予了他全新的使命。自从太空野狼离开之后，科罗努斯扩区再也没人听说过他们的消息，但许多人坚信，总有一天他们会在胜利中凯旋。

##### English

After joining the Stormbiters, Ulfar Thunderlung soon became the pack leader, taking the place of Thorbald, who met an honourable end. The new leader sent a message to Fenris stating his intention to remain in the Expanse — there were too many enemies yet lurking in these hunting grounds that deserved the Allfather's wrath. Legends paved the formidable pack's path in gold. Years later, Ulfar found a battle in which he could meet his honourable death, but the warp was suddenly torn asunder, and ships from Fenris bearing the mark of the wolf head came to his aid. Saved from the battlefield by his loyal companion Halbrandt, Ulfar accepted the honours of the dreadnought and was entombed in sacred armour. During the blessed ritual he cursed and demanded to be left to his death, but his brothers merely answered with mirthful laughter. When he recovered his senses, Ulfar led his forces to the Heathen Stars — the Allfather had given him a new mission in a near-death vision. No one in the Expanse has heard word of the Space Wolves since their departure, yet many believe that one day they will return victorious.

乌尔法埋葬了他的兄弟们之后，疲惫与失落的重担深深地压在了的肩上。尽管他对全父的忠诚依然坚韧，但数十年的战斗中已使他的心灵满是创伤。他无法违背他的誓言，于是便继续孤身一人闯荡，身为“怒嚎者”狼群最后的野狼，孤独地传唱着狼群的史诗。这头孤狼的诸多传说在无数世界流传。他以灰色的斗篷盖住他的盔甲，突然出现，而后又突然消失，在身后留下不义之人的尸体。无数关于这位神秘复仇者的故事被精心撰写出来，数百名自称“幼狼”的年轻英雄自发追随他的脚步，但乌尔法并没有回应他们。他既没有记住那些人的名字，也没有为他们的死亡而哀悼。他唯一渴望的只有光荣的死亡，但他的渴望迟迟未能实现。他活了下来，一如既往。

##### English

After burying his brothers, Ulfar felt the weight of exhaustion and loss on his shoulders. His loyalty to the Allfather was strong, but his heart had suffered much over his decades of battles. Unable to break his vows, he continued to sing the saga of his pack alone, the last of the Baleful Howl. Hundreds of legends on myriad worlds are told of the Lone Wolf. Covering his armour with a grey cloak, he would appear and vanish again, leaving only the corpses of the unrighteous behind. Stories were crafted about the mysterious avenger, and hundreds of young heroes, Wolflings, began to follow him. Ulfar did not call on them. He did not remember their names and did not mourn them. He longed only for an honourable death, but could not find it. He always survived.

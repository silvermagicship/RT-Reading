##### English

Evayne Winterscale underwent his Magnae Accessio and became his dynasty's new Rogue Trader. Many said that noble Evayne lacked firmness of judgement, yet he had more than enough trust, even in those who were unworthy of it. The young Rogue Trader did not forget those who had saved him from the Rykad System or the favours that House von Valancius had bestowed on the Winterscales.

##### 中文

卡里戈斯·温特斯凯尔抵挡住了身边的各种诱惑，回到了他的领地，开始拼凑他那破碎的王朝。直到他去世的那一天，他都全身心地致力于拯救那些被抛弃的世界，保护幸存者。遗憾的是，他所剩的时间有限，无法完成太多事情。他断然拒绝了一切可以让他恢复活力、再次恢复行动能力的植入物。在接下来的数十年里，他始终端坐在他的王座之上，片刻都不曾离开，直至生命的最后一刻。

##### English

Resisting the temptations that besieged him, Calligos Winterscale returned to his domain and began to piece together the shattered remains of his protectorate. Until his dying day, his actions and thoughts were dedicated to saving abandoned worlds and protecting survivors. Alas, he was not able to achieve much in the time he was allotted. He breathed his last breath on the same throne that he refused to leave for decades, adamantly refusing augmetics that would restore mobility and power to his legs.

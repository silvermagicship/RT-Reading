Governor Vedka Dunawski found herself unable to accept the betrayal of her Lord Captain. For seven long years, she successfully defended Zarkov against the forces of Chaos, but with each battle she lost more and more faith. At the end of the seventh year, Vedka disappeared from her office without a trace — mere days before the Zarkov army was struck by a deadly disease that had never been seen before. It is said that shortly afterward, a horrifying priestess of the dark powers appeared on Zarkov — the one-eyed, ulcer-riddled Hope Breaker, who turned the souls of the righteous away from the God-Emperor with her words of salvation in despair.

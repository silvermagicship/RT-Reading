##### 中文

塞贝里安·冯·瓦兰修斯自此绝迹于科罗努斯扩区。然而，达戈努斯那些心怀不满的贵族们仍屡屡搬出他的名号，密谋以“寻找失踪先主”为借口，罢免舰长大人。但等这批阴谋家死绝后，塞贝里安的回归便沦为一种离奇传说，成了旗舰军官们在难得清闲时打发时间的谈资。

##### English

Seberian von Valancius was never again seen in the Expanse. His name, however, was raised several more times by the dissatisfied noble houses on Dargonus, who were conspiring to oust the Lord Captain under the pretence of "finding the lost sire." But when the last of those schemers died off, Seberian's return became nothing more than a strange tale once more, the kind that flagship officers would tell each other in their rare moments of leisure.

##### 中文

塞贝里安·冯·瓦兰修斯的舰船在属地边界之外的虚空中消失得无影无踪。此后的一段时间里，旗舰仍能收到星语信息，汇报他那些匪夷所思的冒险奇遇；但几年后，连此类消息也不再有了。在最后一份消息中，这位复活的冯·瓦兰修斯家族先祖许诺会给王朝送上一份告别礼物，但他的舰船再也没有出现在人类已知星域的边界之内。

##### English

Seberian von Valancius's ship vanished without a trace in the void beyond the bounds of the protectorate. For some time after, the flagship received Astropathic messages with reports of Seberian's encounters with ominous wonders and inconceivable horrors, but after a few years they stopped as well. In the final dispatch, the resurrected ancestor of House von Valancius promised to deliver a farewell gift to the dynasty, but his ship never again crossed the borders of space known to Humanity.

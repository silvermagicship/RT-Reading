帕斯卡判定条件补充 ^cond-pasqal

  • 主路线是三计数器：PasqalAmarnathCounter、PasqalEgoCounter、PasqalLoyalCounter。
  • 三个终局态分别是：Pasqal_Amarnath、Pasqal_Ego、Pasqal_Loyal；对应的结局标签是 CompPasqal_Amarnath / Ego / Loyal。
  • 静态资源里没有像阿洁塔、乌尔法尔那样把每一次“+1”都明着命名出来，但能明确看到路线分流存在于 PasqalQ2_AmarnathOrEgo_DLC2、PasqalQ2_Ego_DLC2、PasqalQ2_Loyal_DLC2。
  • HaneumannLoyalist_dialogue / HaneumannLoyalist_prompt 说明 Loyal 线有单独的 Haneumann 忠诚派收束；ArcShip_Observatorium_Ego_Preset 说明 Ego 线至少有独立场景态。
  • Ch3_Pasqal_PitCorrupted 与 Ch3_Pasqal_PitPurified 更像主路线之外的附加结果，会影响对应卡片的措辞或额外卡。

No one had dared depict the Deus Mechanicus in this manner before, adorned with sinister flesh-embedded lumens and golden mech armour, and crowned with clawed wings. The Omnissiah chose a grim and belligerent likeness in which to appear before His unrighteous Messiah.

中文

English

The death of House Werserian was met with silent glee by the nobility on Dargonus. Their ravenous hunger was whetted. Corruption, larceny, and the loss of control over the capital fleet led to economic collapse not only on the capital world but throughout the protectorate as well.

House Werserian rose above its rivals and was never again the target of persecution — even after their esteemed patriarch died decades later in his bed surrounded by his grieving loved ones.

艾丝塔西娅与文森特步入了婚姻的殿堂，贾帕克家族也和韦尔森家族短暂交织在了一起。然而，他们的结合却没能留下子嗣，这让这对伴侣悲伤不已，而达戈努斯敌对贵族对此幸灾乐祸。艾丝塔西娅·韦尔森·贾帕克和她的先人一样，将她的一生用来侍奉行商浪人的王朝。而文森特，由于家族动荡与无法生育的问题，余生都活在妻子的阴影下。最终，他悄无声息地离世了。

English

Branches of Houses Gaprak and Werserian became intertwined for a time after the marriage of Astartia and Vicent. To the great sadness of the couple and the great amusement of the spiteful Dargonus nobility, their marriage produced no children. Just like her ancestors, Astartia Werserian-Gaprak dedicated her life to service. Crushed by the events that shook the stability of his house and his inability to provide an heir, Vicent lived in his wife's shadow. When his time came, he passed away without notice.

中文

韦尔森家族的成员忠诚地为王朝服务，积累了越来越多的权力。一个世纪后，他们终于赢得了其他贵族家族所享有的尊重。遗憾的是，权力与奢侈可以磨钝任何刀刃。很难想象，那些娇生惯养、游手好闲的贵族，居然会是令人敬畏的韦尔森总领的后代。

English

Members of House Werserian served the dynasty faithfully and loyally, amassing more and more power. A century later, they had finally gained the respect enjoyed by other noble houses. Yet, power and luxury can dull any blade — the now pampered and idle aristocrats were scarcely recognisable as the descendants of the formidable Seneschal Werserian.

English

For reasons unknown, members of the Werserian family found themselves time and time again as targets of assassinations, victims of unpredictable riots, and the focus of palace intrigue. The misfortune that plagued their house suddenly stopped when Regina Sauerback met her end.

卡片 180

详细信息

  • 原始卡片号：180
  • 文本 ID：cab177be-7919-4f68-8879-3b7de604793a
  • 本地化序号：12796
中文

即便是在阿贝拉德死后，人们对他的生平依然各执一词，充满了八卦的热情。据某位匿名的目击者声称，阿贝拉德回到达戈努斯之后，便立即召集他的家人举行庆祝晚宴。然而，宴会上的美酒却掺入了致命的剧毒，正如他的心灵一样。尊敬的总领再也不必离开这个礼堂了，他将会留在这里，永远照看他心爱的家人。从此往后，再也没人能从他身边夺走他的家人了。“再也没有人能伤害你们了。”他一边用肿胀的双手抚摸着溃烂的嘴唇，一边像念着邪咒一样，不断喃喃自语着这句话。死者的声音隐隐地附和着他。

English

Even after the Seneschal's death, conflicting rumours about his life continued to haunt people's imaginations. According to unnamed witnesses, upon arriving on Dargonus, Abelard immediately assembled his family for a celebratory dinner. The wine at that feast was as poisoned as his own mind. The honourable Seneschal would never have to leave the festive hall again, destined instead to look over his beloved family forever. Thenceforth, nobody would ever take them from him. "No one can hurt you now," he muttered, repeating the words like a twisted mantra as he caressed his ulcer-ridden lips with swollen hands, the dead faintly echoing him.

English

Clementia Werserian, granddaughter of Abelard and chancellor of Dargonus, executed her duties irreproachably and even found time to school the younger generations of her family. She was an equally skilled mentor, chancellor, and political operative. After a century of immaculate service, she surprised everyone by leaving her position and becoming the first mate on a trade ship. She would later admit, like a true Werserian, that she had always had a passion for ships and voidfaring.

Abelard's grief sent him on a six-year-long hunt for the despicable xenos, which, sadly, was unsuccessful.

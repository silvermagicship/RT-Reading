关联组 02

中文

卡西娅·奥赛罗放弃了成为大族长的命运，决定与她的爱人度过余下的岁月。她与爱人度过了短暂的时光，努力让共度的每一刻都充满色彩与激情。然而遗憾的是，由于卡西娅的基因突变，她无法生育后代，而她的身体也很快发生了畸变。某天晚上，她给了她的爱人一个吻，之后就彻底消失了，只留下了一张简短的纸条，上面写着:“我的爱人，从我们的初见到我们最后共度的日子，你始终都是我心中唯一的曙光，从未褪色。你还有很长的路要走，所以，请为了我们俩，快乐地活下去吧。至于我……我永远爱你。你亲爱的，阿娅。”

English

Cassia Orsellio renounced her destiny of becoming the Novator and chose to spend her days with her beloved. She lived a short life, trying to fill every moment spent together with colour and passion. Alas, Cassia's genetic mutations did not allow her to bear an heir, and soon they began grotesquely deforming her body. One night, she gave her lover a kiss and disappeared forever, leaving behind a short note: "My love, from our first meeting to our final days together, you were the only morning light that shone in my heart, never fading. You have a life ahead of you — live it happily, for both of us. As for me... I love you endlessly. Yours, Cass."

Like her predecessor, Cassia ruled House Orsellio with an iron fist — through force and fear.

Many years passed before the Orsellio name once again resounded through the Expanse, when Tisiphone II and her crazed followers started a secret war against another Navigator House. Its vicious battles took place in the warp, hidden from any witnesses in realspace.

中文

导航者女士在船上的工作结束之后，便离开了行商浪人，回到了奥赛罗家族的怀抱。所有人都说，一位新的大族长将会登上王位，而她的家族也会在沉寂多年之后再度重生。但遗憾的是，卡西娅没能成为家族的大族长，因为家族之间爆发了冲突，最终演变成一场自相残杀的战争。战争爆发不久，卡西娅女士就遭到了杀害，无数忠诚份子与叛徒也在这场内乱中身亡。其余的导航者家族趁机瓜分曾一度伟大的奥赛罗家族剩余的遗产，将残存的一切据为己用。

English

When the Lady Navigator's service came to an end, Cassia left the Rogue Trader and returned to House Orsellio's embrace. It was rumoured that a new Novator would soon take the throne and the House would be reborn after years of silence. Alas, the inauguration did not come to pass, as the discord within the House flared up and turned into a fratricidal war. Lady Cassia died at its outset, taking the lives of many loyalists and renegades with her. The other Navigator Houses made efforts to scatter the remains of the once great House Orsellio and to absorb what little was left of it.

中文

大族长卡西娅与宿老绮贝菈年纪轻轻就身居高位，这样的负担拉近了两人之间的距离。她们之间的友情并不长久，因为她们肩负着极其沉重的职责，而且两人所在的世界也截然不同。但即便如此，她们依然交换了礼物，既是出于对彼此的尊重，也是为了纪念她们曾经共同经历过的旅程。卡西娅用绮贝菈的鲜血画了一幅令人难以置信的传世之作，这幅画成为了织血罗网的圣物，而绮贝菈则委派织血罗网最出色的织网者留在卡西娅身边，担任她的保镖。据说，许多年后，当大族长卡西娅的身体再也无法承受突变的压力时，那位织网者根据卡西娅的命令，结束了她的生命。

English

Novator Cassia and Vizsier Kibellah were brought closer by their common burden of assuming a high position of power at a young age. Though their friendship was short-lived, as it could not overcome the gravity of their duties and the differences between the worlds to which they belonged, they exchanged gifts out of respect for each other and to honour the memories of the journeys they had shared. Cassia used Kibellah's blood to paint an incredible piece which became a relic of the Bloodspun Web. Kibellah, in turn, assigned one of her best spinners to act as Cassia's bodyguard. Many years later, this spinner was rumoured to have ended Cassia's life on the Novator's own orders when her body could no longer resist the strain of mutation.

Cassia Orsellio died on Epitaph, and her death became the undoing of her noble House. Echoing her cry, they all lost their minds in that instant and became loathsome monstrosities. House Orsellio fell, and its inhabitants were buried in the ruins.

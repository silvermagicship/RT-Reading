冯·卡洛克斯审判官常常包容那些微不足道的罪行，并倾向于使用可疑的方法，这使得他声名远播。数十年后，他的学生将他视为危险的激进分子，认为他准备利用混沌大敌的造物来实现自己的目的。然而，这些人还没来得及把他们的导师交给法庭，冯·卡洛克斯审判官就消失在了科罗努斯扩区的遥远群星之间，没有留下一丝踪迹。

English

The Inquisitor became known as one who showed lenience toward small vices and who was prone to using questionable methods. Decades later, van Calox's own students branded him a dangerous radical, someone ready to use the instruments of Chaos to achieve his ends. Before they could hand their mentor over to the tribunal, the Inquisitor disappeared without a trace among the distant stars at the edge of the Expanse.

冯·卡洛克斯审判官将所有的精力用来创建秘密警察制度，在暗中控制着科罗努斯扩区中最有影响力的人物，将恐惧植入了所有总督与统治者心中。然而，随着时间的流逝，海因里希的手段变得越来越极端，最终倒向了激进派，使用异形的造物乃至混沌的工具来监视、操纵人们。帝国海军和落脚港联合发动了兵变，结束了他的暴政。然而，当海因里希意识到自己走投无路时，这个异端分子将禁忌的力量倾泻在了他的敌人头上，拉上了几百名无辜之人与他一同赴死。

English

The Inquisitor poured all his energy into the creation of a secret police, which he used to clandestinely control the most influential figures in the Expanse. He struck fear in every governor and liege in the region. As time passed, Heinrix's methods became more extreme. Descending into radicalism, he employed the tools of xenos and even Chaos to spy on people and manipulate them. A joint uprising by the Imperial Navy and Footfall put an end to his tyranny, and yet, cast down and cornered, the heretic brought forbidden powers to bear against his enemies, taking hundreds of innocents with him to the grave.

When Inquisitor van Calox's time came to an end, he left behind a massive retinue of acolytes. One of them assumed his mentor's mantle when the Inquisitor fell fighting unknown xenos in the galactic south of the Expanse.

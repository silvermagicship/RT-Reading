卡片 016 ^winterscale-card-016

中文

艾维尼·温特斯凯尔举行了继任颂典，成为王朝的新任行商浪人。尽管许多人认为高贵的艾维尼优柔寡断，但他却敢于对他人报以信任，哪怕是那些不值得信任的人也不例外。这位年轻的行商浪人没有忘记是谁将他从莱卡德星系解救出来，也没有忘记冯·瓦兰修斯家族对温特斯凯尔家族的恩情。

English

Evayne Winterscale underwent his Magnae Accessio and became his dynasty's new Rogue Trader. Many said that noble Evayne lacked firmness of judgement, yet he had more than enough trust, even in those who were unworthy of it. The young Rogue Trader did not forget those who had saved him from the Rykad System or the favours that House von Valancius had bestowed on the Winterscales.

卡片 173 ^winterscale-card-173

中文

卡里戈斯残忍与愤怒的统治结束了。在那之后，众多目睹了他邪恶罪行的目击者终于鼓起勇气，颤颤巍巍地走出来，陈述事实。曾几何时，这个王朝在卡里戈斯·温特斯凯尔的英明统治下繁荣昌盛。而如今，他犯下的无数血腥罪行让那段美好的岁月黯然失色。

English

The era of Calligos's cruelty and wrath came to an end. Only then did the many terrified witnesses to his vile crimes step forward to recount them. Memories of the good, prosperous years for the protectorate under the wise rule of Calligos Winterscale were eclipsed by the legacy of his innumerable and bloody crimes.

卡片 193 ^winterscale-card-193

中文

卡里戈斯·温特斯凯尔公然接受颅骨之神的庇护之后，化身成了一股炽热的风暴，席卷了整个科罗努斯扩区。他轰炸了无数和平的行星，摧毁了遭遇的一切虚空舰。他的王朝变成了海盗与异端分子的巢穴，需要立刻进行净化。

English

After openly embracing the patronage of the Skull God, Calligos Winterscale swept over the Expanse like a fiery tornado, bombing peaceful planets and destroying voidships. His dynasty's realm became a nest of piracy and heresy that required immediate purging.

卡片 203 ^winterscale-card-203

中文

卡里戈斯·温特斯凯尔抵挡住了身边的各种诱惑，回到了他的领地，开始拼凑他那破碎的王朝。直到他去世的那一天，他都全身心地致力于拯救那些被抛弃的世界，保护幸存者。遗憾的是，他所剩的时间有限，无法完成太多事情。他断然拒绝了一切可以让他恢复活力、再次恢复行动能力的植入物。在接下来的数十年里，他始终端坐在他的王座之上，片刻都不曾离开，直至生命的最后一刻。

English

Resisting the temptations that besieged him, Calligos Winterscale returned to his domain and began to piece together the shattered remains of his protectorate. Until his dying day, his actions and thoughts were dedicated to saving abandoned worlds and protecting survivors. Alas, he was not able to achieve much in the time he was allotted. He breathed his last breath on the same throne that he refused to leave for decades, adamantly refusing augmetics that would restore mobility and power to his legs.

卡片 560 ^winterscale-card-560

中文

几年后，扩区在大批敌军的猛攻下摇摇欲坠。遵照其傲慢统治者的命令，一个早已消亡的文明从墓穴中苏醒，试图让所有低等种族重新臣服，再加以铁腕统治。为了战胜这个不知疲倦也不知恐惧的敌人，温特斯凯尔和库尔达两大王朝被逼到了灭亡边缘，唯有冯·瓦兰修斯属地在异形入侵中安然无恙。有传言说，行商浪人不知用什么手段，和异形首领达成了交易。这个说法盘踞幸存者们的内心长达数十年之久。

English

A few years later, the Expanse quaked under the onslaught of enemy hordes. Heeding the orders of their arrogant overlords, the tombs of a long-dead civilisation awakened to bring the inferior races back into submission and rule with an iron hand. Victory over an enemy that knew neither weakness nor fear drove both the Winterscale and Chorda dynasties to the brink of extinction. Only the von Valancius protectorate survived the xenos unscathed, and rumours that the Rogue Trader had somehow managed to negotiate with their leaders plagued the minds of the survivors for decades after.

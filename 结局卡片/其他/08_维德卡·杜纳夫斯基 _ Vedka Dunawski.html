首席战士薇德卡·杜纳芙斯卡拒绝接受总指挥官的背叛。整整六天，她将自己反锁在舱室中逃避现实，并向任何试图进入的人开火。第七天，在失去了一只眼睛和所有希望之后，一个浑身遍布血痂，精神破碎的生物从她的舱室里走出，顺从地回到了她首席战士的岗位上。

English

Arch-Militant Vedka Dunawski refused to accept her commander-in-chief's betrayal. For six days she shielded herself from the new reality in her quarters, firing at anyone who tried to enter. On the seventh day, having lost an eye and all hope, a scabbed and broken creature emerged from her quarters and obediently returned to her post as Arch-Militant.

English

Arch-Militant Vedka Dunawski led the righteous army of the von Valancius dynasty with unprecedented zeal. After her heroic death in battle against Chaos cultists, her likeness was revered as a holy icon and placed on the regimental banner of the 32nd. After that, fellow soldiers believed that just speaking her name aloud was enough to keep them safe from heresy and corruption.

English

Arch-Militant Vedka Dunawski served the dynasty faithfully for decades, inspiring the fighters of Zarkov by her example. Each grievous wound she received only served to toughen her further — and add more iron to her body. But Vedka met her death not on the battlefield, but on the operating table during the implantation of another augment to keep her wrecked body alive.

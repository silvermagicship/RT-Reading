English

At the end of the seventh year, Vedka disappeared from her office without a trace — mere days before the Zarkov army was struck by a deadly disease that had never been seen before. It is said that shortly afterward, a horrifying priestess of the dark powers appeared on Zarkov — the one-eyed, ulcer-riddled Hope Breaker, who turned the souls of the righteous away from the God-Emperor with her words of salvation in despair.

然而，行商浪人的行为背离了神圣泰拉所规定的秩序，帝国无法原谅这样的恶行。帝国对冯·瓦兰修斯王朝提出了指控，认为这个贸易王朝扰乱了帝国的正轨。入侵舰队在卡利西斯星区集结，这个年轻的乌托邦，终究未能比它的创造者多活一日。

English

The Imperium could not forgive such insolent deviation from the order prescribed by Holy Terra. The von Valancius dynasty was accused of the seditious formation of their own empire. An invasion fleet gathered in the Calixis Sector, and the young utopia did not outlive its creator.

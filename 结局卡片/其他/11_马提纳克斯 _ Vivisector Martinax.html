English

Genetor Ferrenz, who was left in charge of the Sector Bionica, dedicated his life to researching implants capable of replicating the precision of a chirurgeon's hand movements. Vivisector Martinax assisted him in his research and dictated to the assigned servo-skull one of the most comprehensive treatises on the nature of augmentation in the Koronus Expanse.

Vivisector Martinax, left in charge of the Sector Bionica's chirurgeons, soon discovered that the bay's junior medicae officers had succumbed to the heresy of xenotech. The Vivisector's authority was ended by means of a quiet and swift poisoning, rumoured to have been perpetrated by his partner, Genetor Ferrenz.

Vivisector Martinax, left in charge of the Sector Bionica's chirurgeons, served on the flagship as long as his hands could hold a scalpel. When age and fatigue took his strength and talent, the Vivisector's remains were used in the creation of a medicae servo-skull by Genetor Ferrenz. This great honour allowed him to continue his work for the Rogue Trader.

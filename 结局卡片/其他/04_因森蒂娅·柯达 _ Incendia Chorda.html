The ship was undocked from the station and left to drift toward Furibundus with no fuel and almost no air. Octaviana was not on board. 几年后，扩区在大批敌军的猛攻下摇摇欲坠。遵照其傲慢统治者的命令，一个早已消亡的文明从墓穴中苏醒，试图让所有低等种族重新臣服，再加以铁腕统治。为了战胜这个不知疲倦也不知恐惧的敌人，温特斯凯尔和库尔达两大王朝被逼到了灭亡边缘，唯有冯·瓦兰修斯属地在异形入侵中安然无恙。有传言说，行商浪人不知用什么手段，和异形首领达成了交易。这个说法盘踞幸存者们的内心长达数十年之久。

English

A few years later, the Expanse quaked under the onslaught of enemy hordes. Heeding the orders of their arrogant overlords, the tombs of a long-dead civilisation awakened to bring the inferior races back into submission and rule with an iron hand. Victory over an enemy that knew neither weakness nor fear drove both the Winterscale and Chorda dynasties to the brink of extinction. Only the von Valancius protectorate survived the xenos unscathed, and rumours that the Rogue Trader had somehow managed to negotiate with their leaders plagued the minds of the survivors for decades after.

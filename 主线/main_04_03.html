场景

“舰长大人，我试着解释您很忙，但——”音阵大师话音未落，就被粗鲁地打断了。
"Lord Captain, I tried to explain that you were busy but—" The Vox Master does not have time to finish her sentence before she is unceremoniously interrupted.

“你已经被腐化吞噬了。你每做一个决定，我都会坐立不安，感觉自己不该再跟在你身边了。快住手吧，趁现在还来得及，在帝皇面前忏悔吧。”
"You are consumed by corruption. With every decision you make, I feel more and more that I do not belong in your retinue. Stop. Repent before the Emperor — before it is too late."

“{name}，你的行径引起了我的担忧。老实说，我十分担忧。如果我们最终不得不刀剑相向，那就太遗憾了。”
"{name}, your actions concern me. They concern me a great deal. It would be quite unfortunate if we found ourselves on opposing sides."

“你的行为玷污了你的名声，狼领。很多时候，邪恶的后果都是你一手造成的。你没有遵循全父给予你的法则行事，我请求你赶紧停下来，否则就来不及了。我可不想亲手惩戒我的救命恩人。”
"Your actions have earned you a grim reputation, Aett-Vater. Ill deeds have too often been done by your hand. You do not live according to the law given by the Allfather, and so I ask you to stop before you go too far. I am loath to visit punishment upon my own saviour."

“你已经踏上了危险的道路，外界人。我能从你的眼神、行为、声音和心跳中看出来。离开这条道路吧，虽然不断蔓延的腐化仍然会撕裂你的灵魂，但至少不会将它完全摧毁！还是说，你已经在这个世界里迷失太深，渴望将自己彻底托付给腐化的怀抱？”
"You have started down a dangerous path, elantach. I can see it in your eyes, your actions, the sound of your voice, the beating of your heart. Leave this path while the growing corruption can still be torn from your soul without completely destroying it! Or are you so lost in this world that you long to lose yourself as well?"

I can turn a blind eye to the occasional misdemeanour, but when they become systemic... Suffice to say that the Inquisition's conversations with hardened heretics tend to be short, and I'd rather not have one of those conversations with you."

Farewell

剧情推进 / Story transition

在遗迹中找到卡尔卡扎留下的关键线索、任务更新为前往墓志铭世界后，队伍抵达终局区域，并与同伴进行出发前的告别。

After finding Calcazar's key trail in the ruins and receiving the objective to reach Epitaphia, the party arrives in the endgame area and says its farewells before proceeding.

场景

阿贝拉德带着疲惫的微笑看着你，忍不住以他惯有的方式，向你投来了带有评价意味的目光。“您看，舰长大人……我对您的服务，是在极其不寻常的情况下开始的。至于现在，您把我们置于比以往任何时候都更加令人吃惊、更加令人出乎意料的境地，我对此一点都不意外。有件事情我很肯定——冯·瓦兰修斯王朝今日将沐浴在荣耀之中。”
Abelard looks at you with a tired smile. He cannot resist giving you his characteristic appraising glance. "Well, Lord Captain... My service to you began under the most remarkable circumstances. I should hardly be surprised that you have delivered us into circumstances more unexpected and surprising than ever before. Of one thing I am certain, the von Valancius dynasty will cover itself in glory today."

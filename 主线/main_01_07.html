找到穿梭机 / Find a shuttle

  • 星港已经被异端分子和难民占领了，很难再找到完好无损的穿梭机。但即便如此，也必须继续寻找，因为这是离开这颗行星最可行的方式。
  • The starport has been overrun by heretics and refugees, making it hard to find an intact shuttle. Still, it is the most viable way of leaving the planet.
  • 地点：星港 / Starport

保护穿梭机 / Protect the shuttles

  • “终末黎明”的神秘领袖，又一个自称“极光”的人，打算摧毁所有的穿梭机，这样就没人能离开小莱卡德了。每有一架穿梭机遭到摧毁，就相当于几百人失去了逃离行星的希望，只能慢慢地被毁灭之力吞噬。
  • A mysterious leader of the Cult of the Final Dawn, the second person to bear the name of Aurora, intends to destroy all the shuttles so no one can leave Rykad Minoris. Every shuttle destroyed by him equals hundreds of people unable to escape the planet slowly being devoured by the Ruinous Powers.
  • 地点：星港 / Starport

乘坐穿梭机撤离 / Evacuate on the shuttle

  • 所有异端分子已经肃清，领主舰长可以乘坐其余的穿梭机离开小莱卡德。建议领主舰长片刻都不要耽误，立刻离开这个地方。
  • The heretic has been purged. Now the Lord Captain can leave Rykad Minoris on the remaining shuttle. The recommended course of action is to grasp this opportunity without delay.
  • 地点：冯·瓦兰修斯虚空舰，舰桥 / Bridge, von Valancius Voidship

拼死一搏 / Make a last stand

  • 所有的穿梭机都被摧毁了，领主舰长已经没有办法离开这个地方了。目前建议的行动方针是：准备好所有还能用的武器，面对势不可挡的敌人，发起最后的反抗。
  • All the shuttles have been destroyed: the probability of the Lord Captain's extraction is zero. The recommended course of action is to reload all available weaponry and prepare to make a last stand against the overwhelming enemy forces.
  • 地点：星港 / Starport

大逃亡 / The Escape

  • 小莱卡德在混沌大敌无情的攻势下灭亡了。尽管领主舰长做了最大的努力，但终末黎明的异端预言还是实现了。如果不想被毁灭之力吞噬，就必须立刻逃离这颗行星。
  • Rykad Minoris is falling to the relentless assault of the Archenemy's forces. Despite the Lord Captain's best efforts, the heretical prophecy of the Final Dawn has come to pass. Those who do not want to be devoured by the Ruinous Powers must flee the planet.

与导航者交谈 / Talk to the Navigator

  • 中文：导航者圣所中的敌人已经尽数消灭，但导航者本人的状况令人担忧。
  • English: The enemies in the Navigator's Sanctum have been eliminated, but the warp guide's condition gives cause for concern.
  • 地点：导航者圣所 / Navigator's Sanctum

场景

椅子上的这个生物似乎已经与椅子融为了一体。你看到这个人的四肢上长着许多额外的关节，与正常人的样子截然不同。羊皮纸般的粉红色皮肤从他的四肢蔓延出来，覆盖在座椅周围。此刻，这位导航者正在大口呼吸，空气从他脸上两条如鳃一般的暗沉缝隙中流过，发出了嘶嘶的声音。导航者此刻闭着眼睛，无论是两只普通的眼睛，还是位于额头中央处的第三只眼，都是紧闭着的。
The creature in the chair appears as if it has become one with its throne: you see pink, parchment-like skin stretched between the seat and the limbs that have too many joints for a normal human being. The Navigator is breathing heavily, the air whistling as it exits through the two dark gill-like slits on its cheeks. The creature's eyes are shut — the two ordinary eyes as well as the third one that sits in the centre of its forehead.

“我相信维斯彼亚达大师还活着。”阿贝拉德看到这只生物被拉长的皮肤与脸上的黑色印记时，不禁皱起了眉。“所有仆人都已经死了……但感谢帝皇，这个房间依然没有被亚空间入侵。嗯，或许我们依然有机会活着离开亚空间……”
"I believe Master Vespiadas still lives..." Abelard flinches at the sight of the stretched skin and the dark marks on the face of the creature. "The servants are dead... but the chamber, thank the Emperor, is still sealed from the warp. Well, we have a chance of leaving this place alive..."

阿贝拉德清了清嗓子。“维斯彼亚达大师……”
Abelard clears his throat. "Master Vespiadas..."

导航者颤抖着，勉强抬起了头，半睁开两只正常的眼睛。他的第三只眼紧闭着，一滴黑色的液体从第三只眼的眼睑处流下，在脸上留下了一条黑色的痕迹。“你的时间……已经不多了……”他的声音来自怪异座椅底部的音阵合成器，你并不清楚导航者为什么会说人类的语言。
The Navigator stirs, barely able to lift his head and half-open his normal eyes. A dark drop rolls from under the closed lid of the third eye and down his face, leaving behind a black trail. "Your time... is short..." The voice is coming from the vox-grille at the base of the bizarre chair. It is unclear how the Navigator is able to produce human speech.

统一回答 / Shared response:

突然之间，导航者的身体在椅子上颤抖起来。他猛地朝前面扑了过来，之后又跌回到了椅子上，随后再次冲了过来。他剧烈的动作甚至折断了他的骨头，撕裂了他的皮肉。然而，他的皮肤却与椅子紧紧地融合在了一起。尽管这个变种人想要冲过来，但他的身体依然坐在椅子上，只是尽可能地向前倾而已。他层层叠叠的衣服慢慢被鲜血染红，脸上的鳃也迸裂开来，仿佛两张嘴在狞笑着、尖叫着。“跪倒在地吧，凡人！凝视着最后的黎明吧！”
Suddenly, the Navigator's body starts thrashing in place: it lurches forward, then falls back onto the seat, then thrusts forward again as its bones crack and its skin tears open. However, the fusion between the body and the chair appears to be stronger than these wild impetuses: the mutant remains seated but leans as far forward as he can, his layered raiment slowly turning crimson. The gill slits burst open, forming two hideous screaming mouth-like pits. "Fall to your knees, mortals, and behold the final dawn!"

伊迪拉惊恐地尖叫起来。“是从亚空间里来的东西！他的身体已经不属于他了！”
Idira shrieks in terror. "It came from beyond! His body is not his!"

伊迪拉的尖叫声在你耳边响起，你的脑海中也传来了无声的呐喊。看到那位导航者顺从于腐化的力量之后，某个看不到的存在发出了胜利的呼喊。
Idira's scream is echoed by the silent cries in your head — the unseen creature triumphs at the sight of the Navigator succumbing to the paroxysms of corruption.

“它的步伐肩负着希望的重担，如雷鸣一般滚滚向前！敞开你们的心扉吧，我将用无数文字与含义淹没你们的灵魂，每个文字都代表着我的救赎！”导航者继续在座位上抽搐着，发出了尖叫声与咯咯的笑声，但随即突然停了下来。他的脸已经被黑色与深红色的液体浸透了。第三只眼渗出的黑色液体与鼻孔中流出来的血液混合在一起，眼睛周围也浮现出了紫红色的肿胀。
"Its march thunders under the burden of hopes! Unseal your hearts, and I will flood your souls with myriads of words and meanings, each one a portent of salvation within me!" The Navigator continues to convulse violently in his seat, shrieking and cackling, but then his fit stops abruptly. His face is drenched in black and crimson — the dark ooze from under his third eyelid has turned from a trickle into a stream mixed with the blood coming out of his nose, and there is purplish swelling around the eye.

“快跑……趁我还有力量，快逃出这个地方……”音阵合成器中传来了痛苦的呢喃。“我可以抵抗……入侵……但坚持不了太久。必须立刻进行跃迁……离开亚空间……但只有得到舵手长的协助，才能启动飞船……”
"Run... Flee from this place while I still have the strength..." The words come out of the vox as a laboured rattle. "I can contain... the intrusion... but not for long. We must begin the translation... leave the warp... but it can't be done without the Master Helmsman's help..."

"The Edge..." The Navigator starts thrashing about once more, his gill-mouths bursting with crazed laughter. "Kneel before the ascent of a new champion! When the rite is complete, this shell will burst, and thousands of maws will accept the sacrifice offered to the Edge of Daybreak by the bringer of twilight, the one marked by the will of the gods!"

每个字都像钟声一样，响彻你的脑海，仿佛无数声音加入了这场颂唱之中，在你耳边尖叫着。有那么一瞬间，你仿佛迷失了方向。这邪恶的和声肯定是打算用来自另一个世界的声音来迷惑你。
Each word rings through your head like the tolling of a bell, as if countless voices have joined in, screaming into your ears. For a moment, you lose your bearings — this unholy chorus must be trying to confound you with sounds not meant for this world.

"What? Kunrad betrayed Lady Theodora?" Idira clutches her head. "But how is that... How... He was as loyal as a dog! And I didn't hear a thing, I swear!"

那位导航者，或者是那个打算夺取他身体的实体，爆发出了嘲弄的笑声。“暮光先驱者的脚步声被隐藏了起来，因为这是他的主人们的意志。挖出你的眼睛吧，姑娘，因为你即将成为暮光勇士的仆人，你已经不需要眼睛了！”
The Navigator, or rather the thing that is so determined to seize his body, erupts into mocking laughter. "The footfalls of the bringer of twilight are veiled by the will of his masters. Tear out your eyes, she who is to become the champion's servant, for you need them no longer!"

The Seneschal gazes at the figure in the chair. "The ship can only begin the translation with the assistance of the Master Helmsman... if he is still alive. It is worth a try. We must get to the bridge, but we will need support. Our only option is to fight our way to the officers' deck and join forces with Edelthrad and his people.

  • 艾德萨德准备反攻舰桥，消灭昆拉德和他的异端手下。为了进行反击，他需要一切可以得到的帮助。
  • Edelthrad was going to confront Kunrad and his heretics on the bridge. He will need all the help he can get for the counter-attack to succeed.

场景

艾德萨德捂着胸前流血的伤口，充满恨意地瞪着你。“你这个叛徒……我们需要所有的力量对付敌人，可你却选择了袖手旁观！别打算谎称说什么没收到我的音阵信息！”
Edelthrad, who is clutching the bleeding wound on his chest, gives you a hateful glare. "Traitor... Right when we needed all forces to deliver a decisive blow, you stepped aside! Do not even try to claim that my vox-casts did not reach you!"

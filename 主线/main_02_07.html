场景

穿着华美黑色盔甲的异形扭过头看着你，他瘦削、苍白的脸上布满了纹身与血迹。过了一会儿，他的薄唇舒展开来，露出了令人不快的扭曲笑容。“终于来了，你还真是不急着来见我啊，猴子。”几秒钟过去了，你意识到阐释器并没有在运行——眼前的这只异形正在用低哥特语与你对话。
The xenos in ornate black armour turns his head to you. His thin, pale face is covered in tattoos and blood spatters. After a moment, his thin lips stretch into a crooked, unpleasant smile. "At last. You took your time coming to meet me, mon-keigh." A couple of seconds pass before you realise that the Elucidator is silent — the xenos is addressing you in Low Gothic.

"How is it that out of all of your kind, you are the only one to discover our feasts in advance? Usually, you are all so slow and sluggish that my kin always have enough time to leave the battlefield before aid arrives. But still, time and time again, your dogs have been there to stand in the Kabal's way." He pauses, waiting for your answer.

找到西奥多拉·冯·瓦兰修斯 / Find Theodora von Valancius

  • 西奥多拉·冯·瓦兰修斯刚一离开军官甲板就不见了人影，这到底是怎么回事？
  • Theodora von Valancius vanished right after leaving the officers' deck. What could be behind her disappearance?
  • 地点：领主舰长宿舍 / Lord Captain's Quarters

场景

西奥多拉·冯·瓦兰修斯的尸体伏在沾满鲜血的大桌子上。首席战士的尸体手中紧握着武器，靠在舱壁旁边。
Theodora von Valancius's body is sprawled on the large, blood-drenched desk. The Arch-Militant's corpse, still clutching a weapon, is slumped against one of the bulkheads.

战斗修女勘察着现场，目光停留在首席战士的尸体上。“帝皇在上，请接受这些虔诚的灵魂吧……”
The Sister of Battle surveys the scene, her gaze lingering on the Arch-Militant's body. "Emperor, accept Thy faithful souls..."

“不——！！”伊迪拉撕心裂肺地尖叫起来，整个船舱仿佛都在摇晃。她跪倒在地，身体颤抖着，嚎啕大哭。“我……舰长大人，我没听到！我什么都没听到！就连低语声和呼吸声都没有！那声音……明明这么响亮！可我为什么没有预见到这件事情？”
"NOOO!" Idira's heart-rending scream shakes the walls. She collapses to her knees, her body shuddering as she weeps. "I... Lord Captain, I didn't hear! Didn't hear a thing! Not a whisper, not a breath! It was... It was so loud! Why didn't I foresee this?"

阿贝拉德惊呆了。他朝前迈了几步，沉重地靠在桌边，目光死死地盯着西奥多拉的尸体。“舰长大人……究竟是谁，竟敢……除非……是维特威尔那个叛徒……”
Abelard, stunned, takes a few steps forward and leans heavily on the edge of the desk. His eyes are locked on Theodora's body. "Lord Captain... Who would even dare... Unless... that rat Voigtvir..."

Abelard runs his hand over his face. "First, betrayal, now the murder of the Rogue Trader... and Mort!"

“莫特怎么会这么不中用？我们的首席战士出生在死亡世界，他的反应就像闪电一样机敏……”
"How could Mort have failed her so terribly? The Arch-Militant was born on a death world, he had lightning-fast reflexes..."

伊迪拉啜泣着站了起来。她扫视着四周，看起来不知所措。“我……我可以看到某些东西，以我自己的方式。如果你找到了什么东西……比如带有凶手印记的东西……把它交给我就好，我可以看到其中隐藏的秘密！”
Idira sobs and gets up. She glances around, looking lost. "I... I can look at things. In my own way. If you find something... something that might bear the imprint of the killer... just give it to me, and I'll try to see what secrets it holds!"

[检查西奥多拉的尸体]
[Examine Theodora's body]

你凑近尸体。西奥多拉的死因显而易见，她的生命被一发自动手枪的子弹带走了，而且这一枪无比精确。西奥多拉脸上挂着惊讶的表情，这便是她生前最后一刻感受到的情绪。
You lean closer to the body. The cause of death is apparent: the Rogue Trader's life was ended by an autopistol shot, an extraordinarily accurate one at that. Theodora's face bears an expression of surprise — the last emotion she experienced in life.

地板上的某件东西引起了你的注意，那是一堆溅满血迹的羊皮纸和卷轴，肯定是从桌子上掉下来的。当你俯下身子仔细观察时，你还注意到了另一件东西——地上散落着细小的玻璃碎片。
Something on the floor catches your eye: a heap of blood-spattered parchments and scrolls that must have fallen off the desk. As you lean down for a closer look, you notice something else: small shards of glass scattered on the floor.

  • 选择 Choice

“伊迪拉，你可不可以碰一下西奥多拉试试？说不定你可以透过她的双眼看到凶手。”
"Idira, would you try touching Theodora? You might be able to see the killer through her eyes."

伊迪拉整个身体都在颤抖着。“我的能力不是这么用的。我可以试着读取物品的记忆……但尸体就是另一回事了。如果想感知她的记忆，我需要找到她的灵魂……不过，军官甲板上的惨状，我们也都看到了。我可不打算在亚空间里铤而走险。”
Idira's whole body shudders. "It doesn't work like that. I can try to read the memories of objects... but dead bodies are a different story. Something like that would require her soul... and after what we saw on the officers' deck, I won't risk searching for it in the warp."

[检查首席战士的尸体]
[Examine the Arch-Militant's body]

自动手枪的子弹射穿了莫特的额头，血液、脑浆与头骨碎片喷洒在地板与四周的家具上。莫特武器上的保险栓已经拉开了，恐怕，他的动作只比凶手慢了那么一点点。
An autopistol shot went straight through Mort's forehead, spraying the floor and the furniture around with blood, brain matter, and shards of his skull. The safety on the Arch-Militant's weapon is off — the killer must have been a split second quicker than he.

[检查桌子旁边的碎片]

很难说被打碎的东西究竟是什么。你唯一能确定的，就是那个东西掉在了地板上，摔碎了。仔细检查之后，你发现这些碎片并非完全透明，而是呈现出了彩虹般的色彩，就好像之前被浸泡在了钷素中一样。
It is difficult to say what exactly was broken — the object fell on the floor, and that is as much as you can ascertain. Upon closer examination, the shards appear iridescent rather than completely transparent, as if they had been submerged in promethium.

Idira scoops up a handful of shards and clenches them in her fist with such strength that blood starts dripping on the floor. "I see... streaks of colour, luminescence, blue lights... faces... drawing closer... Lady Theodora... and... Kunrad... and... what is that? Ah!" She shakes her hand, looking frightened. "Something dark... enormous... Oh, {name}, this glass thing was something foul, that's for sure!"

“邪恶的巫术……”你分辨不出阿洁塔这句话指的是玻璃碎片，还是伊迪拉本人。
"Tainted witchcraft..." You cannot tell if Argenta's remark is directed at the glass shards or Idira herself.

Reports, accounts, dispatches... You go over the scrolls until you stumble upon a document that is starkly different from the rest. It is a handwritten letter signed with the initials "X. C." and a seal with a symbol that is vaguely familiar to you...

"So many things can three eyes glimpse at once:
Sha'eil's veiled paths and caverns of the soul,
But with a thousand sights you could not see
The wrecks of fate beneath reality!
Past's creaking waltz will twirl you round in halls
of intertwining serendipity,
But not just yet — the dance calls for a pair,
Your partner is, for now, tied up with me."

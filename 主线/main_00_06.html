这句话在你的意识中产生了共鸣，与那些早已扎根的声音融合在一起。片刻之后，你意识到，所有的声音都是由某个实体发出来的，那个实体的呼唤已经侵入了你的意识。
It resonates and merges with the voices that have already dug their foul roots into your consciousness. A moment later, you realise that all these voices are the myriad manifestations of the same call that has finally invaded your mind.

场景

眼前的一幕令你惊恐不安，你看到了几个长相完全相同的血伶人。他们朝你转过了那张你熟悉的脸，以郁郁寡欢的神情盯着你，满脸冷漠。
You are looking at several completely identical Haemonculi at work. A disturbing sight. The indistinguishable, and familiar, faces turn their attention toward you, morosely and almost indifferently.

眼前的血伶人一个接着一个开了口。“是你！”其中一个齿间发着嘶嘶声。“你竟敢……”另一个血伶人的回声传了过来。“……活着出现在这个地方！”第三个人开了口。“不过，这样更好……”——“我要用你的鲜血洗刷我死亡的耻辱！”——“之后再把你和你身边的渣滓变成更有用的东西！”
The Haemonculi start talking over one another. "You!" one of them hisses through his teeth. "How dare you..." echoes another. "...show up here alive!" a third one finishes. "This is even better, though..." — "...now I'll cleanse the shame of my death with your blood..." — "...and then transform you, and the scum you've brought along, into something useful!"

这个房间之中散落着破碎的岩凝土块。一阵微风从天花板上丑陋的大洞里吹了进来，在你脚边形成了一股小小的回旋气流。

The hole in the lab's vaulted ceiling and the hollow underneath it point you to the obvious conclusion: something broke into this room, ripped out a piece of the lab, and dragged it outside. You are, for now, mystified as to what that something was.

前往导航者圣所 / Reach the Navigator's Sanctum

  • 如果导航者不幸遇难，那整艘舰船在亚空间跳跃的过程中，恐怕都难逃殒灭的命运。其他甲板上发生的事情都可以暂时放下，必须先前往导航者圣所，看看亚空间的向导究竟遭遇了什么事情。
  • The Navigator's untimely demise would spell disaster for the voidship when making a warp jump. Whatever is happening on the other decks, it is imperative to reach the Navigator's Sanctum and see what has happened to the vessel's warp guide.
  • 地点：导航者圣所 / Navigator's Sanctum

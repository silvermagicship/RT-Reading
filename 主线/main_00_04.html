找到首席战士 / Find the Arch-Militant

  • 中文：军官甲板遭到了未知敌人的袭击，首席战士莫特已经离开甲板，前去清理通往上层甲板的道路，然而，他还没有回来，得去弄清楚他究竟被什么事情耽搁了。
  • English: The officers' deck is under attack by an unknown enemy, so Arch-Militant Mort has left to clear the way to the lift leading to the upper decks. The officer has not returned yet, so it is time to find out the cause of the delay.
  • 地点：军官甲板 / Officers' Deck

事件型说明

  • 包含军官甲板遇袭、穿越火焰与可选雇佣护卫内容。

场景

西奥多拉挥着手，做了个命令性的手势。“莫特！带上你的人，把通往运输线的道路清理出来！那群狗娘养的，一个都不要放过！”
Theodora waves her hand in a commanding gesture. "Mort! Take a team with you and clear a path to the transport line! Do not let a single whoreson survive!"

行商浪人将目光转向了你。“那头畜生冲到甲板上的时候，你甚至连眼睛都没眨，嗯？还不错嘛。我见过很多军官，他们一看到上层甲板发生战斗，就会吓得慌里慌张。那群人高高在上，活在虚假的安全感之中，他们更容易受到亚空间湍流的影响。”她看着手中的音阵装置，你只听到里面传来毫无生气的静电声。
The Rogue Trader turns her eyes to you. "Didn't bat an eye when that scum burst onto the deck, hmm? Not bad. I have seen many an officer who panicked at the sight of combat on the upper decks — the false sense of security offered by their position makes them that much more vulnerable to the harsh realities of warp travel." She looks at the vox in her hand. All you can hear is lifeless static.

场景

你耳边响起了火焰的轰鸣声，呛人的烟雾让你咳个不停，喘不过气来。你本能地避开了灼热的火焰，擦着脸。片刻之后，汹涌的火海之中出现了某个朦胧的人影。尽管你耳边到处都是刺耳的噪音与尖叫，但你却清楚地听到了那个身影的脚步声。随后，你看到那个身影从火焰中走了出来，朝你走来。那个人是西奥多拉·冯·瓦兰修斯。
The roar of the flames rings in your ears, and the choking smoke makes you cough and gasp for air. You instinctively recoil from the scorching heat, wiping your face. A moment later, a hazy silhouette forms in the raging wall of fire. You are surrounded by ear-splitting noise and screaming, yet you distinctly hear the sound of footsteps — and then you see the figure walk out of the fire toward you. It is Theodora von Valancius.

她的表情很平静。对于一个身体正在被火焰吞噬的人而言，她的表情平静得令人感到恐惧。她一只手搭在能量剑的剑柄上，另一只手高高举起，做了个邀请的手势。“你是迷路了吗，我的继承人？”
Her expression is calm — terrifyingly so for someone whose body is engulfed in an inferno. One of her hands is resting on the hilt of her power sword, the other is raised in an inviting gesture. "Are you lost, my heir?"

阿洁塔仿佛雕像一样愣在了原地，随后慢慢地举起了武器。“那是幽灵……是巫术召唤出来的幽灵！这是亚空间的诡计！”
Argenta goes as still as a statue, then slowly raises her weapon. "An apparition... A sorcerous apparition! Trickery of the warp!"

“修女，快把武器放下！”尽管阿贝拉德的语气充满了命令的意味，但他的话语似乎并不是很有信心。此刻，他正盯着行商浪人。你注意到，他脸颊上的肌肉在抽搐着。“威胁领主舰长是骇人听闻的恶行……”
"Sister, you will lower your weapon!" Despite his commanding tone, Abelard does not sound too confident — he is staring at the Rogue Trader, and you notice a muscle in his cheek twitching. "Threatening the Lord Captain is outrageous..."

"It is no easy feat to cross two sectors, especially for a common citizen, albeit one who has some resources at his disposal... And then, after all those years, having spent all of my savings, I reached my dream..."

“可我看到了什么？原本精致的门廊变成了垃圾堆！那些辉煌的柱子旁边到处都是金属制成的蚁丘！那些穷人偷走了大理石瓷砖，就为了盖他们的破茅屋！”戈尔代垂下了头。“我来到这里之后，已经过了十几年了，但我仍然没能从打击中恢复过来。我确实怀疑过，小册子上预想的一切或许没办法完全实现，但我却万万没想到，落脚港竟然陷入了如此糟糕的境地。就好像您无意间发现了您美丽爱人的尸体，而它早已腐烂得面目全非。”
"And what did I see? A dump in place of the exquisite portico! Metal anthills clinging to the columns! The marble tiles pilfered by paupers to use as cladding for their hovels!" Gordei hangs his head. "It's been a dozen years since I came here, and I still haven't recovered from the shock. I had suspected that not everything from the booklet would exist in reality, but I never expected to see Footfall in such a sorry state. It is like stumbling upon the horrible, decomposing corpse of your beautiful beloved."

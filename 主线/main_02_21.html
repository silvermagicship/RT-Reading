场景

“船长老大……”雷沃的声音听起来不太恼怒，更像是疲惫。“我收到了报告，说有几艘飞船正朝我们飞过来。他们对我们的呼叫没有回应，遥测数据也显示他们的宏炮炮台正在装填。由此看来，他们不是来闲聊的。只要你一声令下，我们就准备开战。”
"Lord Cap'n..." Ravor's voice over the vox sounds weary rather than annoyed. "I'm getting reports of several ships moving toward us. Judging by the fact that they're not responding to our hails and the telemetry shows a trail of macro batteries, they're not here for a chinwag. Ready to issue orders to prepare for battle at your command."

"The rubbish of Furibundus. And I'm not talking about these wrecks that are about to open fire, I mean the remains of meteorites, gas clouds, refuse from the shipyards... It all bunches together, and the smaller ships hide behind it to harass ships as they emerge from the warp."

"Could this be the work of Kunrad Voigtvir?"

音阵装置沉默了几秒钟。“有可能，说不定是他雇佣了一群渣滓……也说不定只是一群普通的海盗，打算找咱们来碰碰运气。”
The silence over the vox lasts for several seconds. "It's possible. He could have hired some scum... or maybe it's just a pirate who saw the state of our ship and decided to push his luck."

“这场战斗能避开吗？”
"Can we evade this combat?"

雷沃哼了一声，又或许是笑了笑。“这场战斗恐怕躲不掉，船长老大，而且也没理由躲。比起躲开他们，把他们炸成碎片要简单多了。咱们可以来上几轮齐射，警告整个落脚港，咱们到了！”
Ravor snorts, or perhaps chuckles. "I don't think so, Lord Cap'n. There'd be no reason to — it'll be easier to blast these scum to smithereens than to evade them. We'll warn Footfall we're coming with a few macro salvos!"

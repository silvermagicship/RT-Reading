序章｜摧毁失控的机仆 / Destroy the out-of-control servitor

任务上下文

摧毁失控的机仆 / Destroy the out-of-control servitor

  • 一台机仆发生了未知故障，正在袭击船员。必须阻止这台发了疯的机器！
  • An automaton has suffered an unknown malfunction and is now attacking people. The deranged machine must be stopped!
  • 地点：军官甲板 / Officers' Deck

事件型说明

  • 目标主体是战斗；独立对白在战斗结束后的 AfterKillingBeast。

场景

“这场事故发生的时机实在是太令人起疑了。机仆在军官甲板上发生了故障，而行商浪人与她的继承人刚好就在这里。”维特威尔目不转睛地看着地上的残躯，从腰带上取下了通讯器，快速说了几句命令，随后转向了你。“我已经封锁了上层甲板与下层甲板之间的所有通道。如果这是一场蓄谋已久的袭击，封锁甲板应该可以阻止那些敌人……实施他们的计划。”
"What suspiciously poor timing for such an accident. Servitors malfunction on the officers' deck at exactly the same moment when the Rogue Trader and her heirs are gathered here." Without taking his eyes off the bodies on the floor, Voigtvir removes the vox-caster from his belt and speaks a few quick orders into the device. Then he turns to you. "I have blocked all passages between the upper and lower sectors of the residential decks. If this is a deliberate attack, it should stop the culprits from... advancing their plan."

维特威尔额头上的皱纹平复下来，声音也恢复了一贯的礼貌。“恐怕我必须留在这里，{name}。为了西奥多拉夫人的安全，我必须亲自确保这些命令得到执行。希望您通往观景台的道路不会有什么危险，那个平台就在这条走廊尽头。”低语之主礼貌地向你鞠了一躬。
The lines on Voigtvir's forehead soften, and his voice regains its usual courtesy. "I'm afraid I must remain here, {name} — for Lady Theodora's safety, I have to oversee the execution of these orders personally. I hope you will have no difficulty reaching the observation platform on your own — it is just at the end of this corridor." The Master of Whispers gives you a curt bow.

场景

这只机械生物的四肢正在微弱地抽搐着。它的躯体已经残破不堪，满是战斗磨损的痕迹，但似乎根本没人打算把它修好。你看到它龟裂的外壳下面闪闪发光的肉体与渗透其中的无数电线。怪物沉重地抬起盔甲，发出了吱吱作响的叹息，充满了遗憾与渴望。
The biomechanical creature is flailing its limbs faintly. Its body is broken and bears the signs of combat, but it seems no one is planning to repair it. You can see the glistening flesh under the cracked carapace and the bunches of wires that permeate it. The armour plates rise heavily and the creature emits a wistful, creaking sigh full of regret.

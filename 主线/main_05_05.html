TechArcGrave

场景

巨大金属板黑曜石般的光滑表面泛起了涟漪，一组神秘的符号浮现其上，仿佛是被某个看不见的手指印上去的一般。其中，三个最明亮的符号分别代表“恶意”、“瘟疫”与“解体”。这些符号闪烁着预示性的光芒，宛如无数殉道者喘着粗气，静静等待生命消散的时刻。
The obsidian smoothness of the enormous slab begins to ripple and roil. A set of mysterious symbols appears on it, as if impressed on its black surface by an invisible finger. The three brightest ones are symbols for "Malefice," "Pestilence," and "Unravelling." They shimmer forebodingly, like a martyr gasping for air as life drains from them.

上面的符文闪烁着光芒。“我的主，您了解何为永恒。愤怒的星辰带来了毁灭的黎明，而我将遵从您的守护之言！被囚禁折磨的人，必须永远在无尽维度的迷宫中受到煎熬，以免他们的灾祸降临在我们身上。在这超脱现实的牢笼之中，只要有一丁点微小的缺口，就足以让憎恨的意志与毁灭的思想化为不灭的肉体，抹杀存在本身。我将手放在过去的容器上，我看到了那些违背戒律之人将面临的厄运。”
An inscription above them reads: "My Lord who hast known eternity, I heed mine guarding words that I spoke at the dawn of the wrathful stars! He who is condemned to torture by imprisonment must languish forever in the labyrinth of many dimensions, lest Their plagues befall us. A minute breach in the impossible prison would suffice for the spiteful will, the destructive thought incarnate in unperishing flesh, to obliterate existence. I lay my hand upon the vessel of the past, and I am revealed the doom that awaits one who breaks the commandments."

[Place your palm on the Malefice symbol]

金属板表面涌现出新的符号。它们以令人发狂的速度四处乱窜，相互重叠。在一片混乱之中，某段铭文紧紧地攫住了你的意识，让你感到一阵剧痛。“我的主，在我的永恒之中，我记得他们将遭受的厄运。我看到了第一种灾祸：恶意，这个虫洞会侵蚀思想的本质。”你眼前的一切忽然变得黯淡下来。
One after another, new inscriptions appear on the slab's surface. They dash around maddeningly fast, overlapping each other. Amid this chaos, only one inscription latches onto your mind, causing it acute pain: "My Lord, in my measureless eternity, I remember Their doom. I am revealed the first plague, the Malefice: a wormhole that invades the very essence of thought." Your vision goes dark.

现实仿佛一把冰冷的利刃，划破了永眠的黑暗。你感觉到了错误，并清楚地认识到了错误的本质。你的印记遭到了篡改、破坏，你想要动起来，但却徒劳无功，因为你被活活困在你的金属身体构成的牢狱之中。你的一部分同伴也面临着同样的命运。他们从停滞的棺柩中走出来的一瞬间，就像残缺不全的雕像一样僵在了那里，随后朝旁边倒了下去，重重地砸在地上。疯狂的军团漫不经心地从这些雕像身边走过，走向裂开的深渊，顺从地跳入它无情的巨口。
Reality cuts through the darkness of eternal sleep like a cold blade. You sense wrongness, and gain a clear understanding of the nature of that wrongness. Your engrams have been tampered with and damaged. You try to move, but in vain — you are buried alive in the metal crypt of your own body. Some of your fellows are faced with the same fate. Almost as soon as they emerge from their stasis sarcophagi, they freeze like mangled statues, tilt sideways, and collapse, crashing to the floor. Maddened legions march mindlessly past them toward a gaping chasm, and tumble obediently into its merciless maw.

你的手掌刚刚触碰到这个符号时，金属板就把它牢牢地吸住，像水一样紧紧裹住了它。随后，金属板上浮现出了文字。“我的主，我不敢将目光从我要守护的文字上移开！我看到了第二种灾祸：瘟疫。但仅仅看到它是不敢的，我还要理解它，正如我那没有生命的躯壳要理解自己的厄运一般。”
As soon as your palm touches the symbol, the slab's metal pulls it in, enveloping it like water. The text is transformed: "My Lord, I dare not divert my gaze from the guarding words! I am revealed the second plague, the Pestilence. It is not enough merely to behold it; I comprehend it, as my own lifeless shell learns the truth of its doom."

金属板上的符号开始疯狂变化，不断闪烁的光芒灼伤了你的眼睛，让你感到恶心。你感觉头痛欲裂，仿佛某个来自异界的实体将你的头脑不断挤压，直到它变成一团软泥，再按照它的意愿重新塑造。
The symbols begin to change at a frenzied pace. Their light burns your eyes, and their rapid flickering makes you nauseous. You are struck by a splitting headache, like an alien entity squeezing your mind out of shape as if it were soft clay, refashioning it after its own image.

你看到了一艘虚空舰，看起来仿佛一轮黑色的新月，舰体的镀层闪烁着绿色的光芒。在你眼前，这艘舰船各个部分开始碎裂、崩解，仿佛被无形的白蚁啃噬一般——这艘舰船正在被技术枯萎症吞噬。当它变成了一具没有生命的骨架时，你感到手掌一阵灼痛，仿佛这致命的瘟疫已经蔓延到了你的身上，正在吞噬你的肉体一般。
You see an image of a voidship: a black crescent shape, its plating veined with green lights. Right before your eyes, its various parts start crumbling and flaking away as though gnawed at by invisible termites. It is being consumed by tech-blight. When only a lifeless carcass is left, you feel a searing pain in your hand, as if the Pestilence has spread to your flesh and is now consuming it.

你再也无法忍受这样的疼痛，猛地将手抽了出来。你浑身上下毫发无损，原本闪烁的符号也凝滞在了金属板上，渐渐消逝。代表“瘟疫”的符号，是最后一个从金属板上消失的。
You cannot stand the pain anymore and jerk your hand out of the slab — unscathed. The flickering symbols freeze at once and begin to fade. The symbol for Pestilence is the last to disappear from the slab's surface.

“舰长大人！您没受伤吧？精神还好吗？我干嘛要问啊……答案早就显而易见了。”阿贝拉德小声嘟囔出了最后这句话。
"Lord Captain! Are you uninjured? Sound of mind? Why am I even asking... the answer has been obvious for a while now." The Seneschal mutters the last sentence under his breath.

乌尔法厌恶地抽着鼻子，开口问道。“狼领，你是不管碰到什么异形的脏东西，都非得伸指头戳一戳吗？还是说，你就好这口？要不要趁着还没出什么意外，我先把你的手砍下来？我动手很快，几乎不会有什么痛苦。然后我们再给你挑个不错的义肢……你不愿意？”
Wrinkling his nose in disgust, Ulfar asks, "Do you go poking your fingers in every kind of xenos filth you encounter, Aett-Vater, or have you just taken a liking to this? Would you like me to chop off that hand before something happens? It will be quick and almost painless. We'll choose a nice augment for it... No?"

伊奥贡的俄歇矩阵飞快地对你进行了扫描。“行商浪人未受到侵蚀。脑细胞的损伤仍在可接受范围内。”操纵者贪婪地瞥了一眼那石板，补充道，“允许继续接触。”
Eogunn's auspex briskly scans you. "The Rogue Trader is not compromised. Brain cell damage is within the acceptable range." With a greedy look at the slab, the Manipulus adds, "Repeated interaction is permissible."

The symbol you touched splits into three that are akin to it, similar and yet different from one another. A message materialises in front of you: "My Lord, Their third plague, the Unravelling, is beyond comprehension; therefore I put before myself a question bearing clarity."

“我扪心自问，我的主——如果我的存在出现在无数个永恒之中，那我的存在会是相同的吗？”此刻，你感觉到自己的意识淹没在了思绪与记忆的洪流中之中，这些记忆古老而陌生，却又坚不可摧。你以极为顽强的毅力，紧紧抓住其中一个画面，努力融入在异形的文字之中，以不属于你自己的声音，紧张地开了口……
"This I ask myself, my Lord: if my existence were to be reflected in myriad eternities, would they be the same?" Your mind is now drowning in a flood of thoughts and memories, ancient, alien, and indomitable. With a titanic effort of will, you hold on to one of the images and vest it in the sound of borrowed words, and utter it in a strained voice that is not yours...

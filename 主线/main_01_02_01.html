第一章｜欧拉克五号星站与卡西娅 / Eurac V and Cassia

归档说明

  • 导航者星站线是第一章招募卡西娅并恢复亚空间航行能力的主线地点。
  • NavisCogitator 中有帕斯卡可参与的机器/沉思者交互，作为同伴附着上下文保留在主线章节中。

任务上下文

探寻星站，寻找答案 / Explore the station to find answers

  • 中文：行商浪人需要招募一名新的导航者，但导航宗族没有任何回应。他们的星站发生了某些古怪的事情，似乎需要外来人员进行干预。
  • English: The Rogue Trader needs to recruit a new Navigator, but the Navis Nobilite heralds are not responding, and something strange is happening on their void station. It appears outside intervention is required.
  • 地点：欧拉克五号星站 / Eurac V

重新启动电梯 / Restart the elevator

  • 中文：根据星站的守护者所言，这里发生了暴乱，一群疯子抓走了他的女主人。目前，卡西娅女士被囚禁在了上层大厅，但电梯发生了故障，任何人都无法前往该区域。
  • English: The Keeper of the Station spoke of a rebellion and madmen who captured his mistress. Lady Cassia is being held in the upper chambers against her will, but a malfunctioning elevator prevents anyone from entering.

回到费雷克处 / Return to Felek

  • 中文：行商浪人重新制作了控制手杖，恢复了基地的电力供应。是时候前往星站守护者处，前往导航者女士所在的房间了。
  • English: The Rogue Trader has recreated the control rod and restored power to the elevator. It is time to return to the Keeper of the Station and ascend to the lady's chambers.

把那个怪物带到费雷克身边 / Bring the monster to Felek

  • 中文：事实证明，卡西娅女士并非陷入困境的无辜少女，而是多年来一直恐吓、威胁奥赛罗家族的恶毒怪物。她的能力可以让家族的导航者发疯，只要一个眼神就能夺走他们的生命。费雷克恳求行商浪人答应他最后一个请求：前往上层大厅，用发光的护身符削弱那个怪物的力量，把她带回到费雷克身边，让她接受审判。
  • English: It turns out that Lady Cassia is no damsel in distress but a vicious monster who has been terrorising House Orsellio for years. She is capable of driving a Navigator insane and killing them with a single glance. The Keeper has asked the Rogue Trader to grant him one last request: to ascend to the upper chambers, weaken the beast with a glowing charm, and bring her back to Felek for judgement.

拯救导航者女士 / Save the lady

  • 中文：叛乱分子得到了神秘的武器。这种武器蕴含导航者的力量，如果星站的守护者打算与叛军正面冲突，恐怕会被叛军的武器立刻杀害。费费雷克恳求行商浪人答应他最后一个请求：前往女主人的房间，将护身符带给她，并将那群叛徒绳之以法。在这之后，行商浪人需要把卡西娅女士带到下层房间的守卫身边。
  • English: The rebels have acquired mysterious weapons imbued with the powers of Navigators. The Keeper of the Station will immediately perish if he tries to fight them. Felek has asked the Rogue Trader to grant him one last request: to ascend to the mistress's chambers, give her the charm, and bring the traitors to justice. After that, Lady Cassia must be returned to her people on the lower level.

重新启动电梯 / Restart the elevator

  • 中文：整个星站依然存活的导航者都处于上层大厅区域。行商浪人需要恢复电梯的电力，使用控制手杖前往上层大厅。
  • English: The only surviving Navigators on the station can be found in the upper chambers. It is necessary to restore the power to the lift and use a control rod to reach them.

收集有关导航者的信息 / Find information on the Navigators

  • 中文：这座星站的守护者，导航者费雷克·奥赛罗已经死了，但行商浪人依然还有希望。欧拉克五号星站还有其他存活的导航者，或许他们愿意加入行商浪人的队伍。
  • English: Felek Orsellio, Navigator and Keeper of the Station, is dead. Hope remains that there are some other warp guides left on Eurac V who are ready to join the Rogue Trader.

杀死费雷克 / Kill Felek

  • 中文：导航者费雷克·奥赛罗，这座星站的守护者之一，原来是个叛徒。他正是欧拉克五号星站这场暴乱的根源，是时候让这个叛徒为他的谎言与恶行付出代价了。
  • English: Felek Orsellio, Navigator and one of the Keepers of the Station, turned out to be a traitor and the source of all the trouble on Eurac V. It is time to punish the traitor for his lies and wickedness.

离开星站 / Leave the station

  • 中文：卡西娅女士目前是整座星站上唯一幸存的导航者。行商浪人必须立刻把她带回虚空舰，提供必要的医疗援助，为亚空间跳跃仪式做好准备。
  • English: Lady Cassia is the only surviving Navigator on the station. She must be immediately brought aboard the Rogue Trader's voidship, provided with the necessary medical aid, and prepared for the communion ritual.
  • 地点：冯·瓦兰修斯虚空舰，舰桥 / Bridge, von Valancius Voidship

与星站的女主人交谈 / Talk to the mistress of the station

  • 中文：费雷克·奥赛罗死在了行商浪人手上。现在，行商浪人必须前往上层大厅，与卡西娅女士交谈，弄清费雷克所说的一切是否属实。
  • English: Felek Orsellio has died at the hand of the Rogue Trader. Only speaking with Lady Cassia in the upper chambers will show if he was telling the truth about his mistress.

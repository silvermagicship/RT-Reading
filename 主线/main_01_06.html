第一章｜凯旋巡礼 / The Triumph

归档说明

  • 小莱卡德阶段性胜利后的庆典与灾变转折，承接第一章前半的地点任务并导向行星陷落。

任务上下文

飞往上层街道 / Fly to the Upperway

  • 欢庆的人群聚集在从指挥中心通往星港的大道上，准备庆祝领主舰长的胜利。
  • The street leading from the command centre to the starport is ready to celebrate the Lord Captain's triumph.
  • 地点：上层街道 / The Upperway

与祭仪大师交谈 / Talk to the master of ceremonies

  • 总督的代表将会详细介绍典礼的情况。
  • The governor's representative will describe the ceremony in detail.

登上准备好的庆典车辆 / Board the prepared vehicle

  • 登上祭仪大师为领主舰长准备好的庆典车辆，正式拉开庆典的序幕。
  • The Lord Captain's grand entrance on the vehicle prepared for {mf|him|her} by the master of ceremonies will signify the start of the celebration.

在庆祝胜利之前，先处理尚未完成的事情 / Deal with unfinished business before the triumph

  • 冯·瓦兰修斯家族还没准备好离开这个星系。当一切安排妥当之后，小莱卡德的总督将会与行商浪人见面，开始举行庆祝仪式。
  • The von Valancius flagship is not yet prepared to depart from the system. Once all matters have been taken care of, the governor of Rykad Minoris will see the Rogue Trader to begin the ceremony.

胜利果实 / Taste of Victory

  • 小莱卡德的总督希望能举办传统的庆典仪式“凯旋巡礼”，以此庆祝领主舰长的光辉胜利。
  • The world of Rykad Minoris and its governor want to honour the Lord Captain with an ancient ceremony — the Triumph.

场景

“尊贵的冯·瓦兰修斯大人，我是小莱卡德的祭仪大师。您的凯旋巡礼已经准备好了，请允许我简短地总结一下接下来的流程。”
"Your {mf|Lord|Lady}ship, peerless {mf|Lord|Lady} von Valancius! I am the master of ceremonies of Rykad Minoris. Everything is in place for your Triumph. Allow me to give you a brief summary of the upcoming event."

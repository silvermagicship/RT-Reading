音阵操作员截获了来自该行星的传输信号，内容如下：“怀着对元祖神皇的信仰，我们向所有能听到这条广播的人传达信息。这里是杰西文，忠贞的朝圣者们的居所，殖民地名称为：AIII-649号小型殖民地。根据元祖神皇的命令，我们滋养教会邀请所有需要帮助或处于困境中的民众与我们一起避难。无论你们是虔诚之人还是异端分子，这都不重要。在杰西文，我们会一视同仁，对所有人伸出援手。”
The vox operator has intercepted a transmission coming from the planet. The message runs, "With faith in our Emperor-Progenitor, we are broadcasting this to any who may hear. This is Jesevon, an abode of true-believing pilgrims, colonial designation AIII-649-Minoris. As commanded by our Emperor-Progenitor, we, the Church Nourishing, invite any who need help or are in distress to take shelter with us. It matters not whether you are pious people or apostates. Here in Jesevon, we help everyone."

这是条预先录制好的信息。每当这颗行星轴旋转了十六分之一时，它就会在它的音阵频率范围内自动播放。信息的源头为方舟舰“荒野旅人号”，方舟舰早已降落在了行星表面。
The pre-recorded message is replayed in the vox-frequency range every one-sixteenth of the planet's axial rotation. The source of the transmission is an ark ship, the Wasteland Wayfarer, which landed on the planet's surface.

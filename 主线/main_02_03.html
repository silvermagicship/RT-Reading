场景

一位身着华美丝袍的贵族女性带着期待的神情看着你，但随后便优雅地向你低头致意。她的手臂与脖子上凸起着镶有宝石的植入物。
A lavishly dressed noblewoman in an opulent silk gown watches you with an expectant air, but then gracefully bows her head. Gem-encrusted implants protrude from her arms and neck.

“谨代表高贵的维亚特·阿布·阿拉姆家族欢迎您，我叫维丝坦萨·加努斯·维亚特·阿布·阿拉姆·阿夫·科罗努斯。根据帝皇的恩典，以及行商浪人西奥多拉·冯·瓦兰修斯的意愿，我目前担任这个世界的行星总督，以及帝国军队的总指挥官。维丝坦萨·维亚特微微抬起了头。
"I welcome you on behalf of the noble House Vyatt ab Aram. My name is Vistenza Janus Vyatt ab Aram af Koronus. By the grace of the Emperor and the will of Rogue Trader Theodora von Valancius, I serve as planetary governor and Imperial commander-in-chief of this world." Vistenza Vyatt raises her head slightly.

维丝坦萨·维亚特将目光转向了阿贝拉德。“以及，向您致意，尊贵的韦尔森。自从您上次造访加努斯到现在，已经过了好几年的时间。我很高兴再次见到您。”
Vistenza Vyatt turns to Abelard. "And greetings to you, noble Werserian. It has been years since your last visit to Janus. I am glad to see you again."

“行商浪人的总领很久未曾造访某颗行星，这是个好兆头。您不这么认为吗，维亚特女士？这意味着总督把所有的事情都打理得井井有条，总领无需插手。”
"It is a good sign when a Rogue Trader's Seneschal does not visit a subject planet for a long time. Isn't that correct, Lady Vyatt? It means the governor is taking care of all matters and there is no need for the Seneschal to interfere."

维丝坦萨发出了歌唱般的笑声。“我从您的话中听到了赞美之意，感谢您的赞美。”她的目光再次转向你。
Vistenza lets out a musical laugh. "I hear praise in those words and I thank you for them." She turns once again to you.

“说实话，由您来代替可敬的冯·瓦兰修斯女士来此，这确实很令人意外。”
"In truth, your arrival in the place of the esteemed Lady von Valancius is quite unexpected."

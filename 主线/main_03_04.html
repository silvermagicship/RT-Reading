场景

“啊，大人。”阿吉里亚斯的声音充满了深思的意味，与平时截然不同。“可以占用您一点点宝贵的时间吗？某些重要的情报引起了我的注意，不过……我们还是先去您的书房吧，以免我们的谈话被外人听到。”
"Ah, Your {mf|Lordship|Ladyship}." Achilleas appears uncharacteristically pensive. "Might I ask for a few precious moments of your time? Critical information has come to my attention, however... Please let us first proceed to your study lest we be overheard."

“如果可以的话，请允许我开门见山地说明。在达戈努斯的那场袭击过后，我进行了详细调查，终于弄清楚那些异形是如何发动了那场毁灭性的袭击。调查结果显示，人类之敌得到了一群秘密阴谋分子的协助。我的特工花了很长时间追踪、审问达戈努斯与王朝其他殖民地的通敌分子，结果……老实说，让我相当吃惊。”阿吉里亚斯顿了顿，将呼吸器压在烧坏的嘴唇上。
"If I may, I will get right to the point. After the attack on Dargonus, I focused my efforts on trying to discover how the xenos were able to launch that devastating raid. According to my findings, the enemies of Humanity received assistance from a hidden cell of conspirators. My agents spent a great deal of time tracking and interrogating infiltrators on Dargonus and other von Valancius colonies. The results... surprised me, to say the least." Achilleas pauses to press his respirator against his burnt lips.

“那个人就是曾经的低语之主，昆拉德·维特威尔。现在我们终于弄清楚他是怎么一次次避开我们的眼线了。那个卑劣的叛徒得到了人类之敌的技术！我的特工已经确定了维特威尔和那些异形秘密会面的位置。那座星站位于百梦岔口，据说前不久出了故障而遭到了遗弃。这对我们很有利，因为那里是个绝佳的伏击地点，我们可以从他们最意想不到的位置发起突袭。”
"Your former Master of Whispers, Kunrad Voigtvir. Now we know how he's been able to dodge our spies time and time and again: he has the technology of enemies of Humanity on his side. Vile traitor! My agents ascertained the location of the base where Voigtvir has been meeting with xenos in secret, a station in the system of the Hundred Dreams Crossroads. The station is allegedly out of commission, abandoned some time ago. That plays into our hands: it is an excellent place to set up an ambush and strike where they least expect it."

场景

你一早起来，便花了好几个小时聆听高阶顾问的冗长报告，关于各种各样的贸易、交流、安排、争端，以及没完没了的官僚程序。阳光射进了紧闭的窗户，达戈努斯日常生活的喧嚣传入了你的耳中。你最近从早到晚一直都在忙于王朝事务，甚至都没有时间享受生活。
You have spent the first few hours of your morning listening to the High Factotum's lengthy report on the latest trade exchanges, arrangements, disputes, and countless bureaucratic procedures. From outside the illuminated, tightly closed windows, you can hear the muffled sounds of Dargonus and its everyday life — the life that has been passing you by lately, as you have been preoccupied with the affairs of your protectorate from dawn till dusk.

克莱门缇雅努力忍住了呵欠。当詹里斯·丹罗克的报告完成之后，她拿出了数据板。“感谢您的报告，高阶顾问，您的工作得到了在座各位与帝国内政部的高度赞赏。我们这边的事情几乎已经处理完了，现在只剩下今天最后几项议程，我想亲自向您汇报。”
Clementia barely contains a yawn. Once Janris Danrok has finished, she pulls out her data-slate. "Thank you for your report, High Factotum. Your work is highly appreciated, both by everyone here and the Adeptus Administratum. Your {mf|Lordship|Ladyship}, we are almost done here. We just need to go over the final items on today's agenda that I would like to report to you personally."

LocustDestroyer

场景

金属悠长的呻吟声构成了痛苦而忧伤的旋律，渗着毒辣的怨恨。“我又出现了！我看到了不和谐的景象！何等令人憎恶！伟大的征服者被转瞬即逝的虫豸摧垮！到处都是腐化！空洞的内壁熊熊燃烧！”这刺耳的轰鸣声听起来不像是智能生物说出的语言，更像是某种金属刑具发出来的声音。
The drawn-out metallic groans form a painful and melancholy melody, but one that oozes with venomous spite. "I exist again and I behold discord! An abominable sight! Great conquerors brought low by a fleeting pestilence! Corrosion everywhere. The hollow insides are ablaze!" The grating roar is more like the sound of a metal torture instrument than a sentient being's speech.

“整个宇宙中的侏儒必将被消灭！我会击溃他们，将他们碾成血尘！我会消灭他们，也会消灭我的悲伤！那个遭到诅咒的人必将后悔曾经存在过的事实！”异形浑身颤抖起来，从头部的装甲板到重力垫的尾翼都在不断震动，就连它脚下的岩石都被震裂了，陷了下去。
"The runts of the universe shall be eliminated! I rout them and I pulverise them into bloodied dust! I rid myself of them and of my sorrow! The cursed one will regret ever existing!" The xenos starts to shake, from the armoured plates on its head down to the tail flaps of its grav-pad. The tremor causes the rock below it to crack and sink in.

这只异形的整个躯体都由某种不知名的金属制成，金属上交错着绿色的能量通道，散发着电流的光芒。异形的腰部之下，重力板周围的炮口发出了嗡嗡的响声，而所有上肢都融合成了一门威力巨大的重炮。它的身体无论哪个部位，都没有任何弱点。毫无疑问，这台机器通过精湛的技艺设计制造而成，专门用来消灭所有生命。想要摧毁这样的机器，恐怕不是件简单的事情。
The xenos's entire skeletal body is made of an unknown metal interlaced with greenish energy channels aglow with currents. Below its waist, the flares of a grav-pad are humming and rattling. Its upper limbs fuse into a formidable heavy cannon. Not a single part of the monster's body betrays vulnerability. Without a doubt, this is a technologically superb machine designed to annihilate all life. Disabling it will not be easy.

振动的金属板发出的声音，既带着进行曲般的恶意节奏，又带着空洞的轰鸣声。这台不朽的机械装置发出的哀鸣，预示着它注定要经历漫长的愤怒，无尽的战斗，以及即将到来的灭亡。巨炮齐射时发出的轰鸣，宛如地狱交响乐的和弦，让整个空间都为之颤抖。
The sound of the vibrating metal plates has both the rhythmic malice of a march and the hollow hum of misery — the lament of an immortal mechanism doomed to aeons of wrath and battle, or else an impending demise. The salvo from the monstrous cannon is an infernal orchestral chord that makes the very fabric of space tremble.

“报上名来，异形。”
"State your name, xenos."

音阵翻译器发出了金属的声音，这个太空死灵战士发出了愤怒的闪光，笨重的身体轰隆作响。“这是对智能语言的亵渎！我要将亵渎者彻底抹除！”
The vox-translator spits out several metallic sounds, and a new flash of anger makes the Necron's bulky body rumble. "Desecration of intelligent speech! I will sooner see it obliterated!"

太空死灵的声音听起来既像是大笑，又像是啜泣。“锈渍无权目睹高贵的金属如何毁灭！毁灭者终将遭到毁灭。被诅咒者正在散播疾病。在这场伟大、光荣的战斗中，渺小无用之人注定灭亡！”
The Necron's screeching distantly resembles laughter, or perhaps sobs. "Rust does not have the privilege of witnessing noble metal perish! The destroyer shall be destroyed. The accursed one is sowing disease. The great and mighty do battle — the petty and worthless shall fall!"

太空死灵骷髅般的头颅抽搐了几下，刹那间，下颚之间出现了一个圆形的开口，仿佛惊讶地张开了嘴一样。随后，圆形开口中发出了一阵恐怖的震颤声，震耳欲聋的轰鸣声与随之而来的尖锐痛苦仿佛要将你刺穿一样。鲜血从你的耳朵里渗了出来。
The skull-like head jerks, and for a few moments, a circular opening appears between the jaws, resembling a mouth open in astonishment. The wound closes with a terrifying, tremulous ringing. The deafening roar that follows sharply pierces you with pain. Blood starts seeping out of your ears.

“你是来自远古的梦魇，来自早已被遗忘的过去……”伊莉耶特的呼吸变得有些惊慌。她瞪大了眼睛，看着你对面的生物。“我曾遇到过像你这样的生物，遗落者。遇到过一次，也打败过一次。而这次，我要做的事情也一样：我要把你送回你的坟墓深处。”
"You are an ancient nightmare from a long-forgotten past..." Yrliet's breathing becomes unsteady as she stares wide-eyed at the being opposite you. "I have encountered others like you, Oghyr. Once before. And I will do to you as I did to them: send you to the depths of your tomb."

这只异形发出了金属的咔哒声，仿佛在笑。“在那场被遗忘的战斗，遭到毁坏的远古武器……没有任何价值！”
The xenos lets out a metallic click, a semblance of laughter. "An ancient weapon that was broken in battles forgotten... worthless!"

“我承认他是位不错的领袖，也是位真正的战士。没错，虽然它是个该死的异形，但他也是个战士。我敢打赌，我肯定能亲手干掉这个值得尊重的战斗疯子。”乌尔法欣喜地环顾四周，仿佛在期待有人与他对赌。
"I recognise a leader and a true warrior in this one. Yes, still a xenos unworthy of life, but a warrior all the same. I bet this noble madman of battle will fall by my hand!" Ulfar casts a joyous look over the party, as if expecting someone to take him up on the wager.

玛拉斋咧嘴一笑。“不过是竞技场的奴隶罢了，还真能夸海口。我接受这个挑战，只希望你输的时候别太往心里去。”
Marazhai grins. "Bold words for an arena slave. I accept the challenge. Just do not take your defeat too close to heart."

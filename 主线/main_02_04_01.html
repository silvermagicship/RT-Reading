"But... After seventy years of service — and not a single directive! Not from Holy Terra or from Dargonus! A complete breakdown in communication! Jae nervously licks her lips — she clearly did not expect this response to her remark. "Atilius, I do not think the Exalted One has any particular desire to see you lobotomised and turned into a mindless clanking ghoul just because someone somewhere changed a law..."

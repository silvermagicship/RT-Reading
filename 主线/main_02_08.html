场景

一个穿着军官制服的男人跪在地上，在一片狼藉中摸索。当你走近时，你听到他在狂热地喃喃自语。“不，不，不……他在哪里？我的儿子在哪里？”军官的下半张脸上沾满了某种奇怪的东西，那些苍白而凹凸不平的东西显然并不是鲜血。军官并没有注意到你，他的双眼正直勾勾盯着地面。
A man in an officer's uniform is kneeling and scrabbling around in the mess of blood and dirt on the floor. As soon as you draw nearer, you hear his feverish muttering. "No, no, no... Where is he? Where is my son?" The lower half of the officer's face is grimy with some strange substance too pale and lumpy to be blood. The officer pays you no attention — his eyes are fixed on the floor.

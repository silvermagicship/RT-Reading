Footfall

场景：Chaplain

场景

你面前这位牧师看起来非常威严。他身材高大，肌肉发达，脸上和手臂上到处都是伤疤。他整个人都像是无声地诉说着他的过去——无数异端分子都死在了这位真正虔诚之人的手中。
The priest in front of you looks imposing. He is tall and muscular, and has scars on his face and arms. His whole appearance seems to tell a story of many heretics who met their end at the hands of this adherent of the true faith.

当他看到你的时候，他朝你鞠了一躬，但动作有些生硬，似乎还并不习惯于这种恭敬的动作。“愿德鲁苏斯保佑你，大人。我从来没想过，居然会在这种地方见到行商浪人，尤其是在这么动荡的年头。”
As he sees you, he bows — respectfully but none too gracefully, as if he is not used to making the deferential gesture. "May Drusus watch over you, Your {mf|Lord|Lady}ship. I never thought I'd see a Rogue Trader in this kind of establishment, especially in these troubled times."

“我干过很多肮脏的事情，也见过很多令人不安的事情。可即便如此，我还是成为了一名虔诚的信徒，说来真是挺难以置信的。我的导师没能劝我走上正路，可那些残酷的战斗，还有我的战友每晚痛苦的忏悔，让我踏上了这样的道路。如果我不帮他们，他们还能向谁忏悔呢？再怎么说，我也知道祈祷词怎么说。于是我就成了那艘船上的牧师，再后来，我彻底放弃了我的犯罪道路。身为海盗的我死了，身为传教士的我重生了。这就是圣德鲁苏斯为我指明的道路。他自己也曾死过一次，后来被帝皇复活了。”
"I have done many unclean deeds and seen many unsettling things. And I have become a sincere believer — imagine that. Deadly battles and my comrades' nightly confessions of their atrocities did for me what my mentors couldn't. Who else could they confess to? I knew the words of the prayer, if nothing else. That was how I became the ship's chaplain. Then I renounced my criminal ways altogether. I died as a pirate and was reborn as a missionary: thus commanded Saint Drusus, who himself passed away and was resurrected by the Emperor."

Shrugging, Ulfar mutters quietly, "I know piracy is condemned in the Imperium... but on Fenris, participating in a glorious raid on a neighbour is considered a valiant deed, not a shameful one."

"The laws of death worlds can be surprisingly unique at times. But for the home of the Space Wolves, the Emperor Himself commanded that special customs be established... ones that may be beyond the understanding of ordinary people."

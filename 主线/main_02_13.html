场景

一扇令人不寒而栗的大门挡住了前往下个区域的道路。这扇门上布满了划痕，仿佛有什么东西在冰冷的金属上抓了很久一样。你碰了碰这扇门，但它对你的触碰没有任何反应。它矗立在原地，拒绝打开。门上的字写着“主计算大教堂”。
A formidable-looking door blocks the way into the next module. It is covered in scratches, as if someone clawed at the cold metal for a very, very long time. The door does not respond to your touch and stands fast, refusing to open. The writing above the doorway reads, "Main Computing Cathedral."

门边墙板的情况一下子就吸引了你的注意力。墙板上有许多条划痕，五条划痕连在一起，像是爪子留下来的痕迹。部分墙板看起来仿佛随时都会掉下来。墙板后面到处都能看到损坏的电线，偶尔还能看到电火花，听到噼里啪啦的声音。
The main thing that catches your eye about this place is the state of the wall panels by the door. They bear numerous scratches, five in a row, resembling claw marks. Some of the panels look like they could fall off at any moment; here and there you see the occasional sparks and crackling of the damaged wires behind them.

突然，操纵者的声音降了好几个八度，发出不祥的低沉嗡鸣声。“欧姆尼赛亚，唯一真理之主，愿您的源力粉碎邪恶，将其根源彻底拔除……”伊奥贡那重度改造的核心中内置的线圈，伴随着祷词的节奏发出低沉的嗡鸣。
Suddenly, the Manipulus's voice drops several octaves,, vibrating with an ominous thrum. "Omnissiah, sole in truths, may Thy Motive Force crush evil, and may it root out the source thereof..." Coils built into Eogunn's heavily augmented core drone in time with the prayer.

场景

房间里的空气弥漫着机械与生物各种混合在一起的味道。血液、铁锈、呕吐物、机油、排泄物、煤油……所有的气味混合在一起，散发着浓烈的臭味，你仿佛都能触碰得到这要命的味道。
The air in the room is permeated by a wild mixture of miasmas of both mechanical and biological origin. Blood, rust, vomit, machine oil, excrement, kerosene — all these smells blend together into such a thick stench that it almost feels palpable.

空气中混杂着各种各样疯狂的味道。你面前的这台设备仿佛是由蠕动的电线与各种混搭的碎片拼接而成的缝合怪。在这一片杂乱之中，你险些没有注意到这台机器的主要“部件”。那是个浑身上下都植入了各种装置的人，上半身被镶嵌在机器的框架中，几十根电缆刺穿了他的肉体。
Just as the air here is one mad tangle of odours, the device in front of you is a mishmash of wriggling wires and transplanted fragments. In all the clutter, one could almost miss the main "component" — a heavily augmented man, whose upper body is embedded into the frame of the machine, dozens of wires piercing his flesh.

伊莉耶特敏锐的双眼率先在杂乱的电线中看到了人类的身躯。她僵在原地，满心震惊与厌恶。
Yrliet's keen eyes are the first to make out the human being amidst the jumble of wires, and she stiffens in shock and disgust.

这个男人的身体上覆满了凝固的血液与结痂的体液，但他的双眼是睁开的，目光非常锐利。他把目光转向你，眼神中没有任何惊讶或敬畏。“你是谁？竟敢在我们的圣所打扰神圣殉道者的安宁？你想干什么？”
A crust of congealed blood and other bodily fluids covers the man's entire body, but his eyes are open and his gaze is keen. This gaze is turned to you, devoid of surprise or deference. "Who are you, disturbing the peace of holy martyrs in the home of our grief? What do you want?"

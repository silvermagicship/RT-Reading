场景

这个浑身脏污、衣衫不整的女人并没有理会你。她低下头，饶有兴趣地看着地面，盯着那些并不存在，或是你的眼睛看不到的东西。
The dirty, dishevelled woman pays you no heed, lowering her head and looking at the ground with genuine interest, at something that either does not exist or is invisible to your eyes.

哪怕是在这个距离，你也闻到了明显的臭氧味。这新鲜的气味，与充满煤烟与工业润滑油的闷热房间格格不入。那个女人举起了一只残缺不全的手，心不在焉地用剩下的两根手指拨弄着她蓬乱的头发。
Even at this distance you can detect the distinct smell of ozone — too fresh for this stuffy room reeking of soot and industrial lubricant. The woman raises a maimed hand and absent-mindedly runs its two remaining fingers through her matted hair.

The woman jerks her head and transfixes you with a delirious gaze. Only now do you notice a reddish black mark on her cheek — a many-pointed star, a symbol of Chaos. The prisoner bares her teeth, but you cannot tell whether she does so as a threat or simply out of fear.

场景

沉思者的屏幕闪烁着，仿佛系统电源发生了故障。你打算伸手触碰控制面板，但随后就立刻把手缩了回去——这台设备上涂着某种黏性物质。
The cogitator screen flickers as if the system is suffering a power failure. You reach out and touch the control panel but quickly jerk your hand back: the device is coated in some sticky substance.

The cogitator's case is covered with scratched symbols and signs thickly smeared in a pungent oily mass. You spot tiny bubbles emerging from the gleaming metal: it is almost as if the malodorous slime is seeping from these scratches like sweat oozes from pores on human skin.

The eye-like sparks are yellowish orange in colour, but you are pretty sure this monitor cannot display such a vivid hue. You lean forward, and you hear a predatory growl coming from inside the device, a sound full of hatred and hunger.

You try pressing keys and spinning a couple of reels. In response, a few lumens light up on the control panel, a low guttural growl reverberates through the machine, and a small auxiliary screen lights up, presenting you with information.

你不仅没能顺利查看日志的内容，就连访问日志的过程本身就是一种折磨。屏幕不断闪烁，所有投射到屏幕上的数据仿佛熔化胶片上的图像一样，变得越来越模糊，最后消失不见。
Not only do you fail to examine the log, even accessing it is quite an ordeal — any data projected to the blinking screen blurs and fades like images on melting film.

无论是按下按键，还是转动手柄，沉思者都没有任何反应。屏幕上依然显示着你第一次接近这台机器时看到的信息。
The cogitator does not react to the buttons being pushed and the handles being turned: the screen continues to display what you saw when you first approached the machine.

你浏览着辅助屏幕上的报告。看来，这台沉思者中的数据已经被完全清除了，如果说它还能有什么有价值的信息的话，就只能去外部驱动器中找找看了。
You skim the report on the auxiliary screen. It seems the cogitator has been completely purged of data. If there is anything of value left in this machine, it will be on the external drives.

沉思者上确实连着几块数据密存器。其中两个密存器似乎已经被彻底融化，无法修复。不过，如果你可以成功关闭这台机器本身的逻辑网络，或许你可以在不损坏密存数据的情况下，取走第三块数据密存器。
The data-crypt slots are occupied: two of them appear to be melted beyond repair, but you might extract the third one without incurring data loss if you successfully deactivate the logical network of the machine itself.

沉思者上确实连接着数据密存器，但它的插槽被两排金属爪子保护着。这些爪子直接从沉思者的外壳中伸出来。在你的凝视之下，它们蜷曲起来，将数据密存器抓得更紧了。
The data-crypt is in place, but its slot is protected by two rows of metal claws growing straight out of the cogitator's casing. They flex under your scrutiny, clutching the device even tighter.

你检查了沉思者外壳上所有的插槽与插座，但什么都没找到，只找到了一根隐藏在保护盖板后面的数据棒。
You examine all the slots and sockets on the cogitator case, but to no avail. All that you manage to find is a single data-stick hidden behind a protective panel.

Heinrix gives the screen and the control panel a thorough examination. "Another manifestation of the same corruption we encountered on Kiava Gamma. Perhaps if we used an auxiliary connection system..."

海因里希用刀子撬开了机器的侧板，火花与腐蚀性的液体喷了他一脸。他一边咒骂着，一边擦拭着被烧伤的脸，随后扯出一团电缆，切断了右边的电缆。随后，沉思者便关闭了。原本紧紧钳制着数据密存器的金属爪，也无可奈何地松开了手。
Heinrix pries a side panel open with a knife and receives a face full of sparks and caustic reagent. After swearing and wiping his singed face, the Interrogator manages to pull out a bunch of wires and cut the right cables, causing the cogitator to shut down. The claws holding the data-crypt unclench helplessly.

“我需要欧姆尼塞亚的仆人协助我。”
"I need the assistance of a servant of the Omnissiah."

技术神甫用次声波念起了咒语，让你感觉有点恶心。他走近沉思者，将双手放在控制面板上，以流畅的动作进行着神圣的操作。他的绝大部分操作你完全看不懂是怎么回事。你看着他拨动开关，虔诚地敲击几个继电器，同时还在其他地方画着保护性的符号。
The Tech-Priest starts chanting an infrasound litany that makes you feel slightly nauseous and approaches the cogitator. He places his hands on the control panel in one smooth motion and starts performing sacred manipulations. Most of the Magos's actions are a total mystery to you, and you watch as he flips toggle switches and ritually taps several relays while drawing protective symbols over others.

仪式完成之后，技术神甫从他红色法衣的褶皱中抽出一根电缆，将其插入沉思者的插座中。一股电流沿着电缆涌了进来，技术神甫向后退了一步，空气中顿时充满了电缆被烧焦的味道。不过，沉思者顺从地关机了。屏幕上邪恶的内容消失了，数据密存器也从端口中掉了下来。
At last, when the rituals are complete, your companion withdraws a cord from the folds of his red vestment and plugs it into one of the sockets. A discharge surges along the cable, making the Tech-Priest flinch and filling the air with the smell of burnt wiring, but the cogitator obediently shuts down, erasing the sinister entity from the screen and releasing the data-crypt from its port.

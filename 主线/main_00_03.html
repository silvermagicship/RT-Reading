"That is simply irrational, Lady Theodora," says the man dressed in peculiar garb, his voice tight with tension. His face, half of which is covered by synskin, is twisted in a grimace of displeasure and disbelief. 一位身材高大的灰发女性凝视着他，电子眼闪烁着深红色的光芒。“那就提出一个让我满意的替代方案吧。情况我已经都说清楚了，你的方案不能逊色于我的总领提出的方案。做个决定吧，你会如何处理艾恩六号星的情况，艾德萨德？”
The tall, grey-haired woman pins him with her gaze, her cybernetic eye flashing crimson. "Then suggest an alternative that will satisfy me. I have described the situation — no worse than my Seneschal could have done. Make a decision: what will you do with Aion VI, Edelthrad?"

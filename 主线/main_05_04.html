NecronObelisk

场景

几具身穿审判庭制服的尸体已经面目全非，被丢在沉思者与方尖碑般的巨型雕像之间，惊惧可怖。杀害这些士兵的凶手没有留下任何痕迹，但你始终感觉到某种压抑的感觉，仿佛墙壁本身也在注视着你。空气中回荡着电流的声音，弥漫着腐肉的恶臭，让这种感觉变得更加糟糕。
Several disfigured corpses in the uniforms of Inquisition soldiers have been tossed in a grotesque pile between the cogitator and the large obelisk-like statue. Their killers left no trace, but you have the nagging, oppressive feeling that the very walls are watching you. The air, thick with electricity and the heavy stench of rotting flesh, only makes that feeling worse.

乌尔法闭上眼睛，用力地嗅着，随后发出了冷冷的低吼。“杀害这些凡人的东西还在附近。我能嗅到……某种毫无生机的气味。不是尸体的气味，更像是莫凯嘴里的钢牙。”
Closing his eyes and sniffing noisily, the Wolf snarls grimly, "What killed these mortals is still near. I can smell it... A lifeless stench. Not like a corpse, but like the iron teeth in Morkai's maw."

“当心，行商浪人。那些遗落者很擅长设置陷阱。”
"Careful, Rogue Trader. The Oghyr are adept at springing traps."

沉思者视讯屏幕上的信息正在不耐烦地闪烁着。“进程：语言分析 [编目： 54782136]。状态： 等待数据编目。启动信息面板。”
*A message is blinking impatiently on the cogitator's vid-screen: "PROCESS: Linguistic analysis [Alg.54782136]
STATUS: Awaiting data cataloguing. Activate information panels."*

源力流过了沉思者周围粗大的电缆。“数据已接受。[编目：84521-4521]语言分析完成。是否开始最终编目？”
An impulse of the Motive Force runs through the thick cables around the cogitator. "Data accepted. [Alg.84521-4521] Linguistic analysis complete. Initiate final cataloguing?"

机器不断处理着信息，沉思者内伺服电机的嗡嗡声越来越响。“警告，数据不足，无法开始编目。 [编目： 54782136 -125]。状态： 等待数据输入。”
*The hum of the servo-motors inside the cogitator grows louder as the machine processes information. "ATTENTION. Insufficient data for final cataloguing. [Alg.54782136-125]
STATUS: Awaiting data input."*

[开始最终编目]
[Initiate final cataloguing]

视讯屏幕上出现了无数符号，这些符号你在周围的建筑上也见过。不知为何，这些符号取代了你原本熟悉的文字和数字，光是看着就让你感到莫名地反胃。几乎一模一样的几何图形组合在一起，形成了长长的文字链。它们快速闪过，让你目不暇接。
The vid-screen fills with the same symbols you saw on the surrounding buildings. You feel an inexplicable rush of disgust at the sight of them in place of familiar words and numbers. Almost identical geometric shapes combine into long chains and flash by too fast for you to take them in.

随后，石刻下方出现低哥特语写成的文字。“避免肉体的瑕疵、短暂与限制，我们已经在群星之中踏上了新的轨迹，成为了不朽的存在。我们成为了新世之物，通过节奏、图像与符号，与旧世之物保持着联结。在一起存在转变为黑暗之前，它将被征服，在恐惧中颤抖。”
A text in Low Gothic appears under the glyphs: "Shunning the imperfections of the flesh, its transience, and its finiteness, we have embarked on a new trajectory amid the stars, and have become immortal. We have become the New, preserving a bond with the Old through rhythm, images, and symbols. Before existence turns to darkness, it shall be conquered and it shall tremble in fear."

“报告，[参考错误]，[编目：7458-14] 编目工作顺利完成，异形主动词汇已编译完毕。[编目：7458-41] 注意，若要保存此编目，需要将数据传输至翻译设备，如果拒绝此要求，则会导致编目内容删除。是否继续？”
"REPORT. [Ref. err.] [Alg. 7458-14] Cataloguing completed successfully. Active vocabulary of the xenos race compiled. [Alg. 7458-41] ATTENTION. Data transfer to the translation device is required to save the catalogue. Declining will result in catalogue deletion. Continue?"

选项 Choices

  • 选择 Choice

[将数据传输到你的阐释器上]
[Transfer the data to your Elucidator]

你将阐释器连接到这台机器上，你的阐释器发出了强烈的嗡嗡声。当它理解了这些陌生文字的含义之后，它的内部也同样发出了金属的呻吟声。随后，阐释器的视讯屏幕亮了起来。“操作完成。”
Now connected to the cogitator, your Elucidator hums intensely. As it learns the meaning of the hitherto unfamiliar glyphs, it responds with rattles and metallic groans. The cogitator's vid-screen lights up: "Operation completed."

[删除所有数据]
[Delete all the data]

所有的异形符号都被滚动的二进制代码取代了，视讯屏幕显示着这样的信息：“语言分析中止。[编目：13697512]”
A scrolling binharic code replaces the xenos symbols. The vid-screen displays the following message: "Linguistic analysis aborted. [Alg.13697512]."

“现在我明白，为什么他们需要如此古老、如此强大的机魂了。破译异形的语言，实际上就等同于理解异形的语言。这台远古科技造物即将转化为邪恶的智能生命。”帕斯卡的声音中夹杂着惶恐与惊骇。“这台机器决不能留在这里。我正在联系虚空舰，要求派遣一队技工前来，将这台神圣的机器进行拆解、提取。”
"Now I see why they needed such an ancient and powerful machine spirit. To decipher a xenos language is practically to comprehend it. This archeotech is on the verge of turning into an Abominable Intelligence." Trepidation blends with horror in the Tech-Priest's voice. "It cannot be left here. I am sending an order to the ship to deploy a team of technomats to dismantle and extract the sacred machine."

“我主张优先获得对这件圣物进行神圣诊断的权利。通过对异形数据的研究，我可以更快地理解并治愈机魂的病症。”技术神甫们在交换了一连串尖锐而漫长的二进制颤音后，似乎终于达成了一致。
"I claim priority right to perform sacred diagnostics on the relic. My research of the xenos data will expedite understanding and the curing of the machine spirit's ailment." After exchanging a few sharp, prolonged binharic trills, the Tech-Priests seem to reach an agreement.

伊奥贡虔诚地俯下身子，几乎触到地面，双手合拢以示尊重。“这台机器，是我们曾在阿姆桑克特使用的那台阐释器的前身。如果当初我们能够掌握它所理解的全部知识……谁知道呢，也许一切都会变得不同。然而，我的多愁善感向来与统计学结果背道而驰。”
Eogunn reverently descends lower, almost touching the floor, and folds his hands in a gesture of respect. "This machine is the forebear of the Elucidator we employed on Amsanct. Had we possessed the full scope of knowledge that it comprehended, who knows, perhaps everything would have turned out differently. However, my sentimentality correlates poorly with statistical probabilities."

这台机器忠诚不渝地为审判庭服务，根本不肯透露它的秘密。外壳上的红灯一盏接一盏地亮了起来，充满了威胁的意味。
The mechanism, having served the Inquisition so faithfully, is loath to part with its secrets. One after another, menacing scarlet dots light up on its casing.

你无情地闯入了沉思者的内存，得到了如下信息：“已经准备开始进行‘神圣守护计划’，可能会发生‘恐怖幻象’事件。收到命令，要求放弃研究任务，与荒垄碑碣的特遣部队汇合。附带用来穿越太空雷区的密码。”在这之后，你在附件中看到了某颗行星的具体坐标，还有一串二进制代码。
Your ruthless intrusion into the cogitator's memory chambers yields the following message: "Prepare for the start of Operation Precious Sentinel. The Horrific Vision event may come to pass. Orders to abort the research mission and join the contingent on Epitaph. Codes for traversing the minefields are attached." The message has an attachment containing the astronomical coordinates of a planet along with some binharic code.

“最高优先级：次元门充能过程中，与屏障分队失去联系。没有收到次元门被封印的确切消息。保持高度警戒！”
"Priority zero: contact with barrier squadron lost during dimensional gate recharging. No confirmation received of gate being sealed. Stay on high alert!"

沉思者发出了愤怒的嘶吼声，刺眼的红色指示灯亮了起来，发出了高频的蜂鸣声。整个视讯屏幕上出现了这样的字样：“[编目 00-02] 发现入侵者！”当你耳边嗡鸣渐渐平息时，你听到了别的声音……某些人沉重的脚步声。机魂仿佛大仇得报一般，带着胜利的姿态，显示着不断闪烁的预言信息。
The cogitator shrieks indignantly, lighting up with blinding red indicators and letting out a high-frequency howl. A word appears across the entire width of the vid-screen: "[Alg. 00-02] INTRUSION!" When the ringing in your ears subsides, you realise you hear something else... someone's heavy footsteps. The vindictive machine spirit triumphantly displays a blinking prophetic message.

Pasqal bows his head and mutters a prayer, then makes a quick motion with his fingers. Several lights on the cogitator go out as its defences crack. "Forgive me, ancient spirit. You must be pacified."

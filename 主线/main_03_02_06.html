“噢，奎莎莉娜……”阿贝拉德微微一笑，立刻咳嗽起来。他的脸上布满了深深的皱纹，眼睛上附上了一层模糊的白内障。“德莱夫斯塔家族的人又来了……他们又要开会了……又要呼吁各种各样的提案……他们真是永远不知满足，亲爱的……恐怕他们只想让咱们和咱们的孩子们彻底消失……”
"Oh, Quatharina..." Abelard laughs faintly and immediately launches into a bout of coughing. Deep lines run across his face and a blurry film of cataracts clouds his eyes. "The Drivestems are at it again... Another council session... Another appeal to the logitiums... They will never be satisfied, love... until they make us and our children disappear..."

“或许……咱们不然就这么算了吧？……你总是说，达戈努斯没有家的感觉，亲爱的……”阿贝拉德的视线越过了你，看着某个只有他自己才能看到的人。他的嘴唇颤抖着，露出羞涩的微笑。“你觉得怎么样，亲爱的？你想这么一走了之……把一切都抛在脑后……去个遥远的地方……卸下担子？”
"Perhaps... we should just give in... You always say that Dargonus is not your home, love..." Abelard is looking past you at someone only he can see. There is a timid smile on his trembling lips. "Would you like that, love? Would you rather leave... leave it all behind... go somewhere far away... away from our toils?"

I will have no heretics under my command, Seneschal. I order you to conquer this illusion at once! Get to it, officer!"

阿贝拉德退了一步。他眉头紧锁，满是眼泪的双眼中闪现了一道光芒。”‘异端分子’？我建议你在指控军官之前好好复习一下规章制度！《勘误补编》第一篇中已经取消了异形影响的分类，因此……”阿贝拉德的目光变得清晰了些。他看着你，颤抖着吸了口气。“圣人之血在上，舰长大人！您还活着！”
Abelard flinches. His brow furrows and a glow lights up in his watering eyes. "'Heretic'? I recommend that you brush up on your regulations before accusing an officer! The Addendum Primum Errandum abolished the xeno-influence categorisation, and therefore..." His vision somewhat cleared, Abelard looks at you and takes a shuddering breath. "Saints' blood, Lord Captain! You're alive!"

阿贝拉德呻吟了一声，试着努力挺直驼背。“感谢神皇，您还没有落入死神的手中！但话说回来，考虑到目前的情况……这可能是最糟糕的结果。”
With a groan, Abelard tries to straighten his hunched back. "Thank the God-Emperor, death has not found you yet! Then again, given the circumstances... it may be for the worst."

任务上下文

找到舵手长 / Find the Master Helmsman

  • 中文：必须找到舵手长，才能启动亚空间跳跃过程。只有他有能力协调全体船员，引导虚空舰脱离危机四伏的亚空间。
  • English: The Master Helmsman must be found in order to begin the translation from the warp. He is the only person capable of coordinating the crew and guiding the voidship out of this perilous dimension.
  • 地点：虚空舰舰桥 / Voidship Bridge

场景

“雷沃！舵手长！立刻报告系统状态！”
"Ravor! Master Helmsman! Systems status report!"

“帝皇在上，是总领！还活蹦乱跳着呢！”这个男人的皮肤呈现着病态的苍白，他向你举手致敬。“这么说吧，事情挺棘手的。整个地方都沾满了那些臭烘烘的烂东西。把它们从视讯屏幕上清理掉得花多长时间，恐怕只有黄金王座才知道了……”
"By the Emperor, it's the Seneschal! Living and breathing!" The white-haired man with sickly-pale skin salutes you. "Well, there's a tricky question — the whole place is so overgrown with foul wickedry we'll be scrubbing it off the vid-screens for Throne knows how long..."

选项 / Choices

  • 选择 / Choice:

“你们这些人是怎么活下来的？”
"How did you people survive in here?"

这个人扫视着舰桥。“这肯定是命运的安排……最好是帝皇的安排，别是其他什么人的安排。低语之主的那群帮凶闯进来的时候，一群军官朝他们冲了过去，但那简直是一场屠杀……我把所有能找到的人都抓了过来，把他们拖到了沉思者后面，之后祈祷能有人来救我们。”
The man glances around the bridge. "Musta been providence... it better have been the Emperor's, not someone else's. When they barged in here, the ones with the Master of Whispers, some officers charged them, but it wasn't even a fight... I grabbed whoever I could and dragged 'em behind the cogitators, then I prayed for our rescue."

  • 选择 / Choice:

“我们必须重新建立与导航者圣所之间的联系。只有这样，我们才有机会逃出亚空间。”
"We need to re-establish contact with the Navigator's Sanctum. It is our only chance of escaping from the warp."

你的话语似乎将军官们从不知所措的状态中唤醒，你周围传来了谈话声与传达命令的声音。你听到有人抽泣起来，某个幸存者承受不了这巨大的压力，崩溃了。阿贝拉德连正眼都没看一眼，便把那位哭泣的军官铐了起来。
Your words seem to knock the officers out of their stupor, and the air around you fills with voices and commands being issued. You hear someone sobbing — one of the survivors buckling under the stress. Abelard cuffs the crying officer without even looking.

“恢复与导航者圣所之间的音阵通讯通道。让所有船员做好准备，准备跃迁到真实世界！”阿贝拉德停顿了几秒，之后将目光转向了你。“您可以说我是个迷信的人，{name}，但跃迁的仪式最好还是不要随意更改。您是唯一一个可以代表冯·瓦兰修斯家族发言的人。或许您目前并不是领主舰长，但您比任何人都更有资格坐在她的宝座上，宣布跃迁仪式即将开始。”他做了个手势，将你的注意力引到了上方——一座位于高耸楼梯顶点处的王座。
"Restore the vox-channel to the Navigator's Sanctum. Ready the crew. Prepare for translation to realspace!" Abelard pauses for a few seconds before turning to you. "{name}, you may call me superstitious, but translation is not a good time to disregard rules. You are the only one who speaks for House von Valancius. You may not be the Lord Captain, but you, more than anyone else, deserve to take her place in the ritual that is about to commence." He directs your attention upward — to the throne that sits at the apex of the tall staircase.

  • 选择 / Choice:

“我该怎么做？”
"What must I do?"

“坐到船长老大的位置上，命令开始进行准备工作，等待官员提交报告……呸，都去他的吧！跃迁可是一门手艺，不是我这么三言两语就能解释清楚的！赶紧坐到船长的座位上吧，{name}{mf|大师|女士}。这些人已经准备拼死一搏了，他们至少应该看到有个身影坐在该坐的地方，这才是安慰他们的方法。请快点吧！我们浪费的每一秒钟，都相当于在冒着巨大的危险！”
"Take the Lord Cap'n's seat. Order the final preparations. Wait for the officers to report... bah, blast it all to the void! Translation is a craft, not something to be explained in a tick! Just go up to the throne, {mf|Master|Mistress} {name} — these people are about to do the impossible, the least they deserve is the solace of seeing a figure sitting in the usual spot. Please hurry! Every second we waste is a terrible risk!"

  • 选择 / Choice:

“我已经准备好了，举行仪式吧。”
"I am ready. Begin the ritual."

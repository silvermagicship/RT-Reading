这艘受损舰船的舰长以带有导航者特色的动作擦去了额头上的汗水。他并不是用整个手来擦汗，而是用两根手指从左到右，向后移动，以免不小心碰到他的第三只眼。他肩膀宽阔，眼睛直视前方，姿势除了有些疲惫之外毫无瑕疵，仿佛并没有刚刚经历一场艰苦的战斗，而是在平淡无奇的日子里站在舰桥上向你打招呼。
The leader of the ravaged ship's crew wipes the sweat from his brow in a motion characteristic of Navigators — not with his whole hand, but with two fingers moving from left to right and back so as not to touch his third eye by accident. This movement is the only sign of fatigue he allows himself. His posture is immaculate, shoulders wide, eyes front — as if he did not just emerge from a gruelling battle and is simply greeting you on the bridge on an uneventful day.

“向您致意，大人。您无需自我介绍，我一眼就认出您是冯·瓦兰修斯家族的继承人。我是卡西尼家族的汉恩，这艘舰船的导航者。作为幸存下来的高阶军官，我在事故发生之后就接管了指挥权。”
"My greetings, Your {mf|Lordship|Ladyship}. There is no need for introductions — I know the heirs of the von Valancius line by sight. I am Hann of House Cassini, the Navigator on this vessel, and, as the senior surviving officer, I assumed command after the incident."

“考虑到当下的情况，这可真是场意想不到的愉快会面，导航者女士。”汉恩彬彬有礼地向卡西娅低下头。“我还没有幸和您见过面，但我认得奥赛罗家族的纹章。尊敬的摄政王贵体是否安康？”
"Lady Navigator, what an unexpected and pleasant meeting, considering the circumstances." Hann decorously dips his head to Cassia. "I have not had the honour of being personally acquainted with you, but of course I know the emblem of House Orsellio. How fares the honourable regent?"

“卡西娅·奥赛罗向您致意。”卡西娅微微点头。“亲切的话语无法掩盖您言语中冰蓝色的冷淡，导航者汉恩，但我会将您诚挚的问候转告给阿伦托摄政王，也请您代我向卡西尼大族长致以最诚挚的祝愿。先把寒暄的事情放在一旁吧，我相信行商浪人想问您一些问题。”
"Cassia Orsellio." She offers a reserved nod. "Cordiality will not disguise the ice-blue coldness of your words, Navigator Hann. However, I will tell Regent Aaronto that you enquired after his health. You may pass on my best wishes to Novator Cassini. And now let's set the pleasantries aside. I'm sure the Rogue Trader would like to ask you a few questions."

汉恩说话的时候，其他人都在盯着你，神色无比紧张。如果仔细看看的话，你可以清楚看到他们每个人都是变种人。他们的脸和身体都出现了怪诞的畸变，与那些袭击你的疯子们一样，不过，他们却似乎非常清醒，没有表现出任何疯狂的迹象。
As Hann speaks, others glare at you with dour tension. Up close, you can plainly see that every one of them is a mutant. Their faces and bodies bear the mark of incredibly grotesque deformities, the same as the muttering madmen who attacked you. However, Hann's associates seem perfectly clear-eyed, showing no signs of madness.

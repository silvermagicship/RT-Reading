三相体匍匐在医疗舱入口前，门上模糊的双头鹰徽记是他呓语的唯一听众。这位正义的三位一体者歇斯底里地哀嚎着，声音时不时被他用改造过的头骨撞击甲板的闷响打断。粘稠的血油混合物从他额头缝合钉的缝隙间渗出，他的指甲因试图挖出体内的植入体而尽数翻裂。
The Triasomata lies prostrate before the entrance to the bay, the faint Aquila on the door the only witness to his incoherent babbling. His hysterical wails of the righteous triune man are interrupted from time to time by the clanging of his augmented skull against the deck. A viscous mixture of blood and oil oozes from the staples on his forehead, and his fingernails are chipped from his attempts to dig the implants out of his body.

执法者用雷鸣般的声音对着空气发号施令：“检……检查军械库！王朝的旗帜是我们安息的床褥和裹尸布……你想让我关你禁闭吗？”他的身体剧烈抽搐，那本就支离破碎的言语化作一片骇人的杂音，几不成语。突然，三相体再次用头撞击甲板，迸出一串支离破碎的句子：“求求你……求求你……活着回来。你的头盔呢，士兵？神皇啊，我无所畏惧！我无所畏惧……”抽搐再次发作。
The enforcer's thunderous voice commands into the empty air: "Ch... check the arsenaaal! The dynasty's banner is ooour eiderdown and shroud... Do you want me to throw you in a cell?" A spasm racks his body, and his already muddled speech turns into a frightening cacophony that barely sounds like language. Suddenly, the Triasomata pounds his head on the deck again and spews forth another medley of ragged sentences: "Please... please... come baaack alive. Where's your helmet, soooldier? God-Emperor, I will not be afraid! I will not be afraid..." The spasm repeats.

伊奥贡不悦地咂嘴，思索时忧愁地摩挲着胡须上的一个活塞。“实验体过早离开了仿生区。努恩修斯说得对，我太专注于三相体融合手术及其初步成功，以至于犯了一个错误——在进行心理评估之前就解开了束缚。行商浪人，我们必须在彻底失去三相体之前，稳定其状况。”
Eogunn clucks his tongue in displeasure, worrying at a piston on his beard as he thinks. "The subject left the Sector Bionica prematurely. Nuncius is right, I was so absorbed in the triasomia procedure and its successful outcome that I made the mistake of unfastening the restraints before the psychological evaluation could be conducted. Rogue Trader, we must stabilise the Triasomata before the specimen is forever lost to us."

You scarcely recognise the formerly sanguine and imperturbable Arbitrator in the frenzied, roiling mass of rage before you. Solomorne's face is twisted in a savage snarl: "I'll kill every last one of you! I'll kill you all! Where's my dog, you scum? WHERE IS HE?"

“舰长大人。”阿贝拉德以惯常的礼节向你问好，但用审视的目光将你从头到脚扫视了一遍。
"Lord Captain." Abelard greets you with customary courtesy but makes sure to scan you from head to toe with his scrutinising gaze.

"I was astonished to learn that our Lady Navigator has been amending entries in the ship's log, and the officers have been keeping silent on her orders. I immediately demanded an end to the outrageous conduct and I reprimanded Lady Cassia in front of the officers. She greeted my words with silence and then quickly departed." Abelard heaves a sigh. "I should not have acted thus. I never even found out what drove her to do it."

阿贝拉德拿起一块数据板，眉头紧锁。“恐怕我并不知道这些藏匿处在什么地方，但我可以提供一些帮助。”他把数据板递给了你。“您的前任曾用这串数字代码关闭过某些防御系统。如果我真的像我想象得那么了解西奥多拉夫人的话，那么我敢肯定，她会对她的藏匿处严加防守。在这种情况下，这串代码肯定会对您有用。”
Abelard picks up a data-slate, his brows furrowing. "I'm afraid I do not know how to find the caches, but I can offer some help." He holds out the data-slate to you. "Your predecessor used this numeric code to deactivate certain defence systems in her service. If I know Lady Theodora as well as I think, then I am quite certain that she left her caches well guarded — in that case, this code will be useful to you."

“这个话题不应该是闲聊的话题，应而该在官方简报中讨论。如果您允许的话，我会以我自己的话，尽可能简洁地回答这个问题。”阿贝拉德喘了口气，说了起来。“科罗努斯扩区与帝国的中心地带相距甚远。这意味着，这片区域的亚空间航线每隔几个月就会失效，已经建立的航线常常会遭到各种敌人的袭击。而在唯一的主要港口中，帝国的护卫舰往往会停泊在海盗船旁边。”
"This is a topic for an official briefing, not a casual conversation, but I will try to answer succinctly — and, if you permit, in my own words." Abelard takes a breath and begins. "The Koronus Expanse is considerably removed from the heart of the Imperium. This means that local warp routes become useless within months, established pathways are regularly subject to attacks from all kinds of rabble, and in the only major port, the Imperium's frigates find themselves moored alongside pirate vessels."

“直到最近不久，科罗努斯扩区还很难称得上是帝国的疆域。大审判官的到来改变了这样的情况，但他的影响也很有限。如您所知，这片区域有它自己的运行规则，它比帝国的律法更加极端。但与此同时，这里的规则也允许您另辟蹊径，获得胜利。科罗努斯扩区被视作行商浪人的领地，这并非没有原因。只有行商浪人才拥有足够的胆识与军事力量，在授状赋予的权力之下，探索科罗努斯扩区尚未开辟的区域，存活下来……如果一切顺利，还能守住他们在探索过程中开辟的疆土。”
"Until recently, the Koronus Expanse could hardly have been called a region of the Imperium. The situation has changed with the arrival of the Lord Inquisitor, but not by much. This place operates under its own rules, you see — more radical, so to speak, but ones that allow for a non-standard approach where there is a promise of victory. The Koronus Expanse is considered Rogue Trader territory for a reason. Only Rogue Traders have sufficient military might, audacity, and the rights granted them by the Warrant to survive the leap into the unexplored part of the Expanse... and in the event of a successful outcome, to hold onto whatever they manage to capture on the frontier."

发起：阿洁塔

  • “行商浪人和那个异形太过亲密，{mf|他|她}甚至触及到了她的灵魂，理解了她的感受……多么邪恶！多么罪不可恕的异端行为！”
  • "The Rogue Trader has grown so close to the xenos that {mf|he|she} has delved into her very soul, comprehended her feelings... Ungodly, unforgivable heresy!"

回应

  • 帕斯卡 | 跳到帕斯卡视图
  • “反对：舰长大人的行为与标准的情感表达方式有所不同。假设：（a）异形在说谎；（b）舰长大人在说谎；或（c）存在未知变量。需要进一步观察以确定该现象的真正性质。
  • "Objection: the Lord Captain's behaviour differs from the standard expression of feelings. Hypotheses: (a) the xenos is lying; (b) the Lord Captain is lying; or (c) there is an unknown variable. Further observations are required to establish the true nature of this phenomenon."
  • 卡西娅 | 跳到卡西娅视图
  • “我看到舰长大人和伊莉耶特的周围，笼罩着一层银蓝色的光芒。这种颜色……与我所见的任何颜色都不一样。他们的感情似乎存在于某种外人所不知晓的位面上。”
  • "I see a silvery-blue glow around the Lord Captain and Yrliet. These colours are... unlike anything I've ever seen. It is as if their feelings exist on some unknowable level."

  • "If you'd asked me before what was more likely to happen — me becoming a nun or Master Killjoy hitting it off with the Lord Captain — I would have gone and bought me some prayer beads."

  • 阿洁塔 | 跳到伊迪拉视图
  • “你的一句话既嘲笑了信仰，又嘲笑了他人的感情。你可真是不知廉耻，女巫。”
  • "With one remark, you have scoffed at both the faith and other people's feelings. You really have no shame, witch."
  • 帕斯卡 | 跳到伊迪拉视图
  • “这位教外人士的统计分析大错特错。事实很明显，你没有办法购买念珠，主要由于你毫无节制的懒惰浪费掉了你的维护费用。”
  • "The laywoman's foray into statistical analysis has failed deplorably. It is evident that you do not possess the means to purchase prayer beads, for the primary reason that you squander your upkeep on continued indolence."

伊奥贡 -> 帕斯卡、索罗蒙

_触发提示：DLC3 / PasqalQ1Finished_DLC3_

发起：伊奥贡

  • “哈努曼贤者，你与机械修会同僚之间的冲突引起了我的好奇。你违反了哪条教义，竟招致如此攻击？你清楚原因吗？我与努恩修斯可以协助检查死者的植入物——”
  • "Magos Haneumann, your conflict with fellow members of the Cult Mechanicus has piqued my curiosity. What dogma have you violated to provoke such an attack? Do you even know? Nuncius and I could assist with examining the augments of the fallen—"

回应

  • 帕斯卡
  • “基因士费布鲁斯发出的无谓质询与亵渎暗示已驳回。在欧姆尼赛亚面前，我的言行与思想均纯洁无瑕。你也敢如此承诺吗，操纵者？”
  • "The idle queries and profane insinuations uttered by Genetor Februs are rejected. I am pure before the Omnissiah in deed and thought. Can you say the same, Manipulus?"
  • 索罗蒙
  • “一个深陷异端泥潭的人，居然还如此自信，真是令人惊叹，技术神甫。”
  • "You're awfully confident for someone who is up to his ears in heresy himself, Tech-Priest."

婕伊 -> 索罗蒙、海因里希

_触发提示：第2章至第4章；DLC3 / PatentStolen2_1_Jae_

发起：婕伊

  • “反正迟早会有人问出口，不如就让我来吧。只是单纯出于好奇……行商浪人要是没了贸易授状，还算不算行商浪人？”
  • "Someone is bound to ask sooner or later, so it might as well be me. Purely out of curiosity... is a Rogue Trader still a Rogue Trader if they don't have a Warrant?"

回应

  • 索罗蒙
  • “依据《帝国法典》的条文，若缺少贸易授权，便无权再自称为行商浪人。至于王朝已征服的世界……其法律地位尚不明确。”
  • "According to the letter of the Lex Imperialis, the lack of a supporting document revokes the right to style oneself as a Rogue Trader. The status of worlds already conquered by the dynasty, meanwhile, is... is unclear."
  • 海因里希
  • “这么问可有点危险啊，亲爱的。现在的你可是借着行商浪人的庇护，才不至于受到异形学说的指控。”
  • "A dangerous question to pose for someone using a Rogue Trader's patronage as a shield against accusations of xenoheresy, 'shereen.'"

阿洁塔 -> 乌尔法、索罗蒙

_触发提示：第2章至第4章；DLC3 / SeberianNOTReturned2_1_Argenta_

发起：阿洁塔

  • “愿神皇赐福于行商浪人的仁慈之举！没有哪个帝国的忠实仆人应当受到被异形囚禁的待遇，尤其那人还是塞贝里安·冯·瓦兰修斯！”
  • "God-Emperor bless the mercy of the Rogue Trader! No honest servant of the Imperium deserves to be held captive by a xenos, and Seberian von Valancius least of all!"

回应

  • 乌尔法
  • “数百年来，那邪异的生物剥夺了狼领先祖为人类而战的荣耀。如今，那位战士理应在浩瀚无垠的科罗努斯扩区，迎来属于他的光荣战死！”
  • "For centuries, that vile creature robbed the Aett-Vater's forefather of the joy of serving in the name of Humanity. That warrior deserves to at last find glorious death in the boundless Koronus Expanse!"
  • 索罗蒙
  • “留下塞贝里安是个可敬的决定。但我们仍需向帝国内政部解释，扩区为何会同时存在两位合法的冯·瓦兰修斯行商浪人。”
  • "The decision to leave Seberian was a brave one. But we will still have to explain to the Adeptus Administratum how there can be two official von Valancius Rogue Traders in the Expanse."

卡西娅 -> 伊迪拉、伊莉耶特

_触发提示：第2章至第4章；DLC3 / PatentStolen3_1_Cassia_

发起：卡西娅

  • “我能感知到四周弥漫的困惑，如同整瓶颜料泼洒在巨幅画布上。我知道，这都是因为那份贸易授状。但为何会引发如此骚动？难道行商浪人本身的血脉还不够神圣么？”
  • "I can sense the confusion all around me. It is as if a full bottle of paint has been splashed onto a huge canvas. It's because of Warrant, I know. But why the commotion? Isn't the very blood of the Rogue Trader sacred?"

回应

  • 伊迪拉
  • “就我个人而言，我只是……一想到贸易授状不在它该在的地方，就心神不宁。我太习惯它的存在了，在我看来，它与舰船已融为一体。”
  • "Personally, I'm just... uneasy at the thought of the Warrant not being where it's supposed to be. I'm so used to its existence, it's like it's fused with the ship itself."
  • 伊莉耶特
  • “猴子总对自己凭空赋予意义的事物过分执着……如今这样一件东西消失了，便让你们的心灵因凡俗的恐惧而颤栗。”
  • "Mon-keigh attach so much importance to things they themselves have endowed with significance... and now the disappearance of such an object chills your hearts with mortal terror."

阿贝拉德 -> 伊莉耶特、绮贝拉

_触发提示：第2章至第4章；DLC3 / SeberianReturned2_1_Abelard_

发起：阿贝拉德

  • “塞贝里安的结局早已在意料之中。伟大的行商浪人时代已然终结，如今异形的阴谋只会扰乱原本的流程与岁月，难以再彻底改变其走向。”
  • "The fate that befell Seberian was to be expected. The time of the great Rogue Trader has passed, and now the xenos' connivances only disrupt the ordinary course of events and time, and do not reshape it completely.

回应

  • 伊莉耶特
  • “你在思索自身即将终结的生命，对吧？然而你的言语却如此从容淡定……着实令人钦佩。”
  • "You are contemplating the approaching end of your time, are you not? And yet your words are filled with serenity... Remarkable."
  • 绮贝拉
  • “不死之神早于诸多轮回前便低语过秘者塞贝里安的名讳，靠近他如同经受酷刑……但你的名字仍属于你自己，信者阿贝拉德，此刻伤怀还为时过早。”
  • "The Undying One whispered Domin'Seberian's name many cycles ago, and being near him was... excruciating. But your name remains with you, Amic'Abelard, and it is too soon for you to sorrow."

卡西娅 -> 婕伊、伊莉耶特

_触发提示：第2章至第4章；DLC3 / VivisectorChoises3_1_Cassia_

发起：卡西娅

  • “甲板上的游行队伍沐浴在鲜艳的色彩之中，尽管参与者皆是卑微的贱民。这种强烈的对比令人感到怪异，但是……竟有几分美丽。我的颜料能够描绘出这一切吗？”
  • "The procession on deck awash with such bright colours, though it consisted of lowly rabble. The contrast seems unusual and even... beautiful. Will my paints be able to convey it?"

回应

  • 婕伊
  • “我眼中的明光啊，求你快去下层甲板探个头，汲取鲜活的灵感吧。我只怕你那颗颤抖的心会承受不住。”
  • "Light of my eyes, I beg you, go and poke your head out on the lower decks for a fresh dose of inspiration. I fear your quivering heart may not be able to bear it."
  • 伊莉耶特
  • “那些因外界人关心他们的身躯而前来致谢的猴子，态度倒的确诚恳。但奴隶们为了区区一口食物，竟温顺地抚摸主人的手——这景象，可谈不上美丽。”
  • "The mon-keigh that came to thank the elantach for showing concern for their bodies were sincere. But a slave caressing their master's hand for a morsel of food is not something I would deem beautiful."

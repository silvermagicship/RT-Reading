人性的代价

任务简介

冯·瓦兰修斯家族期间的星语唱诗班突然收到了一条信息，这条信息让海因里希非常不安。虽然行商浪人并不清楚具体细节，但海因里希已经请求行商浪人的协助，并强调他打算立刻处理这件事情。

A certain message received by the Astropathic Choir of the von Valancius flagship has disconcerted Heinrix. Whatever caused this sudden unease, the Interrogator has requested the Rogue Trader's assistance and stressed that the matter demands immediate attention.

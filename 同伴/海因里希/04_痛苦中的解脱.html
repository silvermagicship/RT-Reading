A Reprieve From Torment

任务简介

海因里希之前提到，他需要在尖啸者巢穴某个安静的角落休养生息。或许现在是时候探望他了，看看这位审讯官在遭受酷刑之后恢复得怎么样。

Heinrix mentioned needing time to recuperate somewhere in a quiet corner of the Shriekers' lair. Maybe now is a good time to see how the Interrogator has been holding up after his torture.

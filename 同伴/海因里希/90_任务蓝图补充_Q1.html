海因里希：任务蓝图补充 - Q1

返回角色目录 | 返回同伴目录

场景：HighjackedShuttle_dialogue

舵手长雷沃的声音颤抖着，几乎无法克制他的愤怒。“船长老大！我们被抢了，真希望亚空间弄死那个狗娘养的！船上有一艘穿梭机被人开走了，你猜是谁干的？音阵大师报告说，那艘船被……被审判庭的狗腿子给‘征用’走了，以黄金王座的名义拿去调查用了！”
Ravor's voice trembles — the Master Helmsman can barely restrain his rage. "Lord Captain! Warp take that son of a— we have been robbed! The voidship has lost one shuttle, and know who we have to thank for it? As the Vox Master reported, the lander has been... 'requisitioned' by the servant of the Inquisition to conduct an investigation in the interests of the Golden Throne!"

选项 Choices

  • 选择 Choice

“你说什么……海因里希开走了穿梭机？他现在在什么地方？”
"Wait... Heinrix took the shuttle? Where is he now?"

维格迪斯从雷沃那里接管了音阵频道，音阵系统中传来了明显的咔哒声。“穿梭机离开虚空舰之后，便向基亚瓦伽马星的制造工厂驶去。我不确定穿梭机是否已经抵达星球表面。俄歇矩阵失去了那艘飞船的踪迹，我们也未能与冯·卡洛克斯取得联系。我相信，那位审讯官认为那颗行星上发生的事情，比护送行商浪人更加重要。”
There is an audible click as Vigdis takes over the vox-channel from Ravor. "The shuttle undocked and headed toward the manufactorum of Kiava Gamma. I do not know if the flyer made it to the surface of the planet — the augurs lost track of it. We also failed to establish a vox-connection with van Calox. I believe the Interrogator has decided that what is happening on the planet is more important than providing escort to the Rogue Trader."

“我们可以继续在轨道上停留，想办法寻找审讯官或是穿梭机的踪迹。不过，那位审讯官把行踪掩饰得非常隐蔽。考虑到这颗行星的当前情况，我不确定我们能否真的很快找到他们。恐怕海因里希·冯·卡洛克斯已经永远地离开了您的舰队。”
"We could linger in orbit and make some effort to find the Interrogator or shuttle, but, given the circumstances on the planet and the ability of the Interrogator to go undetected when he wants to... I'm not sure you can count on quick success. I am afraid Heinrix van Calox has left your retinue for good.'

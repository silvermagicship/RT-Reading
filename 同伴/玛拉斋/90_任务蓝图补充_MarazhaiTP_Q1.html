玛拉斋：任务蓝图补充 - MarazhaiTP_Q1

返回角色目录 | 返回同伴目录

场景：CrazedXenos

“舰长大人，军官甲板上发生了……紧急情况。”维格迪斯的声音有些颤抖。“出现了无数受害者，连接也被切断了……船员报告说船舱里充满了亚空间的声音……但事故原因并不是亚空间突破了力场，而是……您在加努斯带上船的异形造成的。他发了疯，在船上横冲直撞。”
"Lord Captain, there is an... an emergency at the officers' deck." Vigdis's voice is quaking slightly. "Numerous victims, loss of communication... The voidsmen report warp-voices filling the compartments... but this is not a warp breach. This is... the xenos you took aboard at Janus. He has gone mad and went on a rampage."

“我收到了两份相当可靠的证人供词。其中一个人说那个异形突然开始袭击船员，所有被他撞到的人都会被膛破肚。而另一个人说，活下来的人把自己锁在了隔壁的船舱里。执法者成功地将那个发了疯的异形隔断在死胡同里……但到目前为止，没人能将他制服。您有什么指示？”
"I have two fairly reliable witness accounts. According to one, the xenos suddenly assaulted the crew members, gutting them seemingly at random. The other claims that there are survivors who barricaded themselves in the adjacent compartments. The enforcers managed to isolate the raving xenos in a dead end... But they have been unable to pacify him so far. What will your orders be?"

"Do you remember what happened during my very first voyage aboard your ship? When I found myself beyond the veil, the servants of She Who Thirsts descended upon the fragile shell that is protecting your vessel from the outside horrors and managed to break through. The soul of an Aeldari shines like a beacon in the night, drawing the gaze of Sai'lanthresh and stoking her thirst."

“阿苏焉的教诲可以让我们免受她的伤害，可那些黑暗子嗣……他们从来没有努力遏制过对愉悦的渴望，从而抵挡永恒之敌的魔爪。当初，饥渴女士呼唤我时，我通过冥想保护了自己的灵魂。我认为……我认为玛拉斋打算以他唯一了解的方法，填满他那枯萎的灵魂。”
"The ways of Asuryan keep us safe from her claws, but the Dark Ones... The Dark Ones never sought to restrain their desires for excess and pleasure in order to acquire at least some protection against our eternal enemy. When the voices of She Who Thirsts called out to me, I found refuge in meditation. I think... I think Marazhai started feeding his withering soul instead, using the only method he knew."

Vigdis sighs. "Lord Captain... a pair of voidsmen managed to relay to us before dying that their comrades are disappearing into a bloody fog only to emerge in pieces... Your enforcers are trained to suppress human riots, not to restrain deadly xenos."

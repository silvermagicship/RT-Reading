异形的游戏：审判补充

返回角色目录 | 返回同伴目录

场景：MarazhaiTrial

  • 注：出现条件：Etude

在深渊的映衬之下，眼前的黑暗灵族看起来有些陌生。看到你出现后，这些瘦长的黑色身影纷纷动了起来。狂风阴谋团的战士们一个接一个地转过身，你感觉到他们的目光落在了你身上。但令你惊讶的是，他们之中没有任何人举起武器。
The Drukhari look foreign against the background of the gorge, long black figures that stir into motion when you appear. One after the other, the warriors of the Kabal of the Reaving Tempest turn, and you can almost physically feel their eyes land on you. It is surprising, but none of them raises their weapon.

玛拉斋看了那些异形一眼，发出满意的嘶嘶声。“看吧，{name}。你有没有注意到他们摆出的姿势？有没有看到他们头部倾斜的方式？最重要的是，他们的人数比我预想的要多。考虑到整个科摩罗都联合起来对抗——”
Marazhai casts a glance at the xenos and hisses in satisfaction. "Look, {name}. Can you see their poses? The incline of their heads? And most importantly, they are more numerous than I expected to see after all Commorragh rallied against the Ka—"

你看到了一个戴着金色面具的异形，你认出了他。当时就是他负责主持那场针对你的虚假审判。那个时候，他的身形从痛苦记忆的阴霾浮现，笼罩在寄生虫引起的幻梦之上。
You know the xenos in the split golden mask. The figure who presided over your fake inquisitorial trial emerges from the haze of anguished memory, shrouded in the parasite-induced gloom.

“纳札骸，黑心阴谋团的执政官。居然亲自出马了，真是让人惊喜。”尽管玛拉斋故作镇定，但你能从他的声音里听出了发自内心的惊讶，甚至是惊喜。很显然，他根本没意料到会发生这样的事情。
"Nazrakhei. Archon of the Black Heart. In the flesh. What a surprise." Marazhai's voice is deliberately calm, but you catch a note of genuine surprise, even amazement. This is the last thing he was expecting.

“那个执政官看来是忍不住了，打算亲自动手……”
"The Archon would hardly deny himself the pleasure of taking part in a raid..."

“没错，猴子。这是一场突袭，不是针对逃亡者的狩猎。”
"A raid, mon-keigh. Not a hunt for a single fugitive."

在纳札骸的注视下，伊莉耶特几乎要退缩了，可能是出于厌恶或是恐惧。“我……我记得他。在科摩罗的那场审判中，我就是回答了他的问题……”她的声音越来越低，愤怒地扭着脸。
Yrliet almost recoils under Nazrakhei's gaze, either out of fear or disgust. "I... remember him. It was to him that I answered in that trial in Commorragh..." She trails off and grimaces in anger.

“怎么了，行商浪人？既然我们的目标都已经露脸了……我们为什么还要浪费时间？快……下达命令吧……”阿洁塔浑身颤抖着，渴望举起武器。
"Well, Rogue Trader? Why are we wasting time... when all our targets are out in the open? Give... the order..." Argenta is shaking with the desire to raise her weapon.

“你真的来了，你这愚蠢的小崽子。”纳札骸的声音让你感觉脑袋里有点发痒。曾几何时，有条食脑蛆虫就住在你的脑袋里。“看来卡瑞尔并没有说谎。他说你是个蠢货，也是个厚颜无耻的家伙，一定会再次出现在狂风阴谋团面前……或者更确切地说，是出现在黑心阴谋团新的成员面前。”
"You came, you foolish whelp." The mere sound of Nazrakhei's voice gives you an itchy feeling in your head where the brain-eating maggot was once nestled. "Kharael was not lying when he claimed you would be stupid enough, or brazen enough, to show yourself before the Reaving Tempest again... or rather, before the new ranks of the Black Heart."

"Sinisthoria. The last Succubus from the Cult of the Fatal Thirst that ruled the arena." Marazhai responds without taking his eyes off his kin. "It was she who suggested I join the Bloodstained Proselytes instead of decorating her chambers with my innards."

“我叛逆的小玩具。”希斯索莉亚的声音中充斥着难以想象的残忍。“我的教派慷慨地为你提供了庇护，而你却以如此荒谬的方式逃了出去。你该不会真的以为，你做了这样的事情，还能得到原谅吧？”
"My rebellious toy." Sinisthoria's voice rings with every imaginable shade of cruelty. "You did not honestly think your ridiculous escape from the Cult that had graciously given you refuge would be forgiven, did you?"

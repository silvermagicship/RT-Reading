场景

你大口大口呼吸着有毒的空气，每一口呼吸都像是肺里着了火，仿佛连内脏都烧了起来。你身体的每个细胞都被疯狂的痛苦吞噬，就像是被浸泡在熔融的金属中，被活生生地剥了皮一样。你听到远处传来的声音，那是你的同伴呻吟的声音，赤脚行走的声音……以及慢慢靠近的微弱脚步声。
You gasp, sucking in the poisoned air, each breath scorching your insides with liquid fire from throat to lungs. Every cell in your body is engulfed in maddening pain, as if you are being doused in molten metal, skinned alive, and subjected to yet another form of torture. You hear sounds coming from somewhere far away: the moans of your people, the shuffling of bare feet... and light footsteps slowly getting closer.

“行商浪人，我一直期待与你见面。”在痛苦的阴霾之中，你几乎什么都看不清楚，但你依然清楚地记得这个声音，就是这个异形摧毁了你的首都世界。玛拉斋的面孔藏在头盔后面，但他的语气确凿无疑——他是在幸灾乐祸。
"Rogue Trader. A long-awaited meeting indeed." You can barely see anything through the haze of agony but you still remember the voice well: the voice of the xenos who devastated your capital world. Marazhai's face is hidden behind a helmet, but his tone leaves no room for doubt: he is smirking.

玛拉斋将注意力转向了其他人。“你已经证明了你是个有用的猴子。对猴子来说，这确实挺不容易的。”他对躺在附近的阿吉里亚斯开了口。阿吉里亚斯就躺在附近，苍白的脸因恐惧和痛苦而扭曲，眼睛瞪得大大的，满是泪水。“以后你说不定还有别的用途。”
The xenos turns his attention to someone else. "You have proven quite useful, mon-keigh. An uncanny quality for your kind." He is speaking to Achilleas, who is lying nearby, his pale face contorted in terror and pain, and his eyes wide open and tearful. "I may find other uses for you in the future."

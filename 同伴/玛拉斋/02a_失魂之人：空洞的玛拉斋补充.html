失魂之人：空洞的玛拉斋补充

返回角色目录 | 返回同伴目录

场景：HollowMarazhai

当你出现时，玛拉斋猛地抬起了头。他憔悴不已的脸惨白如纸，狂暴地露出了尖牙。他的黑眼睛仿佛变成了两块漆黑的镜子，当你凝视他的时候，可以清楚地看到你自己的倒影。他张开了嘴，但什么都没有说，而是发出了古怪的嘶嘶声，非常刺耳。就算眼前的黑暗灵族曾经拥有智慧，此刻看起来也没有一丝理智的迹象。
Marazhai jerks his head up when you appear. His already gaunt face is tightly wrapped in pale grey, parchment-like skin, his teeth are bared feverishly, and his dark eyes have turned into two pitch-black mirrors that could show your reflection clearly if you were to gaze into them. He opens his mouth, but instead of words, an unnatural, rasping hiss comes out. If this creature ever possessed intelligence, there is no sign of it left.

“终于……”当你穿过甲板时，周围的声音一直回荡在你耳边，听起来仿佛来自四面八方。这不禁令你毛骨悚然。“我们终于见面了，我可怜的孩子。看看他吧，凡人。尽管他的族人如此顽固地拒绝王子的爱抚，可他此刻是多么渴望回到王子身边。来吧，孩子。听我的话，我的王子会赐予你这世上一切痛苦与极乐。”
"At last..." The resonant voice that has followed you on your journey through the deck sounds like it is coming from every direction, chilling you to the bone. "At last we meet, my poor child. Look at him, mortal. Look at how he yearns to return to the one whose caress his kind rejects so stubbornly. Come, child. Follow my voice, and my Prince will grant you all the bliss and torment in this universe."

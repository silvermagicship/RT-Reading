失魂之人

Soulless

任务简介

玛拉斋登舰之后，第一次亚空间跳跃就发生了悲惨的事故。他似乎彻底发了疯，一路肆意杀害船员，在军官甲板上留下了许多惨遭开膛破肚的受害者。行商浪人必须沿着血迹找到威胁的源头，彻底解决这个问题。

The very first warp jump with a Drukhari on board has resulted in tragedy. Numerous disembowelled victims left on the officers' deck mark the path walked by Marazhai, who seems to have completely lost his mind. The source of this threat needs to be located and neutralised.

目标

1. 前往军官甲板，与幸存者见面

标题

前往军官甲板，与幸存者见面

Meet with the survivors on the officers' deck

描述

一群执法者想办法将玛拉斋隔离在隔间里。行商浪人需要询问船员，弄清甲板上究竟发生了什么事情。

A group of enforcers have managed to contain the xenos in a single compartment. The von Valancius subjects must be questioned in order to glean the details of what happened on the deck.

2. 找到发疯的玛拉斋

标题

找到发疯的玛拉斋

Find the mad xenos

描述

执法者打开了通道，通往发狂的玛拉斋所在的房间。行商浪人必须搜索走廊，找到对舰船安全构成威胁的异形。

The enforcers have unsealed way to the compartment containing the xenos in question. The Lord Captain must now explore the corridors in search of the threat.

3. 与玛拉斋交谈

标题

与玛拉斋交谈

Talk to Marazhai

描述

行商浪人已经找到了发狂的玛拉斋。他此刻的精神状态，对周围所有人都构成了极大威胁，行商浪人需要亲自介入这件事情。

The runaway xenos has been found. His present state poses extreme danger to anyone around him. The Lord Captain's intervention is required.

4. 杀死恶魔

标题

杀死恶魔

Kill the daemons

描述

来自亚空间的怪物出现在了船上，必须将其立刻消灭。

Warp entities are confirmed to be manifesting in the compartment. They must be eliminated at once.

5. 决定玛拉斋的命运

标题

决定玛拉斋的命运

Decide Marazhai's fate

描述

船员已经不再受到直接威胁。至于那位精神状态不稳定的同伴，行商浪人必须立刻决定他的命运。

The immediate threat to the crew has been neutralised. A decision has to be made regarding the fate of the Lord Captain's unstable companion.

6. 在舰桥上与玛拉斋交谈

标题

在舰桥上与玛拉斋交谈

Talk to Marazhai on the ship's bridge

描述

紧张局势得到了缓和，玛拉斋也从大屠杀的疯狂中清醒过来。行商浪人需要弄清楚玛拉斋此刻的精神状态。

Once the tensions have eased and the aftermath of the carnage has been alleviated, the Lord Captain should enquire about the xenos's state of mind.

完成文本

军官甲板的问题已经得到了解决。当虚空舰回到真实世界之后，那些被发疯的玛拉斋杀害的船员被埋葬在了虚空之中。

The situation on the officers' deck was resolved. The victims of the xenos's mad rampage were interred in the void upon the ship's translation back to realspace.

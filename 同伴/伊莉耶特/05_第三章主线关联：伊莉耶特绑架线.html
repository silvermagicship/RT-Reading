"Stop shivering like a leaf in the wind, mon-keigh. Answer me: have you seen others like me in this system? If you have, tell me where, when, and under what circumstances your paths crossed and diverged afterward."

军官一见到伊莉耶特就瞪大了眼睛，脸色变得比之前更加惨白。他的嘴唇在无言地祈祷，什么话都说不出来。
The officer's eyes widen at the sight of Yrliet. His face grows even paler than before and his lips move in a wordless prayer, unable to utter a word in response.

场景

你大口大口呼吸着有毒的空气，每一口呼吸都像是肺里着了火，仿佛连内脏都烧了起来。你身体的每个细胞都被疯狂的痛苦吞噬，就像是被浸泡在熔融的金属中，被活生生地剥了皮一样。你听到远处传来的声音，那是你的同伴呻吟的声音，赤脚行走的声音……以及慢慢靠近的微弱脚步声。
You gasp, sucking in the poisoned air, each breath scorching your insides with liquid fire from throat to lungs. Every cell in your body is engulfed in maddening pain, as if you are being doused in molten metal, skinned alive, and subjected to yet another form of torture. You hear sounds coming from somewhere far away: the moans of your people, the shuffling of bare feet... and light footsteps slowly getting closer.

“行商浪人，我一直期待与你见面。”在痛苦的阴霾之中，你几乎什么都看不清楚，但你依然清楚地记得这个声音，就是这个异形摧毁了你的首都世界。玛拉斋的面孔藏在头盔后面，但他的语气确凿无疑——他是在幸灾乐祸。
"Rogue Trader. A long-awaited meeting indeed." You can barely see anything through the haze of agony but you still remember the voice well: the voice of the xenos who devastated your capital world. Marazhai's face is hidden behind a helmet, but his tone leaves no room for doubt: he is smirking.

伊莉耶特倒在你旁边，痛苦地扭动着，几乎无法呼吸。“不……你做了……什么……”
Yrliet, who is writhing in pain next to you, is barely able to breathe out, "No... What have you... done..."

“嗯嗯？难道你不满意吗，我的族人？我向你保证过，会带你见方舟世界的幸存者。别担心，我会带你去见他们的，说不定你们还会上演一场感人的亲人团聚呢。”
"Hmm? Are you dissatisfied, my kin? I promised that you would have your precious craftworld survivors. And so you will. I will take you to them. I might even be there to witness your jubilant reunion."

“有人警告过我，说其中某只猴子特别喜欢用致残类物质进行实验。所以，这种毒气包含了专门为你准备的成分。”眼前的异形脸上挂着幸灾乐祸的嘲笑，把绮贝菈死死地踩在地板上，脚底发出了“咯吱”一声。
"I was warned that one of the mon-keigh was fond of experimenting with incapacitating substances. This gas contains something prepared just for you." With a gloating sneer, the xenos steps on her head, pushing it into the floor with a crunch.

“格雷托……”索罗蒙伸出了手，像是要抚摸他的生化獒犬，又像是在通过手势发出命令，但格雷托却一动不动地躺着，没有任何反应。
"Glaito..." Solomorne is either trying to reach out to the cyber-mastiff or gesture a command to him, but the dog is lying still, completely unresponsive.

“别再白费心机了。有人告诉我，说有只猴子可能会把某些原始的玩具一起带来，于是我亲自做了检查，确保这种毒素对那些有趣的玩具也同样有效。”
"A futile effort. I was informed that the mon-keigh might bring some of their primitive toys here with them, so I made sure that the toxin accounted for their particular quirks."

“血管过滤系统正在失效……毒素的渗透力极其惊人……”他庞大的身躯轰然倒下。鲜血从操纵者的鼻孔和眼眶中涌出，但他的目光始终停留在身旁那无力飘浮的同伴身上。“努恩修斯，活下去……”
"Vascular filters are failing... the penetrative capacity of the toxin is extreme..." His bulky frame collapses. Blood streams from the Manipulus's nose and eyes, but his focus is pinned on his companion drifting listlessly at his side. "Nuncius, save yourself..."

玛拉斋露出胜利者般的笑容，猛地伸手抓住那伺服头骨。“哀嚎毫无意义，不过你的恳求在我听来却如同美妙的乐章，猴子。这小玩意儿现在归我了，你那可悲的小命也一样。”
With a look of triumph, Marazhai thrusts his hand forward, seizing the servo-skull. "Whining is futile, though your pleas are music to my ears, mon-keigh. This bauble belongs to me now. As does your pathetic life."

玛拉斋仔细观察着你那些痛苦的同伴，之后漫不经心地朝其中一个人做了个手势。“把注射器准备好，我答应带给特万提亚斯的猴子就在这些人里面。没必要再和他们多费工夫了，让首席解体大师来收拾他们吧。”
Marazhai takes a close look at your companions squirming in agony, then gestures nonchalantly to someone you cannot see. "Bring in the injectors. The mon-keigh I promised to Tervantias are among them. I do not want these wretches to move an inch until they are in the hands of the Archmachinator."

“至于你……”裁决官眼中闪烁着令人不安的光芒。“你也会享受到令你流连忘返的美梦，但我要把你留在身边。你太珍贵了，我可不能把你交给那些玩火的疯子。”
"As for you..." There is a disturbing gleam in the Dracon's eyes. "You will have your share of paralysing dreams too. But I am going to keep you. You are far too precious to be surrendered to a madman playing with fire."

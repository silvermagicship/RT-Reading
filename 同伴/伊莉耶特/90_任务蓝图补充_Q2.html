伊莉耶特：任务蓝图补充 - Q2

返回角色目录 | 返回同伴目录

场景：AeldariInTrouble_3

“船长老大，咱们前往这片星域不久之前发生过一场混战……黄金王座在上，这根本算不上护卫舰！简直就是一块被掏空了的铁块！”舵手长沉默了片刻。“我的天，罪魁祸首也在这个地方，是那些艾达灵族怪物！哪怕离得这么远，我也能一眼看出他们亮闪闪的屁股。不过……这些异形的行为很可疑。它们一直在原地打转，甚至连逃都不逃。咱们应该就这么过去，还是直接从远处开火？”
"Lord Cap'n, there is evidence of a recent scuffle straight ahead of us... Throne take me, this is not a frigate, this is just a gutted piece of metal!" The helmsman falls silent for a moment. "Blimey, the culprits are here too, it is the Aeldari freaks! I recognise their shiny asses even from this distance. Except... the xenos are acting suspiciously, they are hovering in place and not even trying to run. Should we approach or open fire from a distance?"

“快让你的族人住手，外界人！事情有点不太对劲。虽然阿苏焉的子嗣身处绝望之中，但他们真的会对猴子的舰船犯下这样的恶行吗？……不，我们不是禽兽。我恳求你谨慎行事，遵守你的诺言，不要因为我的亲人没有犯下过的罪行而判处他们死刑。”
"Call off your people, elantach! Something is wrong. The Children of Asuryan are dismayed, but doing something like this to a mon-keigh vessel... No, we are not animals. I appeal to your prudence and the promise you made, do not destroy my kin for the deeds they did not commit."

“舰长大人，我们收到了俄歇矩阵班组的报告，情况……相当不寻常。异形的飞船没有显示出任何生物或能量特征。尽管那艘飞船看起来完好无损，但扫描结果显示……船上一个人都没有。”
"Lord Captain, we have received a report from the augur crew. The situation is... unusual. The xenos ship displays no biological or energetical signatures, even though it appears to be intact. According to the scanners... there is nobody on board."

“不过，那些幸存者应该是藏在受损的帝国飞船其中一个船舱里。除此之外，我们还发现一部分浮在太空中的残骸，属于另一艘目前身份不明的舰船。”
"However, survivors are believed to be hiding in one of the compartments of the damaged vessel of the Imperium. We have also spotted some drifting plating remains that belong to a third, unidentified vessel."

After several hours of tedious waiting, Vigdis gets back on air. "Lord Captain, the scouts confirm our earlier report. The xenos ship is intact. Many valuable artefacts have been found inside, but neither the Aeldari nor their bodies were discovered."

“舰长大人，侦察兵从失事的舰船中救出了几名奄奄一息的军官。尽管他们受了伤，但他们告诉了我们之前发生的事情。他们的护卫舰在巡逻过程中，发现一艘艾达灵族的飞船穿越星系，于是他们决定立刻对人类之敌发动攻击。尽管异形的飞船过于灵活，根本无法击中，但异形飞船的引擎在战斗中突然失灵，护卫舰抓住这个机会进行了攻击。”
"Lord Captain, the scouts managed to rescue several emaciated officers from the wrecked ship. Despite their wounds, they were able to tell us what had happened: during a patrol, the frigate discovered an Aeldari ship crossing the system and decided to immediately attack the enemies of Humanity. The xenos vessel proved too fast and skittish, but at one point its engines failed, giving the frigate a chance to attack."

“宏炮刚刚瞄准敌舰，几艘黑暗灵族的飞船就从暗处中杀了出来。一开始，黑暗灵族只从远处攻击，但很快就登上了护卫舰，在甲板上进行了血腥的屠杀。幸存的那些军官藏在某个技术隔间，奇迹般地躲开了黑暗灵族的屠杀。幸存者声称，那些异形是故意把他们的飞船引到了这个陷阱里，让所有人在劫难逃。”
"When the guns were aimed, several Drukhari ships suddenly pounced from the darkness. At first they attacked from a distance, but soon boarded the frigate and wreaked a bloody massacre on the decks. It is only by sheer miracle that the surviving officers managed to escape, locking themselves up in a hidden technical compartment the xenos had no use for. The survivors claim that the xenos conspired and deliberately led the ship into a trap, where it met its doom."

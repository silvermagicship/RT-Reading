你眼前的艾达灵族用手捂住了脸，转向你。她苍白的皮肤紧绷着，双眼失去了光芒，脖子仿佛被看不见的重担压弯了。你认出了这个人是谁，她是伊莉耶特·拉娜维丝，你曾经的伙伴，正是她害得你被囚禁在这座黑暗的牢笼之中。
The Aeldari runs a hand over her face before turning to you. Her pale skin is tightly stretched over the bones of her skull, her eyes have lost their glimmer, and her neck is stooped under an unseen burden. And yet you still recognise her, Yrliet Lanaevyss, your former companion and the reason for your imprisonment within these dark walls.

伊莉耶特瞥了你一眼，微微睁大了眼睛，但除此之外，她紧张的脸上没有任何其他反应。虽然不知道她此刻心中是怎样的滋味，但她掩饰住了她的情绪。“外界人……”
Yrliet glances at you, her eyes widening slightly, but that is the only change you detect in her tense expression. Whatever emotions she may be feeling, she is suppressing them well. "Elantach..."

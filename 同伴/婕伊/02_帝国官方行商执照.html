帝国官方行商执照

Mercatum Tabula Officiale

任务简介

帝国官方行商执照可以为婕伊·海达利打开无数机会的大门，但如果得不到位高权重的朋友支持，婕伊的梦想、权力与财富仅仅只是异想天开而已。

The certificate of an official trade representative of the Imperium can open many doors for Jae Heydari. But without friends in high places, Jae can forget about her dreams of power and wealth.

目标

1. 与印章大师交谈

标题

与印章大师交谈

Talk to the Master of Seals

描述

帝国内政部的大殿坐落在达戈努斯的尖塔上。达戈努斯是冯·瓦兰修斯王朝的首都世界，而在尖塔上，印章大师负责守护帝国古老的法律。印章大师可以告知行商浪人关于帝国官方行商执照的认证程序。

The palace of the Adeptus Administratum is located in one of the spires of Dargonus, the capital world of the von Valancius protectorate. The Master of Seals, the keeper of ancient laws of the Imperium, can tell the Rogue Trader everything about the procedure for obtaining a Mercatum Tabula Officiale.

4. 回到帝国内政部的宫殿

标题

Return to the palace of the Adeptus Administratum

描述

Getting the required seals turned out to be quite a task due to the distance and lack of coordination between different Adeptus Administratum branches. Now the Rogue Trader just needs to return to Dargonus and certify the document with the Master of Seals.

5. 获得最后一个印章

标题

获得最后一个印章

Obtain the last seal

描述

文件上需要加盖两个印章，之后还需要加盖最终印章才可以。根据帝国法律规定，行商浪人需要与其他几百名请愿者一起排队，才能办好相关手续。

The Administratum clerk refuses to accept the document without the last seal certifying the document and two previous seals. As dictated by the laws of the Imperium, the Rogue Trader will have to stand in the queue with hundreds of other petitioners to obtain it.

附加条目
  • 附加 1
  • 如果行商浪人不打算和其他请愿者一起排队等待，可以在继任颂典结束之后再次返回，直接跳过等待的人群进行认证。
  • If the Rogue Trader does not want to wait in queue with the other petitioners, can return after the Magnae Accessio ceremony and certify the document without the long wait.

6. 将新的文件交给认证官员

标题

将新的文件交给认证官员

Bring the new document to the certification officer

描述

遗憾的是，当行商浪人耐心地排队时，文件上的其中一个印章过期了。行商浪人不得不从头开始整个流程，才能获得有效的帝国官方行商执照。

Unfortunately, while the Rogue Trader was patiently waiting for turn in the queue, one of the seals on the document expired. The whole certification process must be repeated from scratch to obtain a valid Mercatum Tabula Officiale.

附加条目
  • 附加 1
  • 行商浪人需要从印章大师那里得到一份空白表格。
  • The Rogue Trader needs to obtain a blank form from the Master of Seals.
  • 附加 2
  • 行商浪人可以用之前得到的印章在新的文件上盖章。
  • The Rogue Trader can certify the new document with a previously acquired seal.
  • 附加 3
  • 其中一枚印章保管在农业世界加努斯，你需要见当地的秘书一面。
  • One of the seals is kept on the agri-world of Janus. A visit to the court secretary is in order.
  • 附加 4
  • 这些印章必须先得到达戈努斯内政部的印章大师认证才行。
  • The seals must be certified by the Master of Seals in the Administratum Palace on Dargonus.

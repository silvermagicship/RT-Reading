“亲爱的，你听到了吗？”婕伊天鹅绒般的声音从你的通讯珠中传了出来，她的声音颤抖着，充满了焦虑。“维格迪斯刚刚……向我发来了来自落脚港的星语者信息。你没听错，这条信息是以‘个人名义’发送给帝国官方行商执照的持有者婕伊·海达利。”
"Shereen, do you copy?" Jae's velvet voice trembles with anxiety, trickling out of the comm-bead in your ear. "Vigdis has just... delivered an Astropathic message to me from Footfall. You heard that right, an Astropathic message addressed PERSONALLY to Jae Heydari, the holder of Mercatum Tabula Officiale."

“这件事情非常紧急，我们得谈谈。”婕伊的声音失去了平日的甜蜜，听起来极其严肃。“呃……不知你愿不愿意抽出一部分宝贵的时间，留给你忠诚的伴侣，亲爱的？”
"We need to discuss it. This is urgent." Jae is unexpectedly serious, her speech is devoid of the usual honeyed words. "Uhm... I was curious if would you spare a few moments of your precious time for your loyal partner? Shereen."

“亲——行商浪人，我们一起经历过很多操蛋事，所以我接下来的话可能会有点冒犯，希望你能原谅。”婕伊的声音非常平静，没有一丝波澜，仿佛暴雨来临前的海面。“我对你就像我对我自己一样忠诚，我一直很感激你对我的所有帮助。”
"Sher— Rogue Trader. We've been through a lot of shit together, so forgive the impertinence of what I'm about to say." Jae's voice is calm and quiet, like the sea before an impending storm. "I've been as loyal to you as I am to my own self. And I've been grateful for every favour you've bestowed upon me."

“但即便如此……或许他们确实是一群坏种、贱人，但他们是我的坏种、贱人。他们就像狗一样忠诚，不止一次把我从死亡边缘拉回来。如果我现在不去救他们，我恐怕永远不会原谅我自己。你愿意和我一起来吗，亲爱的？你愿意帮忙救他们一命吗？”
"Even so... They may be a bunch of ashmags and kaskhas, but they're my ashmags and kaskhas. They are loyal to me like void dogs, and they've dragged me back from the brink of death more than once. If I don't help them today — now — I will never forgive myself. Will you come with me, shereen? Will you help save them?"

"Lord Captain, permission to report. Jae Heydari... is gone. It is difficult to say when and how she left the ship, but only a few items are missing — her personal weapons, her certificate, and a small box containing securities. I believe she has returned to Footfall. According to vox-officer reports, she was always telling the crew that she would do the 'last decent thing in her life' there."

场景：JaeReminder

信号发出了一段时间之后，你通过安全的音阵频道，收到了婕伊发来的消息。她说出的每个字，声音都异常嘶哑。
Some time after the signal, you receive a message from Jae over a secure vox-channel. Her voice cracks uncharacteristically on every word she says.

“亲爱的，我们本该去落脚港帮帮我的手下，可时间却像沙子一样从我们指间溜走！这或许对你来说不是什么大事，但那些人迟早会没命。如果我不努力把那些人从虚空豺狼的嘴里救出来……那婕伊·海达利这个名字，恐怕还不如一坨臭屎！”
"Shereen, the plan was to go to Footfall and help my gang, but time is slipping through our fingers like sand! It may not mean much to you, but people die. And if I don't even try to rescue them from the jaws of those void jackals... the name Jae Heydari may as well be smeared with groxshit!"

“如果你不能快点帮我这个忙的话，亲爱的……恐怕我只能用我的老办法解决问题了，我自己一个人办。”
"If you do not grace me with your favour soon, shereen, well... I will just have to solve my problems the old-fashioned way. On my own."

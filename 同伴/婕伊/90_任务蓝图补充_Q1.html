场景：FalcoSaysHello

“舰长大人，收到紧急报告！俄歇矩阵探测到几艘虚空舰正在慢慢包围我们。我们要求对方停止追击，但对方完全无视了我们的要求。不过，有一艘无人操控的飞船朝我们的方向驶了过来，只传达了两个词——‘礼物’和‘海达利’。”
"Lord Captain, emergency report! The augurs have detected several voidships slowly encircling us. Our requests for this pursuit to cease have been completely ignored, but an unmanned vessel has been dispatched in our direction, broadcasting only two words: 'Gift' and 'Heydari.'"

“愿至高无上的神皇烧死这些豺狼！这是什么意思？”婕伊激动的声音从音阵频道里传了过来。“亲爱的，这件事情简直比烂透了的格洛克斯兽还让人恶心……而且，这和卡斯巴利卡代理人的行事风格实在是太像了。如果你允许的话，我想看看那群不肯露面的坏种给我送来了什么样的‘礼物’。”
"Exalted One incinerate these void jackals! What does it mean?" Jae's agitated voice comes on the vox-channel. "Shereen, this whole thing stinks worse than a dead grox... and it's also eerily similar to how Kasballican agents operate. With your permission, I would like to see what kind of 'gift' those tight-lipped ashmags have sent me."

音阵大师耐心地等待着你的指示。她熟练地切换着开关，音阵系统时不时发出噼里啪啦的静电声。
The Vox Master patiently awaits your instructions, flipping switches and occasionally emitting crackling static from her integrated vox-system.

紧张的几个小时过去了。这段期间，婕伊·海达利一直在舰长室里踱来踱去。当穿梭机返回飞船，发出第一声警报声时，她便急匆匆地冲向了底层甲板。
Several tense hours go by. All the while, Jae Heydari paces back and forth across the captain's bridge, but at the first shriek of the sirens announcing the shuttle's return, she rushes down to the lower decks.

“科尔！不！”婕伊的怒吼声从通讯珠里传了过来，瞬间震聋了你和舰桥上三分之一的军官，他们都在通过音阵频道聆听。“不，不，不！为什么至高无上的神皇会让如此善良的人遭受这样的劫难？到底是哪头虚空畜生，对你做了这样的事情？”通讯器中传来了一阵低沉的啜泣声，船舱舱板的植入物传来了咯吱咯吱的响声，婕伊的金属手指发出了清脆的噼啪声……随后，婕伊切断了音阵通讯。
"Kor! No!" Jae's outburst blares from the comm-bead, momentarily deafening you and a third of the officers on the bridge listening on the vox-channel. "No, no, no! Why would the Exalted One doom so kind-hearted a person to such a fate? What void beast has done this to you?" The vox-caster picks up a quiet sob, the whining screech of an augment on the bulkhead panels, the crunching of metal fingers... and Jae cuts the vox-connection.

“呃，舰长大人。返回的船员报告称，给婕伊·海达利的包裹里装着某个年轻人的头颅……”维格迪斯迅速敲击着沉思者上的按键。“我们的资料显示，死者被称为‘骗术师科尔’，是婕伊在落脚港‘贸易网络’的成员。他是‘骗术师双胞胎’的其中一位成员，与海达利女士在空间站的小行星上从事形迹可疑的商业活动。”
"Ahem. Lord Captain, the returning voidborn report that the package for Jae Heydari was the head of a young man..." Vigdis types swiftly at the cogitator keys. "According to our archives, he was a member of her 'trade network' on Footfall. The deceased is... Trickster Kor, one of the Trickster Twins who engage in suspect commercial activity on the station's asteroids with Mistress Heydari."

静电声传了出来，警报声再次响起。音阵大师对你开了口。“我们终于收到了对方的音阵讯息！我的军官已经准备好为您接通身份不明的对方成员。”
Static comes over the line, the siren blares again, and the Vox Master turns to you: "We have finally been alerted to an incoming vox-transmission! My officers are standing by to connect you to the unidentified parties."

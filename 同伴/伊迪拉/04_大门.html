大门

The Door

任务简介

伊迪拉·特拉斯向行商浪人寻求帮助。她声称被神秘的幻象所困扰，但她相信这些幻象会改变她的命运。

Idira Tlass has approached the Lord Captain for help. She is plagued by mysterious visions that she believes could change her fate.

1. 找到伊迪拉看到的幻象中显示的世界

描述

伊迪拉恳切地请求行商浪人探索科罗努斯扩区，寻找某个她在幻象中多次看到的未知世界。伊迪拉声称，说当接近那片区域时，她会感觉到这一点。

Idira has earnestly requested that the Rogue Trader explore the Koronus Expanse in search of an unknown world that she keeps seeing in her visions. Idira claims that her senses will tell her when the goal is near.

Idira is convinced that the place from her visions is located somewhere on the surface of Quetza Temer. She entreats the Rogue Trader to help her find it.

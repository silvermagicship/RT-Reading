第三章主线关联：科摩罗帮助卡西娅

“我……能感觉到了，我的感觉终于恢复了……”卡西娅瞪大了眼睛，目光中充满了恐惧与困惑。她的脸颊、脖子和手上带着可怕的抓痕，黏糊糊的血从她爪状的指尖滴下。“啊！为什么同时出现了这么多颜色？红色、黑色、紫罗兰色、血红色、黄色、黑褐色……快消失吧！别再纠缠我了！”
"I... feel. At last, I feel something..." You see horror and confusion in Cassia's wide-open eyes. Her face, neck, and hands are gruesomely scratched and sticky blood is dripping from the tips of her claw-like fingernails. "Ah! Why are there so many of you at once? Red. Black. Violet. Scarlet. Yellow. Dark maroon... Away with you! Stop swarming my thoughts!"

玛拉斋后退一步，发出了暴怒的嘶嘶声。“我当初就该杀了这个怪物……真该死！她是赛·兰斯雷什的大门！”
Marazhai recoils, letting out a frenzied hiss. "I should have killed this thing back then... It's a foul gateway of Sai'lanthresh!"

“{name}，快回来！”
"{name}, get back!"

卡西娅再次捂住头，尽管她遮住了她的第三只眼，但你感觉到她的眼睛下面已经传来了微弱的亚空间气息。她苍白的皮肤之下，原本融入她体内的石头变得越来越烫，烧穿了她的皮肤，之后……出现了裂纹。“{name}，我……我控制不住它了！”强大的力量从卡西娅脆弱的身体流了过来，让你头晕眼花，头发根根竖起。
Cassia clutches her head again, this time covering her third eye, but you can already feel the breath of the warp coming from its gaze. The stone fused into her body heats up under her pale skin, burns through it, and... cracks. "{name}, I cannot control it!" The powers flowing through Cassia's fragile body make your head swim and your hair stand on end.

你感觉身体燃烧起来，仿佛被一大桶熔融的塑钢浇在头上。你的脑海中充满了古怪的低语、阴影、声音……每次呼吸都令你痛苦不已，仿佛有什么东西在撕扯着你的内脏，想要穿破你的胸膛爬出来，你的喉咙也开始不断翻涌。尽管导航者睁开了第三只眼，但你依然存活了下来，可如果再次发生这样的情况，你的身体和精神还能承受得住吗？
Your body is burning as if you have been doused with a vat of molten plasteel, and your head is teeming with strange whispers, shadows, voices... Every breath is a struggle, as though something is scratching at your insides, trying to claw its way out of your chest. Your gorge rises. You have survived being exposed to the Navigator's open eye, but will your body and mind hold if it occurs again?

卡西娅努力将目光集中在你身上，她苍白、憔悴的脸上带着某种陌生的表情，夹杂着严厉、自信与和专横。你第一次见到西奥多拉·冯·瓦兰修斯时，她的脸上就带着这种表情。“……愿祂的明光驱散前方的黑暗，祂的星炬便是我的灯塔，是祂赐予我的祝福与指引……”
Cassia struggles to focus her eyes on you. An unfamiliar expression is imprinted on her pale, gaunt face, one that is stern, confident, and imperious. It is the same expression you saw on Theodora von Valancius's face when you first met her. "...And may His light scatter the darkness ahead, for the Astronomican is the beacon and His guiding blessing to me..."

诡异的寒意贯穿了你的全身。你几乎已经忘记了这种感觉，但随后便回想起来，这是另一个人混乱的情绪，以及来自那个人的意志……这场强大的情感波浪即将把你碾为齑粉。
An eerie chill runs down the entire length of your body. It is a feeling you forgot, but recollection swiftly follows: someone else's turmoil and emotions and a will that is not your own... This crushing wave is about to consume you.

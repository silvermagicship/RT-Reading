返回角色目录 | 返回同伴目录

场景：CassiaTroubled

“舰长大人，我匆忙向您报告，是因为船上的机魂给我带来了令人不安的消息。这件事情极其微妙，而且关系到卡西娅女士。如您所知，自从她第一天上船起，船员们的生活就受到了干扰。如果您允许的话，我已经准备好了一份详细的报告。”
"Lord Captain, I hasten to report the disturbing news brought to me by the machine spirits of the ship. The matter is extremely delicate and concerns Lady Cassia. You see, since her first day aboard, her presence has been a disturbance to the crew's way of life. If you'll allow, I have prepared a detailed report."

选项 Choices

  • 选择 Choice

“把报告交给我吧。”
"Deliver your report."

“第一起事故就发生在我们离开欧拉克五号星站之后不久。导航者女士斥责了船上某个负责跑腿的船员。那个船员回家之后，就杀死了他所有的家人，之后饮弹自尽。第二起事故发生在亚空间跳跃期间，导航者女士向飞行员传达了错误的命令，导致虚空舰偏离航线。尽管只有短短几分钟的时间，但那足以让亚空间的力量激怒机魂，导致服务甲板起火。”
"The first incident occurred immediately after our departure from Eurac V: the Lady Navigator chastised one of the ship's runners, after which he went to his living quarters, killed his family, and then shot himself. The second incident was noted while traversing the warp: the Lady Navigator gave the pilots the wrong instructions and the voidship was thrown off course — for a matter of minutes, but this was enough for the forces of the Immaterium to anger the machine spirits enough for them to start a fire in the service bay."

“在那之后，所有住在导航者女士住处附近的军官，都表现出了极端情绪——歇斯底里、冷漠、兴奋、愤怒。这对船员的士气与工作造成了相当不利的影响。最后一起事故发生在落脚港。我们在落脚港停留期间，大约有一百只活鸟被运到了船上，每只鸟都花了一大笔钱，但我并没弄清楚那些鸟究竟是用来做什么的，也不清楚它们后来怎么样了。”
"After that, officers living near the Lady Navigator's quarters began to express extreme emotions — hysteria, apathy, euphoria, rage. This is quite detrimental to crew morale and performance. The last incident was recorded on Footfall. Around a hundred living birds were delivered on board during our stay on Footfall. Each bird cost a hefty sum, but I have failed to discover their purpose and subsequent fate."

  • 注：出现条件：Companion

“我还听说导航者女士与韦尔森总领之间发生了冲突，不过并没有收到任何细节。如果您愿意的话，您可以亲自询问韦尔森大师。”
"I was also told about a conflict between the Lady Navigator and the Seneschal. Alas, with no details. If you like, you can ask Master Werserian directly."

  • 注：出现条件：Companion

“婕伊·海达利的情况更加糟糕。我截获了一段音阵信息，婕伊赌咒发誓，说如果再见到她的话，就要‘宰了那个小贱人’，这是她的原话。”
"Things are even worse with Jae Heydari — I intercepted a vox-cast in which she promised she would, and I quote, 'end that kaskha' if she ever saw her again."

“如果您允许我发言的话，舰长大人，导航者女士的精神状况令我感到担忧。她沉默寡言，从不和别的船员交流，因此所有人都躲着她。毕竟，就算是高阶军官，也可能会有迷信的想法。恐怕只有您能站在平等的立场上与她谈谈，改善目前的状况。这么做既是为了船员们的安全，也是为了卡西娅女士本人。”
"If I may, Lord Captain — the Lady Navigator's state of mind worries me. She is self-contained and does not mesh with the crew at all, which is why everyone avoids her — even senior officers can be superstitious. I fear that only you are in a position to talk to her on an equal footing and improve the situation. For the sake of the crew's safety — and that of Lady Cassia herself."

“如果您允许我发言的话，舰长大人，导航者女士的精神状况令我感到担忧。她沉默寡言，从不和别的船员交流，因此所有人都躲着她。毕竟，就算是高阶军官，也可能会有迷信的想法。恐怕只有您能站在平等的立场上与她谈谈，改善目前的状况。这么做既是为了船员们的安全，也是为了卡西娅女士本人。”
"If I may, Lord Captain — the Lady Navigator's state of mind worries me. She is self-contained and does not mesh with the crew at all, which is why everyone avoids her — even senior officers can be superstitious. I fear that only you are in a position to talk to her on an equal footing and improve the situation. For the sake of the crew's safety — and that of Lady Cassia herself."

"Cassia is an honoured guest on this voidship, and the voidsmen must come to terms with all her oddities and caprices. And for those who cannot — lashes to every one of them!"

“只要舰长大人您认为合适就好。”维格迪斯沉默片刻，但音阵系统传来的噼啪声表明她并没有切断连接。“关于报告……”
"As the Lord Captain sees fit." Vigdis is silent for a while, but the crackle of the vox reveals she is still connected: "Concerning the report..."

场景：OrsellioGreetings

“舰长大人，紧急报告，我们收到了来自阿伦托·奥赛罗摄政王的星语信息。他的家族听说卡西娅从欧拉克五号星站中失踪，感到无比担忧。摄政王要求您告知卡西娅目前的状况，还询问了亲自见面谈判的可能性，他希望您能安排导航者女士返回她的家族。”
"Lord Captain, urgent report — we have received an Astropathic message from Regent Aaronto Orsellio. The Navigators are concerned about the fate of Cassia, who disappeared from Eurac V. The regent is requesting an update on her current status and he has also enquired about the possibility of a personal meeting to negotiate and to arrange the return of the Lady Navigator to her House."

“这条信息的情绪编码带有极为深重的忧虑，但也同样传达了那些星语者未能用语言表达的东西。希望您能原谅我的直率，我认为有必要将真相告知奥赛罗家族，否则我们可能会与导航宗族发生冲突。鉴于科罗努斯扩区的现状，这样的冲突只会为帝国的敌人带来益处。”
"The psycho-emotional coding of the received message betrays acute concern, but it also contains something that the Astropaths have failed to put into words. If you'll excuse my forthrightness, I feel it is necessary to tell House Orsellio the truth. Otherwise, a conflict with the Navis Nobilite is possible, which, given the current state of the Koronus Expanse, will only play into the hands of the enemies of the Imperium."

选项 Choices

  • 选择 Choice

“奥赛罗家族是怎么发现卡西娅在我这里的？”
"How did House Orsellio even learn that I have Cassia?"

“我也想知道是怎么回事，舰长大人，这确实很令人不安。可能是我们的船上有间谍……也可能是阿伦托摄政王在虚张声势，但无论是哪种情况，我认为都应该做出回应。导航者会成为非常强大、非常有用的盟友。”
"I wish I knew, Lord Captain. It is troubling indeed. Perhaps we have a spy on board... or Regent Aaronto is bluffing. In either case, I believe we should respond. Navigators are extremely powerful and useful allies to have."

  • 选择 Choice

“告诉奥赛罗家族，卡西娅很安全，行商浪人很荣幸能与摄政王见面。”
"Tell House Orsellio that Cassia is safe and that the Rogue Trader would be honoured to meet in person."

“交给我吧，舰长大人，星语者会传达您的许可。奥赛罗家族的代表会在首都世界达戈努斯等待您。接待导航宗族的成员始终一定会是件繁复的事情，请您在抵达达戈努斯后，与宰相讨论这场接待会的细节。”
"I'll see to it, Lord Captain. The Astropaths will transmit your consent — representatives of House Orsellio will be waiting for you on the capital world Dargonus. Receiving members of the Navis Nobilite is always a delicate matter. Please make sure to discuss the details of this meeting with your chancellor upon arrival."

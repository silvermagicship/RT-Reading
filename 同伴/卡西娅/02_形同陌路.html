任务简介

导航者女士与船员们相处不睦，还在船上引发了严重的问题。音阵大师请求行商浪人与卡西娅谈谈，改变这样的情况。

The Lady Navigator is at odds with the crew, which is causing serious problems on the ship. The Vox Master has asked the Rogue Trader to speak to Cassia to remedy the situation.

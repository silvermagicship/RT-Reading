“不过，我并不是要责备你的所作所为。相反，我想告诉你的是，在穿越亚空间的过程中，我看到的幻象越来越多。同样的幻觉一次又一次出现在我眼前……我觉得我要发疯了。如你所知，那些幻象都是关于我、关于群星圣仪，以及奥赛罗家族的前任大族长提西福涅·奥赛罗。我看到了提西福涅的实验，还有关于塞塔拉家族导航者的实验。传言说，她杀光了塞塔拉家族所有导航者，因为那个家族的人不服从她的命令。”
"However, I am not here to reproach you for the deeds of yore. Instead, I wish to tell you of the visions that I have come to experience at an increasing rate during warp jumps. The same visions come to me again, and again, and again... It is as if I am going mad. For you see, these visions are about me, the Atlas, and Tisiphone Orsellio, the House's previous Novator. I have seen Tisiphone's experiments, the ones that involved the Navigators from the Setthala branch, which she was rumoured to have destroyed for disobedience."

“但我看到的幻象，却完全不是这么回事。在我看到的景象里，是塞塔拉家族的人协助提西福涅创造了群星圣仪！实验完成之后，他们便消失不见了。或许我可以……不，我知道，只要我能从幻象中重新找到提西福涅当年的旅程，我就能找到通往圣仪神殿的道路。那里并不是奥赛罗家族的导航者看到的精神幻象，而是切切实实存在的神殿。我原本就怀疑，那座宫殿说不定真的存在，可现在……我几乎可以确定，那座宫殿就是真实存在的。”
"But the visions told me an altogether different story, one where the Setthalas helped Tisiphone create the Atlas! And they vanished immediately after the experiments were done with. Perhaps I can... no, I know I can find the way to the Palace of the Atlas if I recreate Tisiphone's journey from my visions. A real palace, not the psychophysical illusion to which the Orsellio Navigators can reach out. I have always had a feeling that it truly exists in our world. Now, however... I know it with absolute certainty."

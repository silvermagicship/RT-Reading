任务简介

韦尔森家族被指控犯下了叛国罪。为了自己的家人，阿贝拉德请求行商浪人调查这件事情，揭露最终的真相——无论真相究竟如何。

House Werserian stands accused of treason. On behalf of his family, Abelard has asked the Rogue Trader to intervene in this matter in hopes that the truth may be revealed — whatever it turns out to be.

2. 证明韦尔森家族的清白
标题

证明韦尔森家族的清白

Prove House Werserian's innocence

描述

针对韦尔森家族的证据似乎有些可疑，阿贝拉德不肯相信他的家族参与了反叛行商浪人的阴谋。或许领主舰长可以为总领的家族提供证据，证明家族的清白。

The evidence against House Werserian seems dubious, and Abelard refuses to believe that his family had anything to do with the conspiracy against the Rogue Trader. Perhaps the Lord Captain could establish some sort of alibi for Seneschal's noble house.

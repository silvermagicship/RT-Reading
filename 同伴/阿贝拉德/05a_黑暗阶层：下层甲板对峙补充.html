黑暗阶层：下层甲板对峙补充

场景：LowerDeskConfrontation

船舱中回荡着激烈的争吵声。执法者阿芙里拉·温特中尉拦住了她的上级——身披重甲的戒律大师，约卡斯塔·苏尔贝克本人。从她们的表情和语气来看，这场对峙已经持续了一段时间，双方都已经到了一触即发的状态。
The sounds of a heated discussion reverberate through the ship's bays. Lieutenant Avrilla Vent of the ship's enforcers is barring the path of her superior, Master-at-Arms Jocasta Sauerback, who has arrived clad in armour. Judging by their expressions and tone, the standoff has dragged on for some time already and has now reached boiling point.

“温特中尉，我已经要失去耐心了。我的命令你听得很清楚，别挡我的道。”
"Lieutenant Vent, my patience is wearing thin. I have given you an order. Now get out of my way."

“请恕我无法执行命令，长官！”温特看起来有些动摇，但她的目光中依然闪着火光。“甲板的这片区域由我负责。只要这片区域依然在舰长大人的特别监管之下，没有我明确许可的情况下，任何人都无权贸然采取行动。只有三个人有权撤掉我的职位——要么是总领，要么是舰长大人，要么是帝皇本人！”
"I cannot comply with that order, ma'am!" Vent looks shaken, but there is fire in her eyes. "This is my deck and my sector. So long as this area remains under the Lord Captain's special oversight, no action can be taken without my express permission. There are only three people who are authorised to make me stand down — the first officer, the Rogue Trader, and the Emperor Himself!"

“看来，我的教导还是有人听进去了……”阿贝拉德喃喃自语，表示赞同。不过他并没指明是赞同温特，还是赞同站在她身前、身材高大的苏尔贝克，或者两者都是。
"So not all my lessons fall on deaf ears..." Abelard mutters in crabby approval. He does not clarify whether he means Vent, Sauerback looming over her, or both.

“大人！”这两位军官注意到了你，猛地立正，朝你敬了个礼。片刻之后，他们的部下也跟着向你敬礼。
"Your Lordship/Ladyship!" Noticing you, the two officers snap to attention and salute, their subordinates following suit a moment later.

A dozen pairs of eyes stare apprehensively at you. The people before you are typical inhabitants of the lower decks — you take in their simple clothing, crude weapons, and faces that display varying levels of mutation, from the barely discernible, to the strange, to the outright grotesque.

人群中传来一阵窃窃私语，你在他们的反应中看到了恐惧、惊讶与喜悦。到了最后，三个人从人群中挤了出来。那是一个年迈的妇人与两个男人，他们一定就是领头人。这三个人笨拙地在你面前鞠了一躬。“舰、舰长大人！你下到我们这里来了！”
A whisper passes through the crowd, and you detect a mixture of fear, astonishment, and joy in their reactions. Finally, three people break away from the crowd — an elderly woman and two men. These must be the leaders. They bow clumsily before you. "L-Lord Captain! You have come down to us!"

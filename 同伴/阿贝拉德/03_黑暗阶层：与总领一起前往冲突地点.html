任务简介

领主舰长收到了请求，希望能评估韦尔森总领的行为。韦尔森总领对下层甲板下达了某个命令，而这个命令引发了问题，可能会影响到整艘船。

The Lord Captain has received a plea to assess the actions of Seneschal Werserian. As a result of Werserian's decisions regarding a situation in one of the sectors on the lower decks, the problem now threatens to engulf the whole ship.

标题

与总领一起前往发生冲突的地点

Go to the site of the conflict with the Seneschal

描述

领主舰长必须评估阿贝拉德·韦尔森总领的行为。于是，行商浪人打算亲自调查四号货栈的情况。

The Lord Captain must assess the actions of Seneschal Abelard Werserian. This requires the Rogue Trader to survey the situation in Depot 4.

目的地

冯·瓦兰修斯虚空舰，四号货栈

Depot 4, von Valancius Voidship

  • 阿贝拉德反射般地擦了擦额头上的汗水。这一刻，他一直以来如同盔甲一般的强硬姿态露出了疲态，年迈与疲惫占了上风。这样的情况非常罕见。 / “底层甲板总是会没完没了地发生问题。有的时候我感到很遗憾，因为我们没法用机仆取代所有当地船员。”
  • Abelard reflexively wipes the sweat from his brow in a startlingly human gesture, and a telling one — a rare moment where he allows his age and weariness to show through his armour-like veneer of self-assurance. / "The lower decks are a source of endless problems. Sometimes I dearly regret that we cannot replace all the locals with servitors."

To them, you are akin to a creature of legend, one who has stood on the steps of the Golden Throne, no less. Whereas I am the man whose name is attached to every order docking rations or lowering a bay's heating."

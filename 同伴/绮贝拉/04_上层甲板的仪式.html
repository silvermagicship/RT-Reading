Rituals of the Upper Decks

任务简介

绮贝菈已经接受了领主舰长的邀请，准备前往上层甲板，学习更多相关的仪式习俗。

Kibellah has accepted the Lord Captain's invitation to learn more about the ceremonial customs of the ship's upper decks.

目标

1. 与高阶顾问交谈

标题

与高阶顾问交谈

Talk to the High Factotum

描述

为了举办一场令人难忘的宴会，行商浪人应当前往舰桥，与高阶顾问讨论准备工作。

In order to host a truly memorable ceremony, the Lord Captain should discuss the preparations with the High Factotum on the bridge.

Bridge, von Valancius Voidship

Janris Danrok is making arrangements for the event. He will let the Lord Captain know as soon as everything is prepared.

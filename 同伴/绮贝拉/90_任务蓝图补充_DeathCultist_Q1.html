场景：Demagogues

乌合之众从各个方向不断推搡着。他们的脸上写满了恐惧与愤怒，暴虐的能量在无数人的身体中沸腾。这种力量很难控制，因为心怀恐惧的人往往非常危险。这场狩猎本不该是这个样子，但当下的局面正是不死之神的旨意。
The laypeople press in from all sides, pushing and shoving. Their faces are ablaze with terror and rage, and a violent energy boils through the mass of bodies. This power will be difficult to contain — frightened people are dangerous people. The hunt was meant to go differently, but such is the Undying One's will.

其中一个人的声音盖过了所有其他声音。那是个身材魁梧、蓄着胡须的技工，声音低沉得像熔炉的轰鸣，混杂着汗味和神圣机油的味道。他并不害怕，仅仅是装出一副惧怕的样子。在他故作恐惧的颤抖之下，我听到了兴奋的意味。“兄弟们，姐妹们，咱们真的该被活活处死吗？咱们一直都在勤勤恳恳地劳作，是吧？就连咱们的同伴被机魂电死的时候，咱们也没叫冤，没错吧？他们凭什么管咱们叫叛乱分子？咱们凭什么要受到惩罚？”“熔炉”的话让周围的人更加群情激奋。
One voice rises above the others, the voice of a burly, bearded technomat, low as the roar of a furnace. Furnace smells of sweat and sacred unguents. He is not afraid, he merely pretends to be. Beneath the shudder of terror, I hear excitement. "Do we deserve to be executed, my brothers and sisters? Have we not laboured with the sweat of our brow? Have we not perished from the outbursts of raging machine spirits? Why do they call us rebels? Why are we being punished?" Furnace's words make the crowd seethe even more.

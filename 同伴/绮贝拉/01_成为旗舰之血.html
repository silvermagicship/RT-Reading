成为旗舰之血

To Become the Flagship's Blood

任务简介

织血罗网的宿老邀请行商浪人参观教派的圣殿，圣殿位于虚空舰深处的某个地方。然而，只有追随最初的织网者“圣埃兰妮”留下的足迹，才有资格前往神圣的死亡摇篮。

The Vizsier of the Bloodspun Web has invited the Rogue Trader to visit the cult's temple, which lies somewhere in the depths of the voidship. However, the hallowed cradle of death will only reveal itself to one who has walked in the footsteps of Saint Erianne, the Matriarch Spider.

目标

1. 探索虚空舰的货运区域

标题

探索虚空舰的货运区域

Explore the voidship's freight line

描述

拜死教教徒指向了底层甲板的货运区域，蛛母族长就是从那里踏上了织网之路。因此，若是行商浪人打算前往死亡的圣殿，这场旅途也必须从这片甲板启程。

The death cultists have pointed out where on the lower decks the path of the Matriarch Spider began — the freight line. Thus, it must likewise serve as the starting point on the Lord Captain's journey to the temple of death.

货运区域

Freight Line

2. 跟随鲜血画成的符号前进

标题

跟随鲜血画成的符号前进

Follow the blood symbols

描述

拜死教教徒留下的血迹掩盖了《血路之歌》的歌词。这首歌记录了最初的女族长曾走过的道路。行商浪人决定跟随这条道路前进，看看这条道路最终会通往何处。

The blood marks left by the cultists conceal the verses of the Song of Trailing Blood, which chronicles the path walked by the Matriarch Spider. The Lord Captain has decided to follow the symbols and see where they lead.

3. 跳入通风井，成为旗舰之血

标题

跳入通风井，成为旗舰之血

Plunge into the ventilation shaft and become the flagship's blood

描述

行商浪人面临着艰难的抉择——是迈出最后一步，成为旗舰之血？还是放弃最初的织网者曾走过的道路，放弃寻找圣殿的打算？

The Lord Captain is facing a difficult choice: to take the final step and become the blood of the flagship, or to abandon the path of the Matriarch Spider and own search for the temple.

目的地

货运区域

Freight Line

完成文本

行商浪人追随着最初的织网者曾走过的道路，成为了旗舰之血，流淌在无数甲板钢铁制成的血管之中。

The Rogue Trader has followed the trail of the Bloodspun Web's first assassin and has become the flagship's blood that runs through the iron veins of its bays.

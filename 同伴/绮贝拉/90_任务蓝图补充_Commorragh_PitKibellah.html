绮贝拉：任务蓝图补充 - Commorragh_PitKibellah

返回角色目录 | 返回同伴目录

场景：PitKibellah

囚笼的地板上躺着无数尸体，尸体的手从栅栏之间伸出来，苍白的面孔紧贴在金属笼子上。绮贝菈站在尸体之中，一动不动，身边站着另外两个女性。她静静地聆听着那两个人邪恶的低语，双臂无力地垂在身边。
The floor of the cage is littered with corpses, their arms sticking out between the bars and their pale faces pressed against the cold metal. Above them stands Kibellah, frozen between two female figures. She listens to their sinister whispers, her arms hanging limply.

“你不过是件杀人的武器而已，根本没有自己的意志，因此你也无法忤逆他。承认这一点吧。”
"You are nothing but a weapon designed to spill blood. You have no will of your own, and thus you cannot resist him. Admit it."

“他玷污了你，粉碎了你。你不过是个邪恶的冒牌货而已，你该接受这个事实了。”
"He has sullied you. Shattered you. You are an unholy imitation. You should have accepted this by now."

每具尸体上都有一两处残忍的致命伤，要么是心脏被洞穿，要么是喉咙被割开，要么是眼窝变成了黑色的空洞。无论是活着的人还是躺在地上的尸体，都长着同样的脸——绮贝菈的脸。
Each corpse has one or two brutal, indisputably lethal wounds: a pierced heart, a slashed throat, a black void instead of an eye socket. Each body, both living and dead alike, has the same face. Kibellah's face.

两个人都长着同样的脸，但表情却截然不同。其中一个人的脸扭曲着，满是厌恶之意，看起来仿佛面具一般。面具发出了刺耳的声音，似乎想要将自己的喉咙撕开。“你存在的意义就是成为工具，你没有能力反抗主人的意愿，你迟早都会听命于他。”
Both figures have her face, and yet there is no mistaking one for the other. One of them is twisted into a still, mask-like grimace of loathing. It rasps as if ripping its own throat apart, "You were created to serve as a tool. You do not have the capacity to oppose the hand that commands you. That is why, sooner or later, you will succumb."

另一个绮贝菈面带微笑，但笑容充满了嘲讽与痛苦的意味。与之前的绮贝菈相似，这个绮贝菈的表情也仿佛面具一样固定在了脸上。“你已经被毁了，所以不死之神才不肯将你带走。如今的你，已经成为了邪恶之人的猎物，沦为了他们的玩物。”
The other wears a smile, both mocking and pained at once. Like the first figure, this one seems unable to rid itself of its expression. "You are ruined. That is why the Undying One refuses to take you away. Now you are prey for the wicked, their plaything."

"And every time I found myself upon death's threshold, he claimed a part of my soul to create... this!" Kibellah gestures to her copies. "Unhallowed replicas! My soul is mutilated, and I am a mockery of what I once was. And I... can't fight. Not anymore."

另一个冒牌货大笑起来，指着遍地尸体。“真正的绮贝菈就躺在这些尸体之中。至于你，你不过是她的碎片，是异形在她身上留下的污痕。你是不净之人，如今却打算用你污秽的身躯来玷污秘者？”
The other double laughs and points at the dead bodies. "The true Kibellah lies among these corpses. You are just her vestige, her infinitesimal trace defiled by the xenos. You are unclean, and now you would stain the Domin with your abominable essence?"

绮贝菈默默地看着两个和她一模一样的人，之后沉重地摇了摇头。“她们说得对，我不能和你一起离开。说不定这是血伶人的陷阱。谁知道呢？或许……或许就连我都不是真正的绮贝菈，说不定我只是她的碎片而已。把我遗弃在这里吧，只要你安全，就够了。”
Kibellah regards her copies in silence, then shakes her head heavily. "They are right. I cannot go with you. It could be the Haemonculus's trap. Who can say, perhaps... perhaps I am not even the true Kibellah, merely a shard of her. Abandon me here. Save yourself."

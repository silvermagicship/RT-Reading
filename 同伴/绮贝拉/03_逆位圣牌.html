逆位圣牌

A Card Inverted

任务简介

底层甲板的传奇人物正在威胁领主舰长与整艘旗舰的安全，是时候让秘者的利刃，坚盾与影子绮贝菈出马了。让她编织一张血红色的罗网，抓住这个宣扬分裂的叛徒。

A living legend from the lower decks threatens the safety of the Lord Captain and the entire flagship. It is now time for Kibellah, the Domin's blade, shield, and shadow, to spin a sanguine web and catch this sower of discord.

目标

1. 前往船上的礼拜堂

标题

前往船上的礼拜堂

Go to the ship's shrine

描述

船上的礼拜堂遭到了袭击，高阶顾问詹里斯·丹罗克在这场袭击中受了重伤。他乞求领主舰长前往礼拜堂，聆听他的忏悔。

High Factotum Janris Danrok was gravely wounded during a raid on the voidship's shrine. He begs the Lord Captain to join him there and hear his confession.

目的地

冯·瓦兰修斯虚空舰，礼拜堂

Shrine, von Valancius Voidship

2. 举行盘绕祭礼

标题

举行盘绕祭礼

Perform the ceremony of twining

描述

前往等离子驱动室之前，绮贝菈要求领主舰长举行鲜血仪式，保佑她顺利追捕涅墨斯特。

Before heading to the plasma drive chambers, Kibellah has asked the Lord Captain to perform a blood rite that would bless her hunt for Nemesite.

目的地

冯·瓦兰修斯虚空舰，舰桥

Bridge, von Valancius Voidship

3. 揪出涅墨斯特的追随者

标题

揪出涅墨斯特的追随者

Expose the followers of Nemesite

描述

袭击者在礼拜堂内留下的所有痕迹都指向了等离子驱动室。考虑到当地工人可能会窝藏涅墨斯特，绮贝菈必须想办法让这些人放松警惕，揪出煽动暴乱的追随者，最终让他们所谓的英雄无所遁形。

The evidence left by the raiders in the voidship's shrine points to the plasma drive chambers. Kibellah must now ease the vigilance of the local workers, who may be harbouring followers of Nemesite. She will then force the troublemakers to reveal both themselves and, eventually, their so-called hero.

A divination using the Emperor's Tarot and blood spilt over the flesh of the betrayers has pointed to a man close to Nemesite. The traitor is Adalbert, the ship's confessor.

6. 等待执法者的报告

标题

等待执法者的报告

Wait for the enforcers' report

描述

戒律大师已经派人去搜集情报，了解涅墨斯特的下一步计划。等报告准备好之后，领主舰长与各位军官就可以制定行动计划了。

The Master-at-Arms has sent enforcers to gather information on Nemesite's next move. Once the report is complete, the Lord Captain and the officers can then devise a plan of action.

7. 在军官甲板进行伏击，消灭涅墨斯特

标题

在军官甲板进行伏击，消灭涅墨斯特

Eliminate Nemesite during the ambush on the officers' deck

描述

船上的新任告解神父准备举行军官宣誓仪式，而涅墨斯特打算借着这个机会发动袭击。戒律大师安排了许多乔装打扮的执法者扮演参加仪式的贵宾。当涅墨斯特打算袭击那些完全算不上手无寸铁的宾客时，他就会落入陷阱之中。

Nemesite is planning to strike during the formal ceremony of the swearing-in of the officers that is to be performed by the new ship's confessor. In place of the usual distinguished guests, the Master-at-Arms will instead fill the chapel with disguised enforcers. The moment Nemesite decides to attack the not-so-helpless guests, the trap will be sprung.

The Lord Captain has managed to lure Nemesite into a trap, but the resurgent legend of the lower decks will not give up without a fight.

9. 与绮贝菈交谈

标题

与绮贝菈交谈

Talk to Kibellah

描述

绮贝菈砍下了涅墨斯特的头颅，作为献给不死神皇的祭品。战斗结束之后，绮贝菈不禁思考起了她与涅墨斯特两人命运奇妙的相似之处。或许不久之后，她会与领主舰长谈谈她的想法。

After taking Nemesite's head as a trophy offering to the Undying Emperor, Kibellah withdrew to ponder the curious intertwining of their destinies. Perhaps she will share her thoughts with the Lord Captain later.

冯·瓦兰修斯虚空舰，舰桥

Bridge, von Valancius Voidship

在绮贝菈与领主舰长的努力下，虚假的英雄涅墨斯特，以及他那所谓为了被压迫者而战的传说，很快就会被所有人遗忘。

Thanks to Kibellah and the Lord Captain, the solemn legend of Nemesite, aberration and false champion of the oppressed, will soon be consigned to oblivion.

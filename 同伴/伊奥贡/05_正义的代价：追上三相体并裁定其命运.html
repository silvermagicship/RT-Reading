任务简介

特级基因士伊奥贡·费布鲁斯邀请行商浪人前往仿生区参加一项实验。他的目标是掌控死亡，算出量化正义的公式。

Extremis Genetor Eogunn Februs has invited the Rogue Trader to the Sector Bionica to participate in an experiment. His goal is to master death and calculate the formula for righteousness.

伊奥贡创造的正义三相体已逃离仿生区，去往下层甲板。行商浪人必须找到他，并决定如何处理这个逃跑的实验体。

The righteous Triasomata created by Eogunn has escaped from the Sector Bionica, making his way to the lower decks. The Rogue Trader must find him and decide what to do with the runaway subject.

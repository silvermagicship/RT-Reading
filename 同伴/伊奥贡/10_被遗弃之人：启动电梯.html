任务简介

伊奥贡已经道出了努恩修斯的真相，如今行商浪人必须设法前往神秘的阿姆桑克特行星，帮助这位技术神甫决定他那位尊敬的同僚的命运。

Eogunn has revealed the truth about Nuncius, and now the Rogue Trader must find a way to the mysterious planet Amsanct to help the Tech-Priest determine the fate of his esteemed colleague.

启动电梯

Activate the lift

描述

启动那部旧电梯。这是到达努恩修斯遗体所在实验室的唯一途径。

Activate the old lift. This is the only way to reach the laboratory where Nuncius's body rests.

目的地

阿姆桑克特

Amsanct

  • 要恢复电力，你需要用热熔炸弹炸开研究营地的入口，然后重新启动发电机。
  • To restore power, you must clear the entrance to the research camp with a melta charge and restart the generator.

According to Reverend Hieronymus, suspicious individuals are conducting rituals in the Shadow Quarters — in a place marked with a blue and gold sun. If they are heretics, they must be eradicated. The presence of Sister Argenta is highly advisable.

目的地

怒焰星系，落脚港，阴影区

Shadow Quarters, Footfall, Furibundus

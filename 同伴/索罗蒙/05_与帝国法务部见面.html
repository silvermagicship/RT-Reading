与帝国法务部见面

An Audience with the Arbites

任务简介

帝国法务部的元帅正在达戈努斯等候行商浪人，此人似乎与冯·瓦兰修斯王朝之间有所往来。在正式接见帝国法务部代表前，行商浪人应当前往达戈努斯宫殿中的王座大厅，与索罗蒙谈谈。

The Marshal of the Adeptus Arbites is waiting for the Rogue Trader on Dargonus. It seems he has business with the von Valancius dynasty. To commence the audience, the Lord Captain should speak to Solomorne in the throne room at the palace on Dargonus.

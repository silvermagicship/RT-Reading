乌尔法：任务蓝图补充 - Q2

返回角色目录 | 返回同伴目录

场景：UlfarRun

“舰长大人，飞行甲板上发生了事故。我们发现的行星与帝皇尊贵的天使乌尔法所知的坐标相吻合。当他得知这件事情之后，他……弃船离开了。”
"Lord Captain, there was an incident on the flight deck. The planet we discovered is located at the coordinates of interest to the honourable Ulfar, Angel of the Emperor. The moment he learned about this, he... chose to abandon ship."

“他命令我们给他准备好一艘穿梭机，去了我们面前的这个世界。我们也不知道他到底想去什么地方，但我们的俄歇矩阵已经扫描到了穿梭机着陆的位置。”
"He ordered a shuttle to be provided to him and directed it to the world before us. We do not know where exactly he wishes to go, but the augurs have tracked the shuttle to its landing site."

选项 Choices

  • 选择 Choice

“他偷了一艘穿梭机？这是怎么回事？”
"He stole a shuttle? How did this happen?"

“乌尔法大人突然出现在了甲板上，赶走了执法者，要求允许他起飞。所有仆人都被他吓坏了，不敢违抗他的命令。”
"Lord Ulfar simply showed up at the deck, drove the enforcers away and demanded he be granted clearance for take-off. Your servants became frightened and dared not disobey his orders."

  • 选择 Choice

“他这么干的时候，为什么没有人立刻通知我？”
"Why have I not been notified of his actions right away?"

“他……禁止我们通知您，那些执法者也不敢违抗帝皇天使的命令。我很抱歉。”
"He... forbade it. And the enforcers did not dare to defy an Angel of the Emperor. I am very sorry."

  • 选择 Choice
  • 注：显示条件：Etude

“怎么我的随行人员都这么喜欢偷穿梭机呢？”
"Will all members of my entourage be stealing shuttles now?"

“我完全理解您的愤慨，舰长大人。这种滥用飞船资源的恶劣行为实在是不可接受！”
"I fully share your indignation, Lord Captain. Such blatant abuse of the ship's resources is unacceptable!"

  • 选择 Choice

“处决那些负责看守穿梭机的卫兵。”
"Execute the sentries who were guarding the shuttles."

“我们会立刻执行您的命令，舰长大人。”
"Your order will be carried out with the utmost diligence, Lord Captain."

  • 选择 Choice

“我明白了。”
"I see."

“我很抱歉给您带来了这个令人不安的消息，舰长大人。”
"I apologise for having to bring you this troubling news, Lord Captain."

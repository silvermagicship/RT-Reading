芬里斯生生不息

Fenrys Hjolda

任务简介

乌尔法·雷狼的狼群“怒嚎者”，消失在了行商浪人前往的星系中的某个地方。强大的太空野狼乌尔法打算找到他的兄弟们。

The Baleful Howl, the pack of Ulfar Thunderlung, are known to have vanished in the star systems where the Rogue Trader's destiny has led . The mighty Space Wolf is bent upon finding his brethren.

目标

1. 找到怒嚎者狼群前往的地方

标题

找到怒嚎者狼群前往的地方

Find the place where the Baleful Howl pack went

描述

尊敬的索巴德·铁皮提供了坐标，指向这片星团的某个位置。行商浪人必须探索这些星系，弄清楚乌尔法的狼群究竟去了什么地方。

The coordinates provided by the esteemed Thorbald Ironhide lead to a place somewhere within this star cluster. The Rogue Trader must explore these systems to find where exactly Ulfar's pack was headed.

2. 找到乌尔法

标题

找到乌尔法

Find Ulfar

描述

乌尔法离开了行商浪人的舰队，前往某颗遥远的行星，他的狼群似乎去了那个地方。如果行商浪人打算协助的盟友完成这场远征，那必须亲自前往那颗冰封的行星表面。

Ulfar disembarked from the Rogue Trader's ship and headed to the planet that is presumed to hold evidence of his pack's presence, and he did so alone. If the Lord Captain intends to aid ally in this expedition, too will have to make planetfall on this frozen planet.

目的地

奈欧斯战车

Neos Charoitus

3. 想办法与乌尔法交谈。

标题

想办法与乌尔法交谈。

Find a way to speak with Ulfar.

描述

身受重伤的乌尔法被送回了船上。尽管船医已经不抱希望，但乌尔法最终还是撑了下来。此刻，他打算向行商浪人解释他私自离开的原因。

Gravely wounded, Ulfar was transported back to the ship. Despite the chirurgeons' expectations, he did not perish from his injuries and is hopefully now ready to explain the reasons for his abscondment to the Lord Captain.

目的地

船上的礼拜堂

Shipboard Chapel

4. 找到乌尔法的狼群

标题

找到乌尔法的狼群

Find Ulfar's pack

描述

行商浪人已经找到了怒嚎者狼群前往这个世界的飞船，因此，那群太空野狼肯定就在这附近的某个地方。行商浪人需要对这片区域进行侦查。

The ship that brought the Baleful Howl pack to this world has been discovered, and therefore the Space Wolves themselves must be somewhere nearby. A reconnaissance mission is in order.

目的地

奈欧斯战车

Neos Charoitus

5. 击败渴望复仇的幽灵

标题

击败渴望复仇的幽灵

Defeat the vengeful spirits

描述

怒嚎者狼群的死状无比凄惨，他们愤怒的灵魂渴求复仇。行商浪人必须让这些凶猛的野狼安息。

The Baleful Howl's death was a grim one, and their raging souls demand vengeance. The Rogue Trader must lay the fierce Wolves to rest.

完成文本

行商浪人已经得知了怒嚎者狼群的命运，他们命运的丝线已经被切断了。如今，狼群最后的幸存者，乌尔法·雷狼，只能靠自己的力量，在这个宇宙中开辟出新的道路，侍奉全父。

The fate of the Baleful Howl is now known, and the threads of the valiant pack's wyrd have been severed. Their last surviving brother, Ulfar Thunderlung, must now forge a new path of service to the Allfather for himself in this universe.

第四章｜科摩罗归舰：帕斯卡的回应

场景

行商浪人从科摩罗归舰后，舰桥欢迎是全员共享场景。帕斯卡在其中的直接回应如下；会议其余报告不构成他的个人任务。
After the Rogue Trader returns from Commorragh, the bridge welcome is a shared party scene. Pasqal's direct response is retained below; the remaining reports do not form part of his personal quest.

帕斯卡恭敬地向你鞠了一躬。“这座神殿的{mf|主人|女主人}回来了，对居住在这艘虚空舰上的所有人而言，这都是个值得献上敬意的重大事件。”
Pasqal offers you a respectful bow. "The return of the {mf|master|mistress} of the temple is an event honoured by every spirit that dwells within the sacred systems and machines of this abode."

第四章｜尤弗拉忒斯二号：对机械亵渎的反应

场景

围攻尤弗拉忒斯二号期间，异端巫术重新驱动制造工厂的钻机。此段是共同主线战役中的帕斯卡反应，不会开启他的个人任务；其个人线在后续收到隐士之舟号信号时继续。
During the siege of Eufrates II, heretical sorcery restarts the manufactorum drill. This is Pasqal's reaction within a shared main-story battle, not the start of his personal quest; his arc continues later when the Hermetico signal arrives.

“又是亵渎的恶行。这样的恶行实在是太多了，根本记录不完。”帕斯卡干巴巴的声音就像重型车辆的链条一样嘎嘎作响。他的面甲上亮起了耀眼的绿色，随后是蓝光。你听到四面八方传来了金属般的呻吟声，就好像这座制造工厂本身正在遭受折磨一样。
"Another blasphemy. They are too many to count now." Pasqal's dry voice is rattling like a heavy vehicle's chain track. His visor lights up a blinding green, then blue, and you hear a metallic moan coming from everywhere. It is as though the manufactorum itself is being tortured.

第四章｜乌拉隆战后：报告胜利与优先设备

场景

击败乌拉隆后，行商浪人通过升降中心向艾莎拉报告胜利。她随即下令提取“优先设备”；这个节点结束尤弗拉忒斯地下战场的现场流程。它不是隐士之舟号的瞭望台对话，后者由第四章之后帕斯卡收到的信号另行开启。
After Uralon is defeated, the Rogue Trader reports victory to Aishara through lift control. She immediately begins extracting the 'priority cargo'; this closes the on-site Eufrates undercroft sequence. It is not the Hermetico observatorium conversation, which begins separately after Chapter 4 when Pasqal receives the signal.

“喂？有人能听得到吗？”
"Hello? Can anyone hear me?"

静电声中传来了艾莎拉的声音。“收到，突击小队。这里是升降中心，报告你们那边的情况。”
Aishara's voice cuts through the static: "We read you, strike team. This is lift control. Report your status."

场景：Dements

眼前的技术神甫从数据板上移开了视线，抬头看着你。他的呼吸器植入物不见了，下半张脸上覆盖着苍白的人造肉体，宛如死人一般。你注意到，他的头上装着各种各样的植入物，应该是不久之前才装上去的。这些植入物安装得乱七八糟，未经治疗的伤口中渗出了脓液。“我在虚空之中听到过你的脚步声……你就是冯·瓦兰修斯家族的人，是吧？”
The Tech-Priest tears himself away from reading his data-slate and looks up at you. His respiratory augmetic is missing — instead, the lower half of his face is covered in deathly pale artificial flesh. You take note of the multitude of fresh augmetics attached to his head. They seem to have been inserted haphazardly, and pus is seeping from the untreated wounds. "I heard your footsteps in the void... You are von Valancius, are you not?"
